Autism /ASD RW

Triad of neurodivergence (neurodevelopmental)
• Abnormal interaction with others/social skills 
• Poor communication 
• Restricted imagination or rigid/repetitive interests 
Resulting with functional impairment 
Being “different” or “special” 

Is a spectrum, some associates with Ld or some high-functioning (Asperger’s) 

Usually from early childhood <3y, strong genetic basis, some environmental factors 

No perfect screening tool but respond to concerns from parents/school/HV and use free to access M-CHAT tool 

Masking = hiding autistic traits can be linked with MH problems 

Refer paeds neurology if any regression in motor skills or any regression in social or language <3, if >3 with features of autism refer to ASD team (MDT). If adult use Autism Spectum Quotient (ASQ) or AQ-10 and refer if concerns + functional impairment (often home work life MH etc)

Management - no “cure”
• Education & training, SLT - Simple language, gestures, don’t blame be patient, give time, 
• Optimising environment /routines - OH, routines, minimising sensory overload, create safe zones 
• CBT if coexisting anxiety (common)
• Specialist may consider SSRI, antipsychotics ST, melatonin, ritalin depending on MH

Examples 
• Abnormal interactions /socialising with others 
   - Preschool = no smile, not responding to things 
   - Primary/secondary = not respecting/tolerant of personal space, plays alone, can’t adapt 
   - Adult = saying inappropriate things, taking things literally/ not understanding sarcasm 
• Poor communication 
   - Preschool - Language delay (<10 words at 2 or regression)
   - Primary/secondary/adult - repeats stuff, monotonous voice, uses stereotyped phrases, inappropriate /rude, poor eye contact, looking around 
 • Rigid interests 
   - Repetitive movements, extremes of emotion, dislike of change, can’t adapt, strongly adheres to rules

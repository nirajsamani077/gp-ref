Seborrhoeic dermatitis PCDS

Red scaly rash on glabellar (sebum rich) skin of central face eyebrows nasal folds behind ears, can also involve scalp and flexures 

Consider HIV and PD if widespread & lack of response (think bigger than skin)

Scalp - Nizoral (Ketoconazole) shampoo twice weekly for 2w then every 2w for maintenance, lather into scalp then wash off after 5mins. If persistent inflammation can use betnovate scalp or synalar (fluocinolone) if stinging 

Face/chest - Nizoral shampoo applied to same areas (can also use cream if localised). Consider eumovate, daktacort or subbing in protopic for steroid if needing regularly (also trimovate in intertrigo body folds). If marked = itraconazole (sporanox) 200mg OD 7d

Resistant - Add 8-12 week lymecycline 408mg OD & refer

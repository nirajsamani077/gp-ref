Proteinuria & UACR

First pass morning sample 

ACR > 3 = clinically important proteinuria, only refer if persistent haematuria + declining renal function/CVS disease (=A2)
ACR > 30 = nephrotic range - refer to renal if persistent haematuria but no UTI (=A3)
ACR > 70 refer to renal unless known diabetes and already being treated 

ACE or ARB are essential if ACR >3 with diabetes or >30 with HTN/CKD. If >70 then all need.

For HTN in proteinuric CKD
- ACR >70 aim for 130/80
- ACR <70 aim for 140/90

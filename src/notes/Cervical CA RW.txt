Cervical CA RW

In young women, smears for asymptomatic disease, symptomatic disease (usually PCB, others = IMB, discharge, PMB, pain).

Always speculum to visualise Cx although not a great pick-up, and do STI swabs

Only smear in asymptomatic, not for symptomatic (although recent negative HPV within 3y on smear would make cervical CA extremely unlikely)

Almost all need surgical (LLETZ or full open laparotomy + TAH BSO pelvic node clearance & chemo) - although good survival big psychosocial impact

Vaginal itch (pruritis vulvae)

Causes 
- irritant contact dermatitis (most common) from lubricants/condoms 
- atopic dermatitis (eczema)
- seborrhoeic dermatitis & fungal infection 
- psoriasis 
- lichen planus
- lichen sclerosis

Management 
- showers rather than bath
- loose cotton underwear 
- only clean OD, with watery emollient (epaderm)
- topical steroids for most (combined with anti fungal if ?Seb derm/fungal)

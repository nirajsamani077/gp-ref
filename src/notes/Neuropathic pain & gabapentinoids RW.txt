Neuropathic pain & gabapentinoids RW

Shooting/burning/hypersensitive pain - use DN4 score if struggling to distinguish 

Common causes   
• Diabetic neuropathy
• Radicular pain or cancer/chemo related 
• Post herpetic (shingles)
• Trigeminal neuralgia 
• Central (stroke, MS, SC injury) 

Trigeminal neuralgia = exception - CBZ (100mg OD/BD initially (max 200mg QDS))

Otherwise neuropathic painkillers which include choice of 4 agents, if one not working try another, tramadol only for acute rescue (not LT)
• Duloxetine 60mg OD (max 120mg DD), Rv 3mo
• Amitryptilline 10-30mg evening, increase in 10mg steps every week, usual maintenance 30-70mg
• Gabapentin 300mg OD titrate slow max 3.6g in 3DD (eg 1200mg TDS)
• Pregabalin 150mg 2-3DD (eg 75mg BD) titrate slowly to max 600mg 2-3DD (eg 200mg TDS) 

Gabapentinoids (pregabalin & gabapentin) only help in true neuropathic pain but even then they are not great- <5/10 people will get >50% reduction in pain after 8mo - they are completely ineffective in sciatica, lower back pain, fibromyalgia & side effects common 2/3 (dizziness drowsiness oedema MH) & they are addictive especially if combined with opiates - so avoid using unless a very clear neuropathic pain, not chronic pain!!
• Both schedule 3 controlled drugs so should not be issued amounts for longer than 30d and scripts are only valid for this time frame - always only out for 1 issue on repeat so need to resign each find 
• Only other indications = epilepsy, GAD

Capsacin (chilli peppers) as cream or patch only licensed under specialist if can’t use oral 

Refer to pain clinic if severe, affecting QOL, neuropathic meds not helped ..

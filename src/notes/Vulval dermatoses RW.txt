• Typical ezcema rash symmetrical

• Psoriasis typical app use eumovate alternating with weak coal tar prep (exorex)

Lichens 
• Lichen simplex chronicus - common, chronic itch soreness leathery plaques, consider swab/patch test/ferritin, similar to dermatitis & sedating antihistamine and silk underwear 
• Lichen sclerosis - pale white cigarette paper coloured atrophic or hypertrophic played with inflammation and scarring, refer all for Bx as scarring or malignancy change, use dermovate 1 FTU 1 month weaning regimen (4w OD, 4w alt D, and 4w twice weekly) then Rv 3mo then maintenance 
• Lichen planus - purple polygonal shiny wickhams striae similar to sclerosis so refer treat same

Breech & ECV RW 

Bottom down - occurs in 3% and adverse outcomes eg shoulder dystocia /obstructed labour 
• Should be offered ECV which has success rate 50% but can risk sensitising event and very very small risk of bleeding (much less than risk of vaginal breech comps or risks of needing CS)
• Usually offered after 36w if still breech .

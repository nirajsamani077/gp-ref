Birthmarks 

Salmon patch (stork mark) - very common vascular pale pink patch usually midline forehead/eyelid/necks. Usually fade over few months 

Strawberry naevi - raised red multilobed lesions in first few weeks, capillary haemangioma, not present at birth, increase til 6mo then regress over years, generally resolve spontaneously unles large or impairing function/causing bleeding/thrombocytopenia can try propanolol

Cafe au lait spots - brown patches, common but sometimes associated with other conditions 

Port wine stains - rare, asymmetrical, darken with age 

Cherry angioma - red papules, later in life

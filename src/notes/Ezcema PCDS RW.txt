Ezcema PCDS RW

Signpost to Ezcema Care Online - free NIHR funded online self management 

Emollients 
• Liberally +++ 2-4 500g tubs a month , use even when ezcema-free (keeps skin hydrated, keeps barrier prevents irritants, reduces itch), ointments for oiler if v dry
• Use same emollient as soap sub (do not Px bath emollients for kids as not cost effective, instead dilute emollient in small amount hot water
• For shampoo use ezcema friendly unperfumed & wash out fully 

Steroids  
• Only use in flares Od or Bd usually 7-14d or till better than 2d after, if not improving r/o infection and go up ladder/refer
• Can use maintenance “weekend” regime in troublesome areas with recurrent flares for 6mo 
• Cover area thinly ,usually wait 30min after emollient (always leave a gap doesn’t really matter which way round) 
• Amounts 
  - Scalp/face neck /genitals / both hands = 15-30g
  - Both arms = 30-60g
  - Both legs /trunk = 60-120g
  - Half amount in children

Antihistamines - only offer if severe itch if interfering with sleep 7-14d sedating, if not 1mo non sedating  

Types and locations 
• Face /genitals- 1% HC OD /BD or even eumovate shorter time, can change to topical tacrolimus (0.03% protopic <2yrs, 0.1% adults, protopic ointment, pimecrolimus cream, if using Reg HC)
• Trunk-limbs - go up potency ladder, eumovate, betnovate RD, betnovate, elocon, dermovate - safe to use up to 100g/month (so 1000g emollient, up to 100g steroid /month) 
• Hand ezcema - atopic, hyperkeratotic or pompylox (palms fingers itchy vesicles) - usually super potent steroids (dermovate) under occlusion (cotton gloves, clingfilm). If weeping - dry skin with potassium permanganate solution soaks. If hyperkeratotic /scaly - diprosalic ointment under occlusion (betnovate + salicylic acid)
• Scalp - betnovate/dermovate scalp application, if too stingy try Synalar (fluocinolone)
• Discoid ezcema - itchy patches discrete, lighter red border than psoriasis, crusty rather than scaly, edges don’t advance like fungal infection (consider  mycology scrapings though). Short potent steroid OD till thinks settle (usually 6w prolonged)
• Venous ezcema (gravitational stasis)- red and brown (Haemosiderin) dry scaly weepy, most often not cellulitis. Copious emollient ointments using large spoon, potent steroid short bursts, if needing Reg protopic 0.1% OD 6-8 weeks then gradually down to twice weekly maintenance. Can occlude betnovate under clingfilm or use medicated bandages (ZIPZOC or viscopaste) replacing twice weekly for 6-8 weeks. LT compression hosiery made to measure class II & exercises. 

If not working /comps 
• Go up ladder, check compliance, check emollient, consider changing to protopic OD/BD 
• Tachyphylaxis - loss of response after repeated use of steroid, consider switching to Alternative 
• Consider infection
   - Bacterial = weeping crusting worsening, try oral fluclox (if widespread) or Fucibet OD/BD or fucidin TDS and nasal swab for MRSA as may be resistant 
   - Fungal = steroid can change appearance of tinea (loses scaly distinct edge but can be annular), get skin scrapings, stop steroid and use topical or oral antifungal 2w (as for Seb derm)
   - Viral = Ezcema herpeticum is DERM EMERGENCY if rapidly worsening ezcema systemically unwell with blisters vesicles or punched out lesions , same day admission IV abx + aciclovir & swabs, even cold sore (herpes simplex) in atopic person should always give Aciclovir

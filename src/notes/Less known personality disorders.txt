Less known personality disorders

Cluster A - odd/eccentric 
= paranoid, schizoid, schizotypal 

Schizoid —> prefers to be alone, can’t make close relationships, emotionally cold (more negative sx)
Schizotypal —> delusion, perceptual dis, unusual words, magical thinking, struggles to relate (one away from schizophrenia)  (more positive sx)

Cluster B - dramatic/emotional 
= EUPD/borderline, antisocial, histrionic, narcissistic 

Antisocial —> fails to comply with law, aggressive, irresponsible, lacks remorse 
Histrionic —> centre of attention, sexually inappropriate
Narcissistic —> grandiose, entitled, arrogant 

Cluster C - anxious/fearful 
= obsessive/compulsive, avoidant, dependant 

Avoidant —> fear of failure, views self as inferior
Dependent —-> needs too much support, lacks initiative 
OC —> preoccupied by lists/rules, meticulous, stingy

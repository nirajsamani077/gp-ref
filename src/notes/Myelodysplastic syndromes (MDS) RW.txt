Myelodysplastic syndromes (MDS) RW

Rare group of bone marrow CA associated with
• Cytopenias (Hb WCC plts, pancytopenias) or 
• Macrocytosis 
With high risk of transformation to AML

More common in older, often incidental on FBC asymptomatic or vague Sx related to cytopenias 

DDx is haemolytic anaemia so apart from haematinics check haemolysis screen (retics, LDH, haptoglobin, Coombs) & virology (BBV, CMV, parvo) which are another pancytopenia cause 

Refer for BM biopsy & type stratified into number of stem cell lineages affected (eg just Hb, Hb & plts, all 3), % of blast cells (risk of AML) & specific immunogenetic tests 

Treatment - replacing cytopenias (irradiated BT, G-CSF) & stem cell transplant/specialist 

Survival depends on risk - low risk often live up to 10y but high risk live <3y .

Paronychia PCDS

Nail fold infection, with tender swelling redness at edge of nail and skin proximally, due to staph 

Can be viral (herpetic whitlow) with grouped vesicles which can be recurrent 

Acute - warm water soaks, fucidin or fluclox if severe. Aciclovir for whitlow 
Chronic (usually “wet” workers, less painful) - work modifications (gloves, avoid irritants), trimovate BD for flare ups, can swab and antifungal po (Lamisil) if positive

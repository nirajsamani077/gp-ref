Epipen
- <25kg = EpiPen Jr. 150micrograms/0.3ml (1 in 2,000) solution for injection auto-injectors (Viatris UK Healthcare Ltd) - 2 pre-filled disposable injection - Use if symptoms of anaphylaxis develop and call 999. If not improving after 5-15minutes, use a second auto-injector. Always carry two auto-injectors at all times.
- >25kg = EpiPen 300micrograms/0.3ml (1 in 1,000) solution for injection auto-injectors (Viatris UK Healthcare Ltd) - 2 pre-filled disposable injection - Use if symptoms of anaphylaxis develop and call 999. If not improving after 5-15minutes, use a second auto-injector. Always carry two auto-injectors at all times.

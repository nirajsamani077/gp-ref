Screening test stats/terms 

                           Disease present          No disease 
Positive test           True Pos                  False Pos 
Negative test         False Neg                True Neg

Sensitivity - % with disease present who have a positive test (TP/disease present)  ie liklehood of true positive 

Specificity - % with no disease who have a negative test (TN/no disease) ie likelihood of false negative 

PPV - if positive test, % chance of disease present 
(TP/positive test)   

NPV - if negative test, % chance of no disease (TN/negative test)

Likelihood ratio - the likelihood of a test result in someone with the condiiton, vs the likelihood of the same test result in somone without the condition  

Likelihood ratio of positive test - how do odds increase of disease present if positive test (sensitivity/1-specificity)

Likelihood ratio of negative test - how do odds increase of no disease if negative test (1-sensitivity/specificity)

Likelihood ratios are not prevalence dependent 
Precision - how repeatable are results?

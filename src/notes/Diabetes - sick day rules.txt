Diabetes - sick day rules 

General - increase BM monitoring to 4h, increase fluid intake, keep sugary drinks and supplies close, monitor ketones 

OH - continue as sickened raises cortisol which pushes up sugar levels. Metformin should be paused if dehydrated as can cause AKI + lactic acidosis 

Insulin - do not stop, continue @ may need to increase as sugars higher during illness. Corrective dose may be TDD/6 (max 15 unit)

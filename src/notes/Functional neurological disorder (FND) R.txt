Functional neurological disorder (FND) RW 

Disorder of voluntary motor/sensory function 
• Positive diagnosis made by neurologist so all should be referred 
• Abnormal involuntary predictions made in the brain combined with emotional state & inputs 
• Mismatch between intact involuntary movement & impaired voluntary moment 

Symptoms are genuine - just not consistent or explainable by any recognised neurological disease & often multiple, dissociative, non epileptic attacks - filming an event is useful, may have psychological/MH comorbidity but not always 

Should still go down normal neurology referral route but if Dx by secondary care prioritise patient - consider it as an internal software problem that cannot be easily seen

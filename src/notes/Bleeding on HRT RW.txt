Bleeding on HRT RW 

Common in first 3-6mo (esp if an issue before)

If persists >6mo or troublesome 
• Hx Ex swabs 
• Consider short period 6w off HRT to see if stops 
• TV USS for ET

If abnormal - refer Gynae 

If sequental combined HRT (endometrium would be thicker due to cycles of proliferation/shed)
• ET > 7mm = Refer (for endometrial assessment ie hysteroscopy + Bx)
• ET <7mm = Modify progestagen or increase dose /duration to 21d (or Mirena which would thin out endometrium nicely), can add oral progestagen to transdermal or Mirena, if persistent refer 

If continuous combined HRT (endometrium should be thinner and stable due to constant hormones)
• ET >5mm = Refer (esp 2WW if doesn’t stop after stopping HRT)
• ET <5mm = Modify/increase progestagen (Mirena), reduce oestrogen, consider down to sequential and tibolone , if persistent refer 

Check endometrial RFs - BMI, diabetes, PCOS
Lower threshold to refer if RFs or persists after stopping 
Non adherence can also cause breakthrough bleeding so check 

If progestagenic S/E (fluid retention, breast tenderness, headaches etc) usually cyclical with the progestagen, or androgenic S/E more related to older testosterone derived ones (NET, LNG)
• Separate out oestrogen progestagen and use micronised progestagen (min dose 100mg Utrogestan, min time 12d per cycle - to provide enough endometrial protection)
• Oestrogen S/E similar can try lower dose preps (Femoston Conti or Premique low dose).

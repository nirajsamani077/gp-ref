Lithium monitoring 

Narrow therapeutic range (0.4-1), long half life (renal excretion) - check level 12 hour POST dose 

Side effects 
• GI
• fine tremor (coarse tremor in toxicity) 
• nephrotoxicity, nephrogenic DI
• hypothyroidism, hyperparathyroidism
• weight gain, IIH
• leucocytosis (as opposed to agranulocytosis)
• T wave flattening/inversion 

Lithium levels weekly until stable on dose, then 3 monthly with 6 monthly U&E/TFT
Medic alert card & levels/dose recording book

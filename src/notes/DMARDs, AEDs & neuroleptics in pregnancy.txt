DMARDs, AEDs & neuroleptics in pregnancy RW

All should be offered pre-pregnancy counselling by specialist - so if wants to try = refer & pregnancy should be avoided with good contraception until specific plan - will have consultant led joint ANC. Many need high dose folic acid 5mg until 12w 

DMARDs - most safe in pregnancy/BF but not
- MTX = stop 1mo before (M don’t need to stop) 
- Leflunomide = stop 2y before (M don’t need to stop), if exposed <2y & preg refer for cholestyramime washout procedure 
- MMP - stop 6w prior (12w for M)
- Breastfeeding - above defo avoid, sulfasalazine/HCQ/AZA & steroids are safe, biologicals considered safe usually 
- Fertility - sulfasalazine & MTX can effect sperm 

AEDs   
- Ensure good control and specialist, don’t stop drugs prior to this but need good contraception
- Valproate clearly most teratogenic & needs specific Pregnancy Prevention Programme & acknowledgement of risk forms etc - shouldn’t be on this if WCBA +++ risk of NTD
- CBZ also has risk of fetal malformations 
- Lamotrigine & Keppra safest so may be switched to this before trying  
- BF most are safe but check SPC

MH drugs 
- Safety evidence isn’t great so liase with specialist as risk balance of destabilising MH - refer to UK Teratology Information service
- SSRI /SNRI/ TCA - if pregnant discuss uncertainty over risks and gradually withdrawing /CBT instead, in BF sertraline & paroxetine safest due half life 
- BDZ has no benefit anyway and should be withdrawn 
- Antipsychotics & lithium - lithium definitely shouldn’t be on as teratogenic and can’t if BF, liase with psych re specific antipsychotic & risks .

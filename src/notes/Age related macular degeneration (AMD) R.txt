Age related macular degeneration (AMD) RW

Most common cause of blindness in West
• RF = Age >55, smoker, FHx 
• Gradual central visual loss with metamorphopsia (straight line appears wavy) shown on Amsler grid 
• Often found opportunistically by optician 

Types 
• Dry = Drusen & atrophy macula can progress to..
• Wet =Neovascularisation, rapid central visual loss

Management 
• Refer suspected to opthalmology, if ?wet active with haemorrhages & visual loss to eye casualty 
• Will have slit lamp, OCT, flourescin angiography
• Stop smoking (main RF), vitamin supps (vit C)
• Low vision clinics, register sight impaired for benefits & monitoring esp in wet AMD
• Anti-VEGF intravitreal injections (Lucentis, Avastin), which are anti angiogenic for wet AMD for rapid improvement in vision, monthly until becomes dry

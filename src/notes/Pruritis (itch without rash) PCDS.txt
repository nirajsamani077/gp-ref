Pruritis (itch without rash) PCDS

50% idiopathic, 50% underlying cause 

Consider malignancy (lymphoma, PNP) so examine 
Can be other systemic disease, meds, aquagenic or delusional parasitosis

Bloods - FBC, ferritin, TFT, LFT at least. Consider AMA (for PBC), myeloma screen, BBV, LDH

1% menthol in aqueous (menthoderm, dermacool) - cooling for skin 
Non sedating antihistamine up to 4 times a day 4 week trial (lorat/cetr 10mg, fexo 180mg) - only use sedating short term (risk of dementia)

If fails this consider derm referral, 6 week trial low dose of gabapentin (100mg OD to TDS, up to 300 to 600mg TDS) or pregabalin, phototherapy

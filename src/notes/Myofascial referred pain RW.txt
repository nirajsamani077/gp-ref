Myofascial referred pain RW

“Trapped nerve” or “muscle spasms” or “knots” often trigger points which can be released by Osteopaths 
• Neck /occipital pain = traps 
• Earache = SCM
• Lower back pain /buttock = quadratus lumborum, gluteus medius 
• Sciatica = gluteus minimus , soleus 
• Knee pain = vastus medialis 
• Forearm pain = extensor carpi radialis. 
• Abdo pain = rectus

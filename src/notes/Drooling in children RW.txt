Drooling in children RW

Mild common & usually managed with protective clothing, barrier cream but if severe paeds or LD team may use oral glycopyrronium solution (Sialanar)
• Licensed from age 3+ 
• 12.8mcg/kg/dose TDS
• Hyoscine also a good option, less expensive .
• Only use short term intermittent

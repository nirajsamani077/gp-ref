Self-induced skin conditions RW

Skin-picking disorder - ritualised, worse during stress, admitting to it tried to stop but couldn’t, distress & affects QOL, slow healing recurrent monomorphic lesions on easily accessible sites (DDx papular ezcema, scabies, acne)

Dermatitis artefacta - self induced but hides this fact from clinician & family, bizarre shaped lesions easily accessible sites mimic malignancy even, patient seems apathetic whilst family distressed 

Trichotillomania - hair pulling disorder causing non scarring alopecia, ritualised & similar to skin picking with heightened inner tension before released after pulling/picking 

Management = two track approach physical & mental health, R/O systemic causes eg scabies or underlying haematinic/thyroid and explore ideas for emollients & early referral for CBT/consider SSRI.

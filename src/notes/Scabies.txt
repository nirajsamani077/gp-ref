Scabies 

Sarcopetes scabie mite burrows into skin, laying eggs which cause intense itching 
Linear burrows can be seen on finger webs, creases from sites of skin contact 

All household and close contacts should be treated, which includes 2 treatments 1 week apart of insecticide all over everywhere 

1st line - permethrin 5%, keep on for 12h
2nd line - malathion 0.5%, keep on for 24h

Launder all clothes/bedding on day 1 also 
Itch can persist 4-6wks

N.B. Crusted (Norwegian) scabies occurs if immunocompromised and causes teeming mites on crusted skin. Needs ivermectin & isolation.

Achilles rupture RW

Rupture if sudden jump with severe heel pain/snap 
Hx of tendinopathy or RFs (cipro, steroids) 
Simmonds triad to diagnose rupture 
• Lie on front, legs over back of couch dangling - 1) check for Achilles gap, 2) check for injured leg more DF, 3) Squeeze calf if no PF = rupture 

If ?rupture = same day ortho RV,  will need casting in equinus (PF) to allow healing at right length, moonboot, physio, surgery in young athletes

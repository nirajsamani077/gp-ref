PTSD RW 

Symptoms prolonged > 1 month from traumatic event (beyond stress reaction symptoms which are common for most after a trauma but usually fade away over several weeks)
• Flashbacks, hyperarosal, avoidance, numbing 
• Anxiety/depression/substance/insomnia 
Use CBT framework - thoughts/emotions impact on behaviour/physical symptoms & vice versa

If mild, watchful waiting 1 month further but otherwise trauma-focused CBT & eye movement desensitisation and reprocessing (EMDR) 
• 8-12 sessions each usually 90mins to process 
• If severe/complex or drugs needed - SSRI (usually sertraline) or SNRI (venlafaxine) if failed, risperidone if severe (under specialist so refer if failed one or two above CMHT)
• Continue any med 1y post remission to prevent relapse which is common 

Good book - Overcoming traumatic stress C Robinson & NHS inform .

Anti-oestrogen drugs in ER+ breast CA

Tamoxifen 
- SERM, antagonist in breast, partial agonist in endometrium so risk of endometrial CA
- S/E - menstrual disturbance, hot flushes/climacteric, VTE, endometrial CA

Anastrozole/letrozole 
- aromatise inhibitor, reduces peripheral oestrogen production which is main form of production post menopause so 1st line in post menopausal ER+ breast CA
- S/E - osteoporosis (DEXA 1st), hot flushes, insomnia, myalgias

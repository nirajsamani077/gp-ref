Reflux in children 

Most common cause of infant vomiting (40%)
Usually <8wks, usually regurg following feeds 

Options
• feed 30deg head up
• smaller frequent feeds
• thickened formula 
• alginate (gaviscon), not same time as thickened, give mixed with expressed breast milk or cooled boiled water - 2 week trial 
• PPI not routinely recommended unless combined with unexplained feeding difficulties, distress or FTT (used to be ranitidine but may be carcinogenic)- 4 week trial 
• Prokinetic (metoclopramide) only under specialist 

Remember total feed should be 150ml/kg over 24hrs with 6-8feed/d

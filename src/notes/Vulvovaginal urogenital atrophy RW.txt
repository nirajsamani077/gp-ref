Vulvovaginal /urogenital atrophy RW

Physical changes to genitals due to loss of oestrogen causing discomfort dryness sexual dysfunction loss of libido & bladder dysfunction incontinence etc - unlike vasomotor post-menopausal which can spontaneously resolve this won’t and can become chronic progressive 

Management 
• Vaginal lubricants - lots of OTC options try to find one water or silicone based with acidic pH and low osmolality eg “Yes” products or Good Clean Love/Sylk but test on patch of skin for 24h before and don’t use at same time as topical oestrogen as reduces absorption 
• Topical oestrogen for almost all but NOT if undiagnosed vaginal bleeding/endometrial Ca/hormone sensitive Ca options are usually OD for 2 weeks then twice weekly maintenance, can combine esp if prolapse 
- Vagifem tablet (Vagirux same but less plastic)
- Ovestin cream
- Blissel gel
- Imvaggis pessary 
- Estring ring pessary 3 monthly 

Some need annual review but most long term safe

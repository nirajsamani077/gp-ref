VIW management RW

Episodic wheeze only trigger by viral illness with iWOB, usually passes but can become poorly low O2 etc . Crossover with developing asthma depending on factors 
• Duration of wheeze during viral illness (>10d)
• >3 episodes/year, >3x SABA issues/y
• Nocturnal worsening (night waking >1x/w)
• Interval Sx Between episodes (SABA >1-3x/w)
• FHx asthma/atopy, consider parental smoking 
• Multi-trigger wheeze to other allergens exercise smoke etc rather than just VIW higher risk in child

Initial treatment = SABA 4-10 puffs every 4hrs til, stretching or reducing puffs till sx improve (wheeze plan), one puff at a time via spacer with normal 5 breaths between (not big deep ones)

Make sure have pMDI + spacer (aerospacer, volumatic + face mask) - don’t do quick deep breaths do 5 “normal” tidal breaths & clean spacers monthly (use detergent, air dry) - if can’t use spacer consider neb 

If worried (iWOB, can’t speak full sentences, cyanosis) - give 10 puffs & if sx do not improve quickly call 999

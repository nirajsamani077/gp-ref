Gastro in children 

Rotavirus most common 
Diarrhoea 7d, stops by 2wks
Vomiting 2d, stops by 3d
Mild-moderate-severe dehydration
Look for severe hypernatraemic jitteriness, increased tone/reflex 

Stool MC+S only if sepsis, bloody stool, IC, travel or diarrhoea lasting >7d

If shocked IV rehydration 
If dehydration give oral rehydration solution (diarolyte) - 50ml/kg over 4hrs + ORS solution for maintenance 
Continue breastfeeding but can supp with other fluids

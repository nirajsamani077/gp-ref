PCB RW

Main red flag is cervical CA so examine STI swabs and refer (10% present like this)
• Refer 2WW if cervix worrisome appearance 
• Otherwise r/o other causes & if ? Ectropion consider switching out of COCP
• Any PCB >6w refer for urgent colposcopy 

Most PCB is a benign cause 
• STI (cervicitis)
• Ectropion or polyp 
• No specific cause found half the time 
• Ensure smears UTD (CIN a cause) - remember a normal smear within 3y makes cervical CA very very unlikely, can smear only if it is due 

Ectroption is inner columnar skin cells coming outside cervix to squamous skin cells usually hormone (oestrogen dependent) in pregnancy COCP - can treat with laser

Polyp are fleshy lump in endocervical canal , refer for polypectomy + Bx

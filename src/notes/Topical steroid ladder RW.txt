Topical steroid ladder RW

Mild- Hydrocortisone acetate 1% (HC)

Moderate 
• Betnovate RD (0.025%)
• Eumovate (clobetasone), also in Trimovate with nystatin & oxytetracycline

Potent 
• Betnovate (0.1%)
• Elocon (mometasone)
• Also cutivate & locoid (hydrocortisone butyrate)

Very potent - Dermovate (clobetasol)

Usually max 14d for potent+ , do not use very potent on children without derm advice, for non potent max up to 6w really should be less 

Protopic if not working 
• Topical tacrolimus (0.03% protopic <2yrs, 0.1% adults, protopic ointment, pimecrolimus cream)
• Usually only GPwSI or derm specialist

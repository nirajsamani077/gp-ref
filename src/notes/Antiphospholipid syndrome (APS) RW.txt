Antiphospholipid syndrome (APS) RW

Systemic AI condition causing recurrent thrombosis & miscarriage/ pregnancy comps  
• Often young arterial & VTE
• >=3 miscarriage 1st TM, early onset PET/HELLP
• May have livedo reticularis or thrombocytopenia

Refer rheum or recurrent miscarriage clinic 
• Anticardiolipin & antiB2 GPI antibodies + lupus  anticoagulant (functional coagulation assay)

If Dx should avoid any hormones eg HRT COCP as very high risk VTE/stroke & if had a clot then lifelong warfarin (if recurrent may need to change INR target 3-4 or add aspirin /other), if no clot already d/w specialist .

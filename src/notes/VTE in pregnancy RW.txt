VTE in pregnancy RW

Significant increased risk, PE cause of death, 5x higher than non-pregnant women & can occur at any TM and up to 6w post partum 
• RF previous, age >35, BMI >30, smoker, thrombophilia, multiple, HG, comorbid (SLE IBD) = LMWH usually either from start or 28w till delivery and continued 10d to 6w post natally, under ANC 

If high risk /FHx thrombophilia or VTE <50 1st deg relative consider screening prepregnancy via haem = APS, AT3, F5 Leiden 

If clinically suspecting DVT or PE refer immediately !

MCCD/coroners 

Old age only allowed as 1a if >80
Can only use organ failure if 1b specifies cause 

Family take MCCD to local Registrar of Births/Deaths/Marriages, if decision made not to report to Coroner then issues certificate for Burial/Cremation, certificate of register of death for social services & copies of death register if requested for banks/insurance 

Notifiable to coroner 
• unexpected, sudden, accident/injury, neglect
• suicide, poisoning 
• prisoner/police cust, service disability pensioners
• intra-operative or in recovery  
• death within 24hr of admission
• no doctor seen within 14d 
• stillbirth if doubt if child born alive 
• industrial disease (asbestosis)

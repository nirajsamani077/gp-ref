Precocious puberty 

F - SSC <8 (thelarche, adrenarche)
M - SSC <9

Can be
- Gonadotrophin dep/central (raised LH/FSH)
- Gonadotropin indep/pseudo (low LH/FSH) 

F usually idiopathic or familal but rare organic is McCune Albright with rapid onset, neuro sx

M almost always organic/central 
• small testes = adrenal cause (tumour, CAH)
• big testes = intracranial cause (LH/FSH tumour)
• unilateral big testis = testicular tumour

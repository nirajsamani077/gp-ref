HIV drugs (antiretrovirals) RW

At time of diagnosis (used to be CD4 <200)
3 drugs = 2NRTI + NNRTI/PI

NRTI (nucleoside reverse transcriptase inhibitor)
• most cause peripheral neuropathy 
• zidovudine (AZT) - anaemia, myopathy, black nail
• tenofovir - osteoporosis, renal impairment 
• didanosine - pancreatitis 
• abacavir, emtricitabine, lamivudin

NNRTI (non nucleoside)
• nevirapine, efavirenz
• CYP450 inducers (reduce levels)

PI (protease inhibitor)
• “avirs” - indinavir (renal, biliary stones) ritonavir
• metabolic syndrome 

Common for interactions, any issues check with pharmacy or GUM! HIV drug interactions app

Barrett’s oesophagus RW

Chronic GORD causing metaplasia of junction (distal oesophagus, change of cell lining from squamous to columnar /intestinal) which can become dysplastic & malignant transformation to adenocarcinoma (3-5%) = endoscopic Dx 

If found, needs OGD surveillance under gastro 
• Non dysplastic 
   - <3cm affected = every 3-5y
   - >3 affected = every 2-3y
• Dysplastic 
  - Indefinite dysplasia = every 6mo 
  - Low-grade dysplasia = needs ablation 
  - High-grade dysplasia = 2WW treatment 

Treatment 
• Only offer PPI if symptomatic (not enough evidence to show slows progression), unless indefinite dysplasia which is associated with inflammation so should all have PPI
• Endoscopic ablation or surgery (fundoplication)

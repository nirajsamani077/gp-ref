Impetigo 

Staph or GAS infection on face/around mouth spread by direct contact with discharges from other peoples scabs (very contagious)
Gold crusted lesions 

Limited - 1% hydrogen peroxide cream (TDS 5d) now preferred over topical abx (fusidic acid 2% or mupirocin 2% TDS 5d if resistant) 
Extensive or Bullous - fluclox (erythro if pen allergy)5d 
Wash with soap and water BD

School exclusion until lesions crusted or 48hrs after Abx started 

Bullous (blisters that burst) vs non Bullous (mild itchy, gold crust) 

If recurrent send skin swab and nasal swab for MRSA.

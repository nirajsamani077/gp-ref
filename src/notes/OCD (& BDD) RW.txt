OCD (& BDD) RW

Anxiety disorder with obsessions with certain things (eg dirty table/fear of contamination) & resulting compulsions (eg repetitive behaviour to clean table) which interfere with life/functioning and cause ++distress
(Other compulsions - locking doors, washing hands, counting or checking something)

Screen with a simple question about compulsion & explore - assess risk mood self harm suicide etc 

Use CBT framework - thoughts/emotions impact on behaviours/physical symptoms & vice versa
Also frameworks of graded exposure, behavioural activation, mindfullness/meditation, breathing 

Most mild-moderate can be managed by GP with low intensity CBT with exposure & relapse prevention (ERP) - if fails or severe then add SSRI (sertraline, fluoxetine for body dysmorphic disorder) & refer (esp children with suspected OCD) - continue SSRI at least 1y post remission to prevent relapse 

Body dysmorphic disorder (BDD) = variant with obsession with defect in physical appearance - same treatment as above (may confuse with delusions but not psychotic) - high risk of suicide for both BDD & OCD..

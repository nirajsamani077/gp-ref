Benefits/payments 

Chronic disease/cancer: 

Personal independence payment (PIP) - if <65yrs, replaces disability living allowance, must have difficulty with ADLs for >3mo & expected to last >9mo (ie 1yr in total) 

Attendance allowance - if >65yrs, help with personal care, should have needed help >6mo already, not means tested 

Fast-track DS1500 form - terminal illness (LE <6mo) to claim above + incapacity benefit/employment support allowance. Automatically receives highest rate, fee payable to DWP for completion, give to patient 

Death: 
Funeral payment - one off payment to relative of deceased if on benefits already 

Bereavement Support payment - lump sum then 18 monthly payments if under state pension age & NI contributions 

Widowed parents allowance - to parent whose partner died, if child young, NI contributions (can claim if woman was expecting late husbands baby)

Sickness:

Statutory sick pay - employee off sick >4d in a row, earn >£112/week, tell work before deadline or 7d if no deadline

3rd/4th degree tear RW 

Post delivery perineal tears
• 1st deg = perineal skin only 
• 2nd deg = perineal muscles not sphincters 
• 3rd deg = anal sphincter affected 
• 4th deg = anal sphincter & epithelium 

If 3/4th degree should be repaired in theatre, high risk of infection, need ABx 
• Ongoing PFE 6-12w & review in Gynae clinic 
• Faecal incontinence/urgency pain = direct referral (as suggests sphincter dysfunction) & May need further repair .

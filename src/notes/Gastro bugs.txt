Gastro bugs

Short incubation <6hrs 
• Bacilus - rice
• Staph aureus - severe vomiting 

Mid incubation <3d
• E Coli - travellers diarrhoea, most common 
• Salmonella 
• Shigella - bloody diarrhoea 
• Campylobacter - flu prodrome, bloody diarrhoea, may mimic appendicitis or cause GBS

Long incubation >7d
• Giarda - prolonged non bloody diarrhoea 
• Amoeba - prolonged bloody diarrhoea

Vulvovaginitis RW

Candida or STI - most common 

Other causes 
• Irritant dermatitis & other vulval dermatoses 
• Atrophic vaginitis 
• Drug reaction or allergy (esp NSAIDs)
• Desquaminative inflammatory vaginitis (less common “aerobic” vaginitis with petechia & discharge (micro like BV) - 2% clindamycin cream for 3w then maintenance and refer to R/O
• Malignancy! 

Tips 
• If cyclical consider oestrogenic
• Ask douching, products, creams, sex, condoms
• Patchy erythema petechiae think DV
• Loss of architecture/labial fusion/ erosions think lichen planus or drug eruption 
• Don’t forget FGM

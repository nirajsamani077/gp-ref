Plantar fasciitis RW

Common overuse “enthesopathy” where plantar fascia ligament from toes to ankle supporting arches become progressively irritated - heel pain worse with walking hard floors barefoot esp first few steps 

DDx 
• Achilles tendinopathy - usually on not under heel 
• Subtalar arthritis/OA
• Calcaneal issues (Severs, stress fracture) 
• S1 radiculopathy 

Clinical Dx

Rx = no best treatment, avoid aggravating factors weight loss, stretching exercises (using stairs /towel, versus arthritis sheet), orthotics/night splint , steroid injection might help for 1mo but many don’t remain better and risk fat pad wasting 

Most better by 1y but some chronic course

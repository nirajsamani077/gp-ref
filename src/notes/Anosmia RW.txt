Anosmia RW

Isolated = 
• Post COVID, if so signpost to support and olfactory training (post viral sensorineural loss= common)
• Not then consider nasal steroids if local sx as well (not within 2w of acute covid)
• If failed >12w post COVID or >6w not post-COVID then refer ENT 

With new neuro sx = refer same day ?stroke/vasc
If long-standing neuro consider SOL/PD/AD
If UL with nasal obstruction/pain/epistaxis/otalgia/neck lump = 2WW ?post nasal space tumour 
Rare = Kallmanns (hypothalamic), heavy metals 

Any loss of taste also - loss of flavour perception is retro-nasal olfaction 

Olfactory training from AbScent & Fifth Sense, ENT UK leaflet

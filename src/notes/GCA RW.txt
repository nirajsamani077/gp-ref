GCA RW

Think of elderly (anyone >50) with subacute temporal headache, jaw claudication, scalp tenderness, visual symptoms 

Take bloods same day CRP ESR FBC, then start 60mg pred if feel GCA most likely Dx, then refer urgently
• Same day Ophthalm if any visual stuff (sx, RAPD, pale optic disc on fundi)
• Same day rheum otherwise for ongoing, probably need TAB , CT angio or duplex USS 
• Shared care f/u 

Check tenderness TA or loss of Ta pulse, VA, fields, RAPD, carotid bruit, fundi 
Alt DDx - bleed, migraine, cluster, trigeminal, sinus or shingles before rash 

Typical pred regimen 
• 40-60mg for 4 weeks
• Taper by 10mg every 2 weeks to 20mg
• Taper by 2.5mg every 2-4 weeks to 10mg
• Taper by 1mg every 4-8 weeks to 0
• Along with = PPI, bone protection (often before DEXA), no longer aspirin - ensure steroid emergency care from pharmacy and discuss sick day rules 

If sx return then back to higher pred but if new claudication or systemic upset discuss urgent with rheum, new visual change urgent Ophthalm 

Don’t keep rechecking inflamm markers

Perianal abscess/fistula RW

Tender red swelling, may discharge, may be deeper and harder to Dx (vague perianal pain), can cause sepsis or fistula
• Fistulae rarely occur alone, often abscess (1/3 have them) but can be other causes (IBD, CA post surgery)

DDx - Anal fissure, thrombosed haemorrhoid, rectal prolapse, STI, malignancy, hydradenitis 

Management = surgical referral same day 
• Almost all need I&D even if spontaneously discharging, under LA or GA 
• Abx don’t improve healing rates or prevent recurrence, may be used if severe /IC 
• Post-op  recurrence/sepsis should be considered  (otherwise first episode doesn’t need F/U, if recurs needs colorectal F/U).

Headlice /pubic lice RW 

Peducilis capitis lice feed on blood, eggs hatch and empty shells are “nits” - any hairy areas scalp eyelash eyebrows body hair 

Direct contact, 2-3 weeks for symptoms (many AS but can cause itch or irritation) 

Dx - fine toothed comb showing “nits” or lice

Treat if living lice found, with wet combing & insecticides to patient & whole household 
• Malathion (Prioderm) 0.5% rub onto dry hair, keep on overnight & washout, repeat again after 1w to catch live lice from already hatched eggs & avoid close contact till completed)
• Alts = Permethrin (Lyclear) - same way rubbed 
• Need 100ml/head to give effective treatment 

Non-insecticide based shampoo less associated with resistance & may be more effective but are strong chemicals we may not want to use 
• Dimeticone (Hedrin) - apply as above, highly flammable big risk of burns 
• Isopropyl myristate (Full Marks Solution) - only need to be kept on for 10mins .

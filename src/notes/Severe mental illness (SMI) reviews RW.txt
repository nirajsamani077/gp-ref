Severe mental illness (SMI) reviews RW

= those with schizophrenia, psychosis, BAD or severe depression/anxiety/PTSD/OCD under secondary care - at high risk of physical health issues so need an SMI annual review 
• BMI & weight-to-height ratio 
• BP/HR, HbA1C/lipids (prolactin if antipsychotic) 
• Assess diet, exercise, smoking, ETOH, drugs 
• Assess social situation (housing, carers etc)

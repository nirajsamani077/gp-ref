Colorectal CA diagnosis & treatment RW

See 2WW FIT section 
• Weight loss, abdo pain, PR bleeding, CIBH, IDA…

RFs - Age, male, FHx (FAP, Lynch), IBD (esp UC), multiple polyps, red /processed meat, low fibre, smoking ETOH obesity

Ix - FBC & FIT = main primary care tests! 
• Colonoscopy + Bx = gold standard but requires bowel prep which needs sedation, can dehydrate patients (HF, CKD, diuretics or hypokalaemia) & requires a procedure that may not be tolerated 
• CT colonography alternative with no sedation & can pick up other bowel extra-luminal pathology but worse specificity & if positive would still need a colonoscopy 

Management - MDT colorectal CNS oncologist 
• Depends on stage spread etc, if lower rectal may have lower anterior resection (rectal sphincter sparing) but can cause difficulty with BO, otherwise colectomy, Hartman’s etc - stoma depending on what’s removed,chemoradiotherapy/palliative 
• CEA & CT monitoring in secondary care 
• Screen all diagnosed for Lynch syndrome & give high dose aspirin for all if positive

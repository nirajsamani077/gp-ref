Palpitations RW

Common, Hx critical to identify serious (cardiac) vs benign (anxiety) & everything in between (POTs, caffeine, ETOH, drugs, thyroid, electrolyte, phaeo)
• Before during after, irregular fast skipped beats 
• Exertional, syncope, chest pain/SOB = ?cardiac 
• FHx of SCD 

Cardiac causes 
• Sinus tachy 
• Ectopic beats (“skipped” or “missed” beats)
• PAF, flutter, SVT or non sustained VT
• Cardiomyopathy - rarely the cause but frequent VE’s >30/hour in older patient may indicate 
• WPW or LQTS 

Examine - HR, BP postural, murmur, CCF, thyroid 

Check
• Bloods - inc FBC, U&E, TFT
• ECG - often NSR, may show WPW/LQTS/AV block
   - If normal and sounds typical infrequent ectopics then usually don’t need further Ix 
   - If more than this refer for 24-72h tape/Holter or implantable loop recorder (ILR - Reveal device), will only pick up if having frequent sx 

Don’t drive if arrhythmias causing incapacity & probably inform DVLA at this point or persistent no cause found yet

Acute otitis media (AOM) RW

Common in children, fever earache irritable, red bulging TM, most will get better without abx unless high risk
• <2 with BL OM, perf (otorrhoea) or IC/Downs 
• Amox or clary/erythro (coamox if worsening + admission) 5-7d 
• Delayed or immediate script

If not using ABx and no perf then consider topical anaesthetic drops (Otigo - phenolazine + lidocaine) 4 drops TDS til symptoms resolve - safe and often prevents need for Abx in low risk cases

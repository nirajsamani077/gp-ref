Wilsons criteria for screening 

Screening programme needs to meet specific criteria  

- should be important 
- natural history understood 
- diagnosis and tx cost effective
- recognisable latent/early symptomatic stage 
- mx effective if started early 
- easy test to perform, interpret, acceptable, accurate, reliable, sensitive, specific 
- accepted mx 
- policy on who should be treated 
- case finding conitnuous

HRT management 

Lifestyle 
- avoid late evening exercise, weight loss, sleep hygeine, stress 

HRT - usually menopause last 2-5y 

C/I
- current breast CA or any oestrogen sensitive cancer 
- unexplained vaginal bleeding or untreated endometrial hyperplasia

Risks
- VTE, only with oral slightly, nil transdermal 
- Breast CA, risk with all combined but no increased risk of dying from it (ovarian CA also increased risk) 
- Stroke/CHD, slight increased risk esp with oral 

Non-hormonal 
- flushes/sweats - SSRI 2 week, clonidine (sympatholytic) 24 weeks & may take 2-4 weeks to see effect 
- psych - CBT, SSRgzI
- urogenital atrophy - vaginal oestrogen/moisturiser 

Tibolone (livial) is synthetic steroid with oestrogen/progestagen/androgen activity used as alt to combined continuous HRT (>12 months after LMP). OD tablet, may not be as effective as combined HRT.

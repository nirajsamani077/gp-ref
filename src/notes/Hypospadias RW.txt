Hypospadias RW

Congenital defect where urethral opening located on underside of penis - occurs in 1/250 boys 
• If noticed refer paeds surgery for operation in first year of life - most successful with no LT comps or consequences 
• Check FHx, association with Inguinal hernia, congenital cryptoorchidism renal tract Abs 

Oxford University Hospitals Trust leaflet useful!
• Do not have baby circumcised before repair as have to use foreskin to help repair .

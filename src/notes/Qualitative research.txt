Qualitative research 

Big words:
Etnography - study of culture /social interaction. 
Phenomology - lived experience 
Grounded theory - theory about phenomena of interest 

Sampling types:
Opportunistic sampling - first come first served 
Purposive sampling - preselected criteria 
Snowballing sampling - contact uses social network to increase sample 

Assessing validity:
Triangulation - compares results from >1 source
Respondent validation - member checking, comparing investigator and patients accounts
Bracketing - putting aside own belief 
Reflexivity - how data collection is shaped

Analytical approach:
Content analysis - interviews transcribed to make categories/text
Constant comparison - based on grounded theory, in time concepts from data

SSRI tips RW

Fluoxetine - best in younger, no need for weaning
Citalopram - prolongs QTc (max dose 40mg, 20mg if >65 or liver impairment)
Sertraline - best post MI & anxiety 
Paroxetine - most interaction, discontinuation sx & congenital malformations in 1st TM

Review after 2 weeks after starting (1 week if young <30yrs or higher suicide risk)
Continue Rx for 6 months 
Wean off over 4 weeks (not fluoxetine)

Increased risk of GI bleeding so generally avoid if also taking NSAID, but if needs use PPI cover. Use mirtazipine instead if on warfarin/DOAC
Triptans/MAOI increase risk of serotonin syndrome

Discontinuation sx similar to ETOH withdrawal 

In pregnancy small increased risk of congenital heart disease in 1st TM (esp paroxetine) & persistent pulmonary HTN in 3rd TM, weigh up risks and benefits 

Breastfeeding - sertraline (& paroxetine OK)

SSRI to another - withdraw then start new (fluoxetine leave 1 week washout break due to long half life)
SSRI to SNRI - can cross taper apart from fluoxetine (withdraw first as long half life)
SSRI to TCA - can cross taper apart from fluoxetine (withdraw first as long half life)

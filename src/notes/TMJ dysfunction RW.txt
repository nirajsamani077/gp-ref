TMJ dysfunction RW

Common pain with jaw muscles talking eating smiling - multifactorial (stress, myofasical pain, anatomy, arthritis, overlap with fibromyalgia)- can cause clicking, associated with teeth grinding 

DDx important! 
• Temporal headache and jaw claudication in elderly = GCA!!!
• If intermittent UL sharp lancing pain = trigeminal neuralgia 
• Bisphosphonates or denusomab think risk of osteonecrosis of jaw (dental check prior to starting, if having dental procedure use chlorhexidine mouth wash & avoid extractions)

Treatment 
• No good treatment , most resolve without Rx
• Soft diets, avoid chewing gum, warm massage, simple analgesia 
• Dental check esp if grinding ? Jaw split. 
• CBT  psychosocial 
• Refer max fax if not improving

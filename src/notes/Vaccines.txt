Vaccines

Live (avoid if immunocompromised)
• MMR, BCG, VZV
• Oral polio/typhoid/rotavirus
• Yellow fever 
• Intranasal influenza

Inactivated 
• IM influenza 
• Hep A
• Rabies
• DTP (toxoid)

Subunit/conjugate
• Pneumococcus/meningococcus/haemophilius
• Hep B
• HPV

For MMR contraindicated if received another live vaccine within/after 4wks, allergy to neomycin, recent immunoglobulin within 3mo, immunosuppresion or pregnancy 
Can cause similar illness after first dose, after 5-10d

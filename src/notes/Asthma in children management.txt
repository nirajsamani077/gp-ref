Asthma in children management 

Move to next step if symptoms >3x per week or night time waking 

5-16 (similar to adults)
- SABA PRN
- + Paed low dose ICS (Clenil)
- + LTRA (montelukast)
- + LABA (stop montelukast if not helping)
- switch LABA/ICS to MART with low dose ICS + LABA or change to separate LABA & medium dose ICS
- consider specialist review, high dose ICS or theophylline 

<5
- SABA PRN
- + Paed moderate dose ICS 8 week trial, if no change then alt diagnosis, if resolved then recurred within 4 weeks of stopping then maintain on low dose ICS, if recurred after 4 weeks of stopping then repeat trial 
- + LTRA (montelukast) 
- Refer to specialist 

MART contain steroid & fast acting LABA (formoterol) used both for maintenance and relief 

Steroid doses in paeds = budesonide equivalent 
- low dose <200mcg
- moderate dose 200-400mcg
- high dose >400mcg

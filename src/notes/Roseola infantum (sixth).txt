Roseola infantum (sixth)

6mo-2yrs very common HHV-6 viral exanthem

High fever (+ associated febrile convulsions in 10%) first for few days which settles then…

Macpap rash generalised usually lasting 2 days (not starting on face like MMR) that doesn’t blister (unlike chickenpox, HFM) 
Nagayama spots (red papules on soft palate)

May rarely cause hepatitis/meningitis 

Reassurance, no Rx, no school exclusion

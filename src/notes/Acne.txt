Acne 

Mild - comedomes, few inflammatory lesions 
Mod - widespread inflammatory papules/pustules 
Severe - extensive inflammatory + nodule/cysts/scarring

3 months/8-12 weeks of 
- single topical therapy (retinoid e.g adalepene, (differin), benzoylperoxide (acneicide), azelaic acid)
- combo topical therapy (epiduo = adalepene + benzoylperoxide, duac = clindamycin + benzoylperoxide)
- oral ABX (lymecycline, doxycycline but avoid tetracycline if <12yrs, pregnant or breastfeeding so erythromycin) - only use max 3 months & always give in combination with topical adalepene or benzoylperoxide to prevent resistance but never with topical abx. Gram negative folliculitis occurs after long term ABx use, responsive to high-dose trimethoprim.
- COCP (anti-androgenic) such as Dianette (co-cyrindol) in combo with topical treatments, but higher risk of VTE so only use short term 3 months or go with Microgynon
- Refer for oral isotretioin

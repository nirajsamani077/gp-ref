Molluscum PCDS RW

Clusters of small shiny flesh coloured umbillicated papules, can become inflamed & crusty, due to pox virus, very contagious from direct contact 

Most do not need any treatment and resolve by itself within a year although some can persist for up to 5yrs!!
• Avoid scratching/squeezing & sharing towels 
• May flare up ezcema - treat as usual steroids etc 
• May have bacterial superinfection - Abx 

If treatment wanted then Mollutinc 10% OTC or Molludab 5% up to BD for 2w (potassium hydroxide) or cryotherapy for stubborn lesions 

Refer derm if - IC (extensive disease HIV) or difficult area (anogenital need GUM STI screening, eyelid with red eye needs ophthalmology)

New potential drug = Berdazimir (NO releasing gel) may be available in the future

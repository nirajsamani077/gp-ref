Child development milestones RW

Take parental concerns seriously - usually right 
• Baby check 6-8w, HV 10mo and 2.5y rv otherwise opportunistic 

Refer any child to paeds if regression, high risk premi (<30/40) would have specialist surveillance til 2y or 4y if very prem, or red flags 
• 6w - no response to sound/visual stimuli 
• 3mo - lack of smile/social response
• 6mo - no reaching for objects or head control 
• 9mo - no babbling 
• 12mo - can’t sit unsupported, not weight bearing 
• 18mo - can’t walk, no words (can’t walk talk) 
• 2y - can’t run/join 2 words, doesn’t share interest 
• 3y - can’t climb stairs or communicate words 
• Any - loss of skill, concerns about vision/hearing, assymetric movements <1y esp,  high or low tone, toe walking only, crossed 2 centile, HC >99.6th or <0.4th centile 

For all 
• Full height weight HC and plot on RCPH chart 
• Check FBC, TFT, bone, vit D, CK (only if not referring and having period of observation)
• Ensure full visual and hearing checks 
• Share care HV /physio etc 

For premis used corrected age til 2y for milestones

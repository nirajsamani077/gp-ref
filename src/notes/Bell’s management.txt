Bell’s management 

High dose prednisone within 72h 
Eye care (artificial tears/drops, tape eye closed at night) to prevent exposure keratopathy

If not improved at all by 3 weeks - refer urgently to ENT

Most fully recover by 3 months but 15% have moderate to severe weakness (refer plastics if not fully recovered by 6mo)

Aciclovir isn’t routinely recommended, speak with neuro if needed

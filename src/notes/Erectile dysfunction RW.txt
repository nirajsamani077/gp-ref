Erectile dysfunction RW

Any man with CVD/DM/HTN should be screened 
Endocrine (hypogonadism, hyperthyroid, high PRL)
Prostate problems /TURP /esp CA can cause 
Drugs, ETOH, stress/MH all causes 

Hx - Check timings (with partner, alone, everytime, morning erections - if not consider organic), ejaculatory, libido, testicular problems, urinary sx & overall health /stress/etoh./smoking /fhx /meds/ Mh
• Drug causes - SSRI, ACs, Bb, diuretics, steroids, finasteride, phenothiazines (PRL), H2RAs

Examine - BP, BMI, testicular/genital, Prolactinoma if ? (Bitemporal hemianopia), consider DRE & PSA. QRISK all 

Bloods for all baseline +
• HbA1C/lipids, TFT, PSA after counselling
• Testosterone + SHBG (fasting early morning) - if abnormal check LH FSH
• PRL if sx suggestive of Prolactinoma (neuro, galactorrhoea etc)

Rx 
• Lifestyle = deal with CVD/endocrine issue, stop smoking/ETOH, stop causative drugs, less cycling <3h per week etc

• PDE5 inhibitor - increase blood flow to penis by relaxing blood vessel supplying it, work 3/4 times but less effective if DM/hypogonadism/TURP or RP
 - Do not use nitrates, nicorandil, alpha blockers risk hypotension, blue pill blue eye AION, not within  6mo of MI/stroke 
 - Common S/E flushing nasal cong dizziness 
 - Still need sexual arousal, take on empty stomach before sex, avoid etoh/grapefruit juice as interacts 
 - Sildenafil (Viagra) OTC cheapest take 1h before - lasts 4h (25-100mg titrate up based on response 6wkly)
 - Tadalifil (Cialis) expensive take 30min before - lasts 17h, 5mg can be used regularly due to half life 
 - Avanifil 15min before - lasts 15h
 - Vardenafil 30min before - lasts 4h

• Refer if young and always had, abnormal exam, no response to 2x PDE5 used at least 8 times at highest dose - urology consider vacuum pump/alprostadil injections 

Premature ejaculation 
• Primary = lifelong, needs drug therapy (any SSRI like paroxetine off licence, EMLA private option) 
• Secondary = after previous normal, check reversible causes (relationship, prostate issues), usually behavioural (squeeze technique or start/stop technique)

Graphs 

Box & whisker - sample minimum, lower quartile, median, upper quartile and sample maximum

Scatter plot - coordinates to display values for two variables for a set of data

Histogram - continuous data where the values have been categorised into a number of categories(continuous bar chart) 

Forest plot - meta-analyses and provide a graphical representation of the strength of evidence of the constituent trials

Funnel plot - publication bias in meta-analyses

Kaplan-Meier curve - survival function showing decreasing survival with time

Cates diagram - 4 face categories, NNT = total number over number who benefitted, NNH = total number over number harmed

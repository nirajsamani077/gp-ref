Short stature RW

4 presentations 
• SS +  symptoms of chronic illness (GI, headaches, anaemia, dysmorphic, delayed puberty)
• Height <2nd centile (<0.4th off bottom red flag)
• FTT dropping >1 centiles 
• Height >3 centile spaces below MPH

Plot height weight HC and MPH (mid parental height) using WHO or RCPCH charts /online tool 

Refer if red flags

Trochanteric bursitis RW

Greater trochanteric pain syndrome or lateral hip pain = tendinopathy of gluteus medius , tenderness worse on lying on side 

DDx - OA, lumbar referred pain, ITBS 

Single leg standing 30s hard but putting socks hard easy (more OA)

Tendinopathy treatment - RICE, sleep with pillow between knees, avoid sitting cross legged, eccentric exercises esp hip abductors, NSAIDs, steroid injection for ST, physio shockwave USS

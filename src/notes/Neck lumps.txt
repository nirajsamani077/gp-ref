Neck lumps 

Branchial cyst - Benign developmental lump filled with cholesterol crystals, prone to infection & enlarge with URTI, usually anteriolateral triangle in late childhood AS L sided soft smooth slowly enlarging and can fistulate - refer to ENT, USS and FNAC

Other congenital - cystic hygroma, dermoid cyst, thyroglossal cyst, vascular malformation 
Inflammatory - LN, reactive lymphangitis
Malignancy - lymphoma, thyroid, salivary

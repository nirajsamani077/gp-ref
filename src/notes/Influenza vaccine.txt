Influenza vaccine 

Contains egg protein, so don’t give if egg allergy 
In adults it’s inactivated but children it’s live and intranasal, first given 2-3y then annually 
Don’t give if immunosuppressed child, ill, taking aspirin (risk of Reyes), breastfeeding 
Store 2-8degs in dark, 75% effective, 2 weeks for full effect, 2 days of fever/malaise 
In adults, all >65y annually, at risk workers or chronic conditions 

Asthma only indication if using steroid inhaler (not SABA alone)

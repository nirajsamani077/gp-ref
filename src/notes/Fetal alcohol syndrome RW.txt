Fetal alcohol syndrome RW

Lifelong condition caused by maternal alcohol intake & exposure to developing features 

Always causes neurodevelopmental problems (LD, microcephaly) & has 3 classic facial features 
• Flat philtrum
• Short palpebral fissures
• Thin top lip 

Advise women that safest to avoid any ETOH in pregnancy & monitor this during pregnancy - if considering FAS refer to specialist for management .

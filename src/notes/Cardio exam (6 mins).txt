Cardio exam (6 mins) 

Wash hands, introduce and consent for exam 
Gen inspection & around bed 
Hands & nails 
Radial pulse, RR delay & collapsing pulse 
Face - under eyes & mouth tongue out then up 
Neck - JVP (+ HJ) and carotid pulse 
Chest inspection - scars, device 
Chest palpation - apex, L parasternal heave, thrills 
Chest auscultation 
• Apex (5 MCL) + time S1 + 2 with carotid pulse 
• Mitral (5 MCL) bell, axilla, roll left, end expiration 
• Tricuspid (4 LSE) inspiration if PSM TR 
• Pulmonary (2 LSE) focus on S2 split, P2? 
• Aortic (2 RSE) carotids, end expiration, if ?diastolic murmur AR sit forward & end expiration at same area (root) & LSE (valvular AR)
Lung bases + sacral oedema 
Pulsatile liver edge only if ?TR
Legs for oedema 

Complete + offer bedside observations including BP, urine dipstick and fundoscopy

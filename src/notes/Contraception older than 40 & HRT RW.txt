Contraception older than 40 & HRT RW

If using CHC/COCP go with low dose lower risk (20mcg pills), consider CV VTE risk and switching to POP/implant/Mirena which can be used up to 55yo through menopause and on HRT (whereas COCP and depot can’t be used after 50yo)

Can use POP or implant alongside HRT, Mirena is only one that provides endometrial protection, consider osteoporosis risk of depot 

If fitted after age of 45 a Mirena can attach in till 55 if being used for HRT (if contraception alone then only 5y)

Remember if >50 amenorrheic due to contraception check FSH, if elevated can stop contraception after 1y (only the depot intereferes with FSH level so check just before next injection due , again less likely to use depot around this age)

Can stop contraception if 2y amenorrhoeic <50 or 1y amenorrhoeic >50, or even if periods >55 

COCP can help with menopausal sx so can be used instead of HRT up to 50 if contraception also needed but generally can add POP or other progestagenic for contraception alone

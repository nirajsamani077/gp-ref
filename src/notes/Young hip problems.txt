Young hip problems 

DDH - usually newborn pick-up, unequal leg length 

Perthes - AVN of femoral head causing flattened degenerated femoral head, 4-8yo boys >> girls, 10% bilateral, progressive hip pain/stiffness & lump 

SUFE - displaced femoral head epiphysis posteroinferiorly (back & down) in usually obese boys aged 10-15, causes more thigh/knee pain & limited IR ++, 20% bilateral slip, frog leg lateral XR, needs internal fixation with screw 

Irritable hip/transient synovitis - acute limp with viral infection usually <10 (R/O septic joint)

JIA - <16 with arthritis lasting >3 months, rest pain, pauciarticular with 4 medium joints or less is most common causing limp, ANA +, anterior uveitis 

Any acute limping child <3 should have urgent Ax ?septic arthritis

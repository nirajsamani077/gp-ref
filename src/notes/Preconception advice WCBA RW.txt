Preconception advice WCBA RW

Use good contraception until wanting to conceive 
Fertility starts to decline age >35 as risks go up 

Healthy Start programme provides free multivitamins if on benefits/low income - from 10w pregnancy & onwards 

Folic acid 400mcg pregnacare multivitamin inc vitamin D 10mcg/d for at least 3mo prior to conception prevent NTD until 12w but 5mg dose if higher risk 
• FHx NTD, epilepsy on AED, BMI >30, DM, Coeliac 
• Hx of thalassemia or sickle cell = 5mg whole preg  & need to be under haem monitoring risk anaemia & will have Hbopathy screen as part of booking 

NB routine iron supp not needed, if booking Hb <110 or 28w Hb <105 then should be given iron 
• Avoid any vit A supps >700mcg = teratogenic!
• Diet - ensure good iodine levels fish dairy to prevent any subclinical hypothyroid worsening!

Stop smoking/ETOH risks PET loss FAS congenital 
If planning pregnancy discuss drugs which need to be stopped eg DMARDs statins MH meds & 
• Ensure UTD cervical screening, MMR, shingles & Hep B if indicated 

Important to manage long term health conditions & ensure they are optimal prior to conceiving 
• Hypothyroid needs higher doses & TSH <2.5
• DM control can worsen, avoid statins ACEs 
• Epilepsy rheum IBD must discuss with specialist  
   - Valproate “prevent programme” as +++ risk of NTD and need to be on LARC & annual risk acknowledgement form 
• BP renal big RF for PET, change labetolol (100mg BD), nifedipine or methyldopa
• Avoid pregnancy is severe cardiac/resp disease 
   - Congenital HD discuss with specific cardiologist obstetric specialist preconception
   - ACE, warfarin, amiodarone, spiro, atenolol should all be avoided 
   - Major reason for maternal & foetal deaths 

Signpost The Family Planning Association & the Healthy Start Programme if eligible .

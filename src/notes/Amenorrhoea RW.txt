Amenorrhoea RW

No period 6mo if periods before (secondary) 
No period by 15 (primary), or 13 if no SCCs 

By far 2 most common causes (60%)
• Hypothalamic (functional hypothalamic amen) - female athlete triad low BMI stress exercise in primary and secondary (other primary = constitutional, endocrine, GU anatomy (imperforate hymen), chromosomal (Turners), Kallmans
• PCOS in secondary (other secondary = POF, Prolactinoma, endocrine, CNS or Sheehans, Ashermans intrauterine adhesions)

Secondary
• Ask other Gynae Sx, androgen XS, headache/neuro sx (pituitary), contraception /pregnancy and life/hobbies (female athlete triad )
• R/O pregnancy 
• Check FSH and LH (both low = hypothalamic, both high esp FSH >30 when age <40= POF), oestrodiol (similarly low in both but fluctuates), PRL (>1000 significant), FAI (>5) with testosterone & SHBG but total >5 ?tumour/CAH, TFT
• TV USS if ?PCOS, refer endocrine or Gynae any issues like for MRI pituitary 

Primary 
• Ask about SSCs (breast voice hair) and timings
• FHx ?constitutional ?chromosomal 
• Female athlete triad 
• Refer all after tests as above 

Progestagen challenge to bring back period?
• MPA 10mg 5-10d then stop to see if period comes (progestagen should cause bleed by mimicking the high progesterone levels which usually occur after ovulation, if bleed occurs then good amounts of oestrogen which would help the lining proliferate and then bleed once progestagen drops) so…
A) If works /bleed = good levels of oestrogen, but no ovulation I.E. PCOS most likely 
B) If doesn’t work = low levels of oestrogen I.E POF or hypothalamic; or an outflow disorder. 
• Next time add 50mcg oestrogen patch for 21d then the MPA like a normal cycle and if no withdrawal bleed then can only be outflow 

Remember female athlete triad at high risk of amenorrhoea/infertility, osteoporosis, eating disorder - encourage weight gain (rather than COCP to “regulate” cycles), some might benefit from regimen as above with oestrogen patch + cyclical progestagen to protect effects

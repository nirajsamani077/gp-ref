Anaphylaxis RW

A-E
Lie down with legs up
Adrenaline IM 1:1000 1mg/ml (AL thigh)
• Adult >12 = 0.5mg (0.5ml)
• Child 6-12 = 0.3mg (0.3ml)
• Child <6 = 0.15mg (0.15ml)
Repeat after 5min if no improvement 
All admission for at least 6h monitoring (biphasic response) & referral allergy clinic 

Autoinjectors 
• Must carry 2 at all times wherever you are (eg for school, home), so prescribe but be pragmatic (don’t need 2 for home 2 for purse 2 for car etc - should be taking with them)
• Options are Epipen, Emrerade, Jext (if <15kg then 150mcg pens, otherwise 300mcg pens and now adults can have 500mcg pens)

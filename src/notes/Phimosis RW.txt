Phimosis RW

Often physiological in children, mostly asymptomatic but can cause painful erection/intercourse, difficulty keeping clean, rarely ballooning of foreskin with difficulty PU

Tight preputial ring 
https://www.rch.org.au/clinicalguide/guideline_index/the_penis_and_foreskin/

Primary = not resolved from birth 
Secondary = due to skin disorder (balanitis, BZO/lichen sclerosis of penis, poorly controlled DM)

Retract foreskin gently check skin, maybe tight band distallly, check skin and for hypospadias 

Rx only if symptomatic 
• Potent steroid betnovate BD 4-6w with gentle retraction as far as can go each time (if any fungal balanitis give Canesten)
• Refer urology if not improving, concerns re lichen sclerosis (needs dermovate 3mo), for surgical options(usually circumcision)

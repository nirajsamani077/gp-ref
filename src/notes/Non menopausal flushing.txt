Non menopausal flushing 

Due to body’s nervous system directing blood to the surface of facial skin = redness, warmth, burning (occurs normally in exercise/overheating) but rosacea can occur with very little trigger and be debilitating 

Topical (brimodine) or oral (clonidine) vasoconstrictor (alpha 2 agonist) - topical can work within 30mins but has s/E & can cause rebound flushing when stopped same with PO that is used to lower BP too but has S/E 
• even topical can cause systemic hypotension etc 

Brimodine gel apply OD til erythema gone 
Clonidine 50mcg BD for 2 weeks, increase slowly under supervision (ideally derm) 

Other unlicensed drugs - SSRI (fluoxetine), beta blocker (carvedilol, propanolol), mirtazipine 

Laser treatment via derm is often best permanent solution 

Only acne rosacea responds to topical ABx

Recurrent UTI RW

Recurrent = >2 episodes in 6 months, >3 episodes in 12 months 
Send MSU’s and check cultures 

3 steps 
• Self care and hygeine (wipe front to back, avoid douching or post coital urination)
• Vaginal oestrogen if PM
• Cranberry products like D-mannose or Hyprex 1g BD can kill E Coli but have limited evidence 
• Consider prophylactic ABx 
   - single double dose ( TMP 200mg or nitro 100mg) with clear trigger (usually sex/postcoital)
   - nightly TMP 100mg or nitro 50mg, review in 3-6 months, should look to withdraw after 6mo (alt = cefalexin/Amox)
   - Ensure continues to seek rv for acute sx 

Any recurrent UTI >60 non urgent urology referral to consider flex cysto, USS KUB etc 

Remember haematuria 2WW (>45 vis, >60 non vis)

Consider if recurrent urinary sx with negative MSUs - OAB/bladder pain syndrome (IC)

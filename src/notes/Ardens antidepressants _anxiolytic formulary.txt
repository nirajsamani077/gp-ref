SSRI 
- Sertraline 50mg tablets - 28 tablet - Take ONE tablet daily for anxiety/depression (max 200mg OD, do not prescribe 150mg/200mg tabs v expensive)
- Citalopram 10-20mg tablets - 28 tablet - Take ONE tablet ONCE a day (Start on 20mg with max of 40mg, elderly start on 10mg with max 20mg risk of QTC, also comes in drops)
- Escitalopram 5-10mg tablets - 28 tablet - ONE tablet to be taken ONCE daily (start on 10mg with max 20mg, max 10mg if liver dysfunction elderly start on 5mg, max 10mg) - consider ECG first for citalopram or escit 
- Fluoxetine 20mg capsules - 30 capsule - Take ONE capsule in the morning (max 60mg) - 40mg max in elderly
- Paroxetine 20mg tablets - 30 tablet - take ONE in the morning with food (max 40mg)

Consider gastroprotection if elderly + NSAID or aspirin, consider pregnancy risk vs benefit 

SNRI  - check BP first and monitor every rv  
- Duloxetine 60mg gastro-resistant capsules - 28 capsule - Take ONE capsule ONCE daily ( max 120mg although specialist advised)
- Venlafaxine
  1. IR = Venlafaxine 37.5mg tablets - 56 tablet - Take ONE tablet TWICE daily (max 75mg bd)
  2. MR = Venlafaxine 75mg modified-release tablets - 30 tablet - Take ONE tablet ONCE daily (max 375mg in 2 divided doses )

TCA usually will be secondary care initiated
- Imipramine 10mg tablets - 28 tablet - Take ONE tablet ONCE daily for elderly  (Imipramine 25mg tablets - 56 tablet - Take ONE tablet TWICE daily otherwise up to TDS)
- Lofepramine 70mg tablets - 56 tablet - Take ONE tablet TWICE daily (up to tds)

Other 
- Mirtazapine 15mg tablets - 28 tablet - Take ONE tablet every night (lower dose 7.5mg more sleep help, max dose 45mg on)
- Secondary care starts = Vortioxetine, Trazodone, Moclobemide, Reboxetine

Anxiolytic (avoid if possinle)

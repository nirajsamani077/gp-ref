Skinfold rash (intertrigo)

Top causes 
• Fungal (Candida or tinea)
• Seb derm 
• Irritant dermatitis (eg nappy rash)
• Flexural ezcema/psoriasis 
• Eryrthasma
• Acanthosis 

5 differentiators 
• Itchy? - ezcema 
• Symmetrical? - Seb derm 
• Satellite lesions? - fungal 
• In baby? - 
• Fluoresce under Woods light? - fungal 

Consider skin scrapings 
Daktacort a good option to cover all bases 
Fungal groin (tinea cruris) often co-exists with athletes foot (tinea pedis)
If resistant consider flexural psoriasis 
Recurrent boils consider hydradenitis (oral abx)

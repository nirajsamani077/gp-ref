Pyoderma gangrenosum

Neutrophilic dermatosis causing rare rapidly growing painful skin ulceration, usually lower limb, often starting as red bump and breaking down skin into large deep ulcer with undermined purple violaceous border +/- systemic upset 

Idiopathic (50%)
IBD
PBC
RA/SLA/GPA
Haem malignancy 

Oral steroids +/- systemic immunotherapy (ciclosporin, infliximab)

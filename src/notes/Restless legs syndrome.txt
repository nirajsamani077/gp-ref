Restless legs syndrome 

Uncontrollable urge to move legs (akathisia) often at night, crawling/throbbing paraesthesia, periodic limb movements in sleep 

Associated with FHx, IDA (check ferritin), DM, CKD (uraemia), pregnancy 

Stretching/massage of limbs 
Treat iron deficiency 
Dopamine agonists (pramipexole/ropinirole)
BDZ/gabapentin

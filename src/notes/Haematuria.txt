Haematuria 

Top causes 
• UTI (or STI urethritis) stone or tumour (bladder kidney prostate)
• Vigorous exercises/trauma/PV/PR/ discoloured urine from beetroots/nitro) - spurious 
• Non malignant - bladder (interstitial cystitis), kidney (pckd, gn), prostate (Bph)
• Bleeding diathesis (blood thinners, haemophilia /low plts)
• Rare in UK (schisto, IE)

5 ready reckoners
• Visible/frank?
• Dysuria? 
• Discharge?
• Poor stream (LUTS)?
• Loin pain?

History 
• Visible (frank) = worrying
• Painful/dysuria/loin pain (UTI/stone), painless = worrying for tumour (although can cause renal colic from obstructed blood clot) 
• Other LUTS (fx ux stream emptying dribbling) 
• Testicular lumps, penile issues, urethral d/c (STI) 
• Fever, abdo pain, nausea/vomiting, constitutional (weight night sweats) 
•Blood thinners, drugs (ketamine abuse causes ulcerative cystitis), kidney issues, travel, FHx cancer 

Examine 
• Urine dip, abdo exam, vital signs, testicular PR 

Investigate 
• All = urine dip/MSU/ACR, most = bloods (FBC, UE, PSA, maybe clotting) 
• USS / CT KUB
• Refer for cystoscopy or renal (consider urine cytology ?malignant cells) 

Management
• Treat as UTI if doesn’t settle refer? 
• 2WW

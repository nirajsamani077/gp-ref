UGIB (Upper GI bleed) RW

Serious & common - refer straight to ED
10% mortality 

Normal BP & Hb are not good indicators acutely (raised HR & urea better) - refer on a good Hx 

Causes - Variceal vs PUD/gastritis, Mallory Weiss 
• NSAIDs, antiplatelets, DOACs, SSRIs contribute 

Post hospitalisation/treatment 
• Ensure H Pylori tested & eradicated (may have false neg during Bx in acute bleed so retest 1mo off PPI for 2w though consider famotidine)
• PPIs continue, avoid trigger drugs unless essential then with PPI cover (consider COX-2 inhibitors but have CV risk).

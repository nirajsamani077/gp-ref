Amenorrhoea 

Primary - no period by 15 with SSC/13 without SSC (use Tanner staging) 
• Turner’s (gonadal dysgenesis) - most common
• Testicular feminisation 
• CAH
• Genital tract congenital abnormalities 
• Imperforate hymen 
• Hypothalamic (e.g. anorexia)

Secondary - no period 6 months or 12 months if previous oligomenorrhoea 
• PCOS
• POF
• Prolactin 
• Thyroid
• Sheehans syndrome 
• Hypothalamic (e.g. stress, exercise) 
• Asherman’s (intrauterine adhesions)

Investigations 
- rule out pregnancy
- bloods including TFT, coeliac, prolactin, free androgen index, oestrodial, FSH/LH (low in hypothalamic, raised in POF or gonadal problem)

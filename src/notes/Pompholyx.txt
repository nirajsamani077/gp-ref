Pompholyx

Ezcema on palms/soles associated with humidity/sweating that is intensely itchy and forms blisters which crack and dry out 

Most common irritant contact dermatitis 
Itching —> blisters —> burst —> redness/peeling/cracks 

Rx - as per ezcema
Avoid irritant, gloves 
Emollient + soap sub - wet soak with potassium permanganate for acute flare
Topical steroid +/ ABx

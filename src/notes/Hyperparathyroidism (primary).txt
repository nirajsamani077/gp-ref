Hyperparathyroidism (primary) 

Primary HPT usually adenoma or hyperplasia 
High calcium with bones, stones, abdo groans and psychic moans + low phosphate, PTH may be normal
Associated with MEN
Pepperpot skull, osteopenia, erosion of terminal phalangeal tufts (acral osteolysis) 2-3rd on XR

Conservative if calcium <2.85, >50 & no end organ 
Surgery with total parathyroidectomy otherwise 
If unfit then cinacalcet, mimics action of calcium on receptor, reduces PTH and calcium by feedback

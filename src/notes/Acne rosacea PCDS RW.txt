Acne rosacea PCDS RW

No comedones (unlike vulgaris), usually in fair skinned 30-50yo with erythema telangectasia flushing 

4 features
• Inflammatory lesions (erythematous papules, pustules, nodules) 
• Vascular lesions (erythematous telangectasia)
• Blepharitis & eye symptoms (ocular rosacea)
• Rhinophyma 

Steroids and ETOH make worse - DO NOT use 

All treatments 3 months 
For inflammatory 
• Mild = topical Soolantra (ivermectin) cream OD (other options Rozex (metronidazole) gel or Finacea (azelaic acid) cream or gel BD)
• Severe = oral tetracycline (like acne vulgaris, lymecycline 408mg Od, doxy 100mg Od), if pregnant erythro - if failed refer for isotretinoin (Roaccutane) 

For vascular /flushing 
• Mirvaso (Brimonidine) gel OD = a2 agonist could help reduce facial redness (used in glaucoma to reduce IOP), some but expensive & S/E ++ (flushing burning) so most refer for laser therapy (refer Rhinophyma for this also) - blacklisted in most of UK due to lack of evidence, safety concerns (hypotension/bradycardia esp when applying on damaged skin, rebound exacerbation on stopping) - so pretty much refer for laser & don’t use 

For ocular /blepharitis
• Lid hygeine warm compress, artificial tears, po tetracyclines (most effective), refer

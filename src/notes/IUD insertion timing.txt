IUD insertion timing

Cannot (UKMEC 4) insert if pregnant, uterine abnormality, pelvic infection or unexplained bleeding 

Should not (UKMEC 3) insert >48h or <4 weeks post partum due to risk of perforation (unchanged if CS)

Screen for STI prior to insertion if at risk & give prophylactic ABX prior to inserting if at risk but no test done

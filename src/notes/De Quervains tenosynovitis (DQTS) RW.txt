De Quervains tenosynovitis (DQTS) RW

Common, esp pregnancy and post partum 
Painful radial styloid - “gamers” thumb due to thickening and inflammation of tendon sheath with APL and EPB 
Thumb base /1st CMC Oa main DDx = positive “grind” test by pushing down on top of thumb causing pain 

Finkelsteins - wrist sideways over edge of table with thumb side and UD causes pain, move passively UD causes pain, move thumb passively flexing into palm causes pain - pain on the “selfie” position 

Thumb spica/splint, NSAID or single steroid inj
If not improved 6w refer OTS consider release of 1st dorsal extensor space

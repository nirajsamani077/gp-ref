NICE 5-16

Reliever SABA - Salamol 100micrograms/dose inhaler CFC free (Teva UK Ltd) - 200 dose - Inhale ONE to TWO doses as needed  (pMDI lower impact, cant be breath activated in kid) BUT IF CAN = Easyhaler Salbutamol sulfate 100micrograms/dose dry powder inhaler (Orion Pharma (UK) Ltd) - 200 dose - Inhale ONE to TWO doses as needed

1st line maintenance = low dose ICS (200mcg) 
- Clenil Modulite 100micrograms/dose inhaler (Chiesi Ltd) - 200 dose - Inhale TWO puffs TWICE a day (mDI not BA), alts= flixotide evohaler/soprobec  
- If can breath activate then - Easyhaler Budesonide 200micrograms/dose dry powder inhaler (Orion Pharma (UK) Ltd) - 200 dose - Inhale ONE dose TWICE a day (flixotide, pulmicort alts) is licensed above 6

2nd line = add LTRA or change to low dose LABA/ICS (DPI ideally)
- Montelukast 5mg chewable tablets sugar free - 28 tablet - ONE to be taken AT NIGHT
- Symbicort 100/6 Turbohaler (AstraZeneca UK Ltd) - 120 dose - Inhale TWO doses TWICE daily is only one licensed >6yo (rest seretide accuhaler, relvar etc are for >12yo), if cant BA then use pMDI Seetide evolhaler or flutiform  

3rd line = MART  (only licensed >12yo) or go up to LABA/ICS med dose 
- Symbicort 100/6 Turbohaler (AstraZeneca UK Ltd) - 120 dose - Maintenance, ONE puff TWICE a day. For immediate relief of symptoms, 1 puff as required, increased if necessary up to 6 puffs as required, max. 8 puffs per day. Up to 12 puffs daily can be used for a short time but medical assessment is recommended.   (can also go up to Med 200/6 MART)
- For LABA/ICS med dose use Seretide 100 Accuhaler (GlaxoSmithKline UK Ltd) - 60 dose - ONE blister to be inhaled TWICE daily 

4th line = High dose ICS (800mcg) and refer  

EMERGENENCY = Prednisolone 5mg soluble tablets - 12 tablet - Dissolve FOUR tablets (20mg) in a small amount of water once a day for 3 days (ie 20mg but can use 30mg)

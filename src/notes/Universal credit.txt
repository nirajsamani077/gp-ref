Universal credit 

Cost of living benefit replacing older benefits like Income Support/Jobseeker’s Allowance (JSA)/
/Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)/Working Tax Credit/Child Tax credit/Housing benefit 

The requirements to receive this are that the person and their partner 
• Live in the UK
• >18 + earn a low income or are out of work
• < £16,000 collectively in savings

Standard allowance + extra payments dep on circumstances, benefits cap 
Apply online 
First payment after 5wks, then monthly

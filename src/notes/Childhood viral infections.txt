Childhood viral infections 

Chickenpox - fever then itchy Mac —> pap —> vesicular rash

HFM - cocksackie a16, sore throat, fever, vesicles palms/soles/mouth 

Measles - fever/irritable/conj prodrome then macpap rash starting behind ears + Koplik spots 

Mumps - fever/myalgia prodrome then parotitis 

Rubella - pink macpap rash starting on face then fades after days + LN (subauric/postauric)

Fifth disease/slapped check/erythema infectiosum
- parvovirus b19, fever/malaise then slapped cheek appearance to arms 

Sixth disease/roseola infantum - high fever settled after 2d then macpap rash + red palate spots 

Scarlet fever - GAS infection, tonsillitis, fever, strawberry tongue, fine punctate erythema with circumoral pallor

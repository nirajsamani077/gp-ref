Young hip pain RW

Child 
• DDH, SUFE, Perthes, irritable hip, septic 

Young 
• Femoroacetabular impingement (FAI) & acetabular dysplasia common = congenital variant causing xs interaction between ball and socket in certain positions causing pinching anterior groin pain often worse when sitting or previous high activity - physio and IA steroids - may be OA precursor so more doing osteotomy 
• Tendinopathy (adductor)/ snapping hips 
• Osteonecrosis /AVN steroids ETOH sickle cell or stress #

Old
• OA, trochanteric bursitis, lumbar referred pain 
• Think red flags fracture malignancy or non MSK (hernias, gynae, lymph nodes, PVD) - remember night pain, unable to WB, trauma, inflammatory = red flags 

Have low threshold for XR hip AP in younger adults and if failed physio analgesia activity change or IA steroid consider OTS referral for MRI

Stress #
• More in runners high intensity, commonly tibial , can be fatigue or insufficiency due to osteoporosis , women extra risk due to “female athlete triad” = low BMD, low BMI, absent period 
• XR first line but low threshold for MRI as many missed

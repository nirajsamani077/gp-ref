Wilm’s tumour 

Unexplained abdo mass in children = possible Wilm’s tumour which needs urgent review

Renal malignancy <5yo causing abdo mass (often UL), flank pain, haematuria, systemic upset 
Lung mets most common (20%)

Rx: nephrectomy +chemo/radiotx 
80% cure rate

Genetic associations - Beckwith Wiedermann, WAGR syndrome, hemihypertrophy

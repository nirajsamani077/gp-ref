Asthma exac in kids 

Severe - unable to talk, HR >140 (if >5 = >125, >110 in adults), RR >40 (if >5 = >30, >25 in adults), using accessory muscles, sats <92%, if old enough PEFR <50% (silent chest is LT)

Pre-hospital or in hospital 
10 puffs salbutamol via spacer or close fitting mask in young (1 puff every 30-60s), deep long breath holding 10s + oral steroids (1-2mg/kg, >5y like adults 30-40mg, <5 is usually 20mg)

Systemic sclerosis /scleroderma / CREST syndrome RW 

Rare but important 

Scleroderma can be localised morphoea or true systemic sclerosis which can be limited (CREST) or diffuse (more organ involvement) 
• CREST = Calcinosis, Raynauds, Esophageal dysmotility, Sclerodaytl/scleroderma,Telangectasia 
• Abnormal nail fold capillaries on dermoscopy
• Antibodies - anticentromere (limited) & SCL70 (diffuse) - most are ANA positive 

If suspecting refer rheum urgently 
• DMARDs, biologics, systemic vasodilators 
• Stop smoking, warm baths, keep body hands feet warm & physio 
• New Raynauds in middle age with puffy hands or feet & fatigue consider systemic sclerosis! 

Comps of diffuse = organ fibrosis & vascular constriction (especially relevant for renal crisis)
• ILD, renal crisis, HF, dysphagia, arthritis .
• Very poor prognosis & no cure

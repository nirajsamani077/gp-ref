Combo ointment help 

Fucidin - topical Abx (2% fusidic acid), TDS/QDS (resistant /MRSA = mupirocin 2% (bactroban)
Fucidin H - topical ABx + steroid (HC) 
Fucibet - topical ABX + betnovate if bad

Daktarin - topical miconazole (2%)
Daktacort - topical miconazole + steroid (HC) 
Canesten & Canesten HC

Topical antiseptics (usually more appropriate than abx)  = hydrogen peroxide 1% cream, povodine-iodine 10%)

Prescribe all for max of 7d to prevent resistance 
Oral agent usually more suitable unless very localised

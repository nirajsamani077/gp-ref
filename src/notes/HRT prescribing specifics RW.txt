HRT prescribing specifics RW

https://bswtogether.org.uk/medicines/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2023/03/BSW-HRT-Treatment-Options-Mar-2023.pdf


For all check 
• Smoking, BMI, BP, VTE and breast lumps/CA
• Any bleeding and check smears UTD 

Oestrogen-only if TAH or Mirena IUS in date (5y) 
• Gel patches preferable to tabs which are cheaper but VTE/CV risk (eg Premarin 300/600/1250mcg)
• Lots of options with patches (start 50mcg /d) usually twice weekly 
  - Evorel 25/50/75/100 or Estraderm (evorel 50 is about 1-1.5mg oestrogen per day) 
  - Estradot has 37.5/Femseven more expensive 
• Gels and sprays less available 
  - Oestragel 80mg pump 2-4 pump (1.5 - 3mg oestrogen) per day 
  - Sandrena 0.5/1mg sachets one per day 
  - Lenzetto spray 1-3 sprays (each spray 1.5mg)/ d

For most = COMBINED 
• Sequential/cyclical progesterone if perimenopausal within 1y of LMP to match cycle (progesterone last 2w cycle to trigger bleed) as continuous might cause erratic bleeding, then swap to Continuous if >1y since LMP or >54 
• If needs/wants contraception (<54 sexually active with cycles - check LMP if >1y ago above 50 then don’t need if >2y ago below 50 then don’t need)….but if need then 
 - Oestogen patch/gel + Mirena IUS (best option) 
 - Sequental HRT + POP/implant/depot 
 - Usually COCP more risk with age but can be use
 - Can stop any hormonal contraception at 55
• Generally micronised progesterone (Utrogestan) safest option for risk VTE breast etc 

Sequential options
• Combined patch - Evorel Sequi (NET 2nd half of month 2 patches/wk) or FemSeven Sequi (LNG) both expensive with 50mcg oestrogen but good 
• Combined pill (VTE..) - Elleste Duet, Femoston (Dydro), Tridesta (MPA) probably better options than NET containing (Elleste duet, Clinorette, Novofem)
• Oestrogen only patch/gel + ideally micronised progesterone 2nd 2 weeks of cycle (Utrogestan 200mg, or Gepretix), MPA 10mg /NET 5mg less used (advise to use first 14 days calendar month easier to remember) 
• Oestrogen only + Mirena IUS 

Continuous options 
• Combined patch - Evorel Conti & Femseven Conti as above , expensive 
• Combined pill (VTE..) - Elleste Duet Conti, Femoston Conti, Indivina (MPA) or older NET ones (Kliovance , Kliofem, Elleste duet Conti)
• Oestrogen only + continuous micronised progesterone as above (Utrogestan 100mg cont)
• Oestrogen only + Mirena IUS 

Review 3mo and annually with all above checks (smoking BMI BP VTE breast ) 
• If sx increase to max dose or change form 
• If bleeding erratic >6mo peri then refer (can happen with new sequential or sequential to combined after 1y no periods, can change back to sequential for a bit) - true change ?PMB. 
• If sequential with heavy bleeding <6mo consider increasing progesterone dose/duration or changing to IUS (esp if progest Sx) 
• Can get irregular bleeding 3-6mo after any switch but refer if doesn’t settle 
• Can stop if risks outweigh benefits pt led, no limits but consider whether still having effect 

For other 
• PV oestrogen safe for local sx - Vagifem tab or Ovestin cream OD for 2w then twice weekly maintenance 
• Tibolone synthetic HRT 2.5mg OD (Livial) - if can’t take HRT may be less effective usually discuss menopause clinic 

Dose equivalent - oestrogen 1-2mg oral 
• 1mg oral = 25-50mcg patch = 1-2 pumps/spray = 0.5-1mg Lenzetto spray 
• 2mg oral = 75-100mcg patch = 3-4 pumps/spray = 1-1.5mg spray 
• For progestagens only oral Utrogestan or Gepretix licensed (200mg for 2w cyclical, 100mg continuous), MPA 10mg cyclical (5mg continuous with high dose oestrogen, 2.5mg continuous with lower dose oestrogen) or NET 5mg either 

Availability & switching 
• 2023 HRT prescription prepayment certificate - can have a year of HRT for only £19.30
• If one prep not available switch to similar alternative equivalent dose, warn may be short erratic bleeding 
• Only Mirena licensed as progestagenic contraception for endometrial protection in HRT
• Check BMS if off-licence

AED specifics 

Sodium valproate 
• 1st line for generalised tonic clonic (increases GABA) 
• teratogenic with NTD - all women of child bearing age should be enrolled into pregnancy prevention programme, use effective contraception, annual acknowledgement of risk form by specialist & annual review 
• GI upset, increased appetite/weight gain, alopecia, ataxia, tremor, hepatitis, pancreatitis, hyperammoneamjc encephalopathy 

GTC - valproate, lamotrigine, CBZ
Partial - CBZ, lamotrigine, keppra, valproate 
Absence - valproate or ethosux (not CBZ)
Myoclonic - valproate, lamotrigine, clonazepam (not CBZ)

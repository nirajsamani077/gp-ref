Traumatic knee pain RW

Ottowa knee rule to R/O fracture for XR
• >55 any patellar/fib head tenderness, unable to flex to 90, unable to WB at time or 4 steps now 

Mechanism critical 
• Meniscal = Twisting, anchored foot, pop, usually can WB, effusion usually overnight, stairs difficult - joint line tenderness & squat + duck walk test 
• ACL = Direct blow when WB, footballers common, as above often can’t WB and rapid effusion (haemarthrosis), Lachmans (lying flat, move tibia forward of femur with left hand securing thigh right hand on TT moving back and forward, if no fixed end point = ?tear), Drawer 
• PCL = posterior sag, dashboard hyperextension 
• MCL = valgus stress 

After acute injury reassess within 2w of RICE
Urgent OTS for any instabilty, if meniscal without locking or MCL not improving after 6w physio - MRI!
Surgery Vs rehab for ACL rupture controversial

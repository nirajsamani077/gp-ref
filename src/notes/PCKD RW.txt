PCKD RW

AD PCKD most common inherited renal disease (1/1000) and ESRF, multiple cysts destroy renal tissue and often ESRF by 50s but may vary

Renal - CKD with comps, HTN, anaemia, 2 HPT, metabolic bone, CVD, haematuria, stones, cyst pain /infections 

Extra-renal - other cysts (hepatic , panc), Berry aneurysms, aortic valve lesions 

Refer genetics (1 in 2 children may get it as AD)
• PKD1 on Chr16 - common severe progresses 
• PKD2 on Chr4 - rare less severe less progress 

Rx 
• Specialist, no cure, treat infections 
• CVD risk - salt (<6g), avoid smoking/NSAID
• BP control - strict <110/75 with ACE
• Statin if eGFR <60
• Tolvaptan - vasopressin/ADH antagonist which means more water lost from kidneys and less water in cysts, can dehydrate and high Na 
• Dialysis /Tx when ESRF

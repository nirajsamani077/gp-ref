Missed pill: POP

Usually 3 hour window for lateness for most apart from desogestrel (Cerazette) which is 12 hour window 

If late:
- take missed pill asap (if >1 then take just 1)
- take next pill at usual time (so may take 2 in one day) 
- 48h extra barrier precautions until normal pill taking re-established 
- continue with rest of pack

Vesicoureteric reflux (VUR)

Backflow of urine from bladder into ureter/kidney 
30% of children who have UTI, risk of renal scarring

Presentations
- Antenatally can be shown as hydronephrosis
- Recurrent childhood UTI 
- Chronic pyelonephritis/reflux nephropathy with renal scarring 

Diagnosis - micturating cystourethrogeam (MCU), DMSA to look for renal scarring 

Grades 1-5 based on degree of ureteric/renal dilation

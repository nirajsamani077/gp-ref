Bedbugs RW

Itchy macpap erythematous painless rash in clusters (on exposed skin) suggests bedbugs  
• Fast moving rapidly reproducing brown insects the size of apple pip, feed on blood, hide in bedding clothing textiles 
• Ask about infestation & professional checking of home (musty sweet smell, dark faecal spots, visible exoskeletons)

Can cause insomnia, psychological distress & secondary bacterial infections from scratching 

Management 
• Specialist professional eradication, washing textiles at 60degs or freeze -20degs for 2h, bag vacuum & seal bag dispose immediately, steam mattresses, if travelling decontaminate stuff before entering. 
• Don’t use insecticides or “bug bombs” as they are resistant and can be irritable 
• Rash usually self limiting 1-2w, treat any symptomatic topical HC, antihistamines, superadded bacterial infection

Standard deviation & confidence intervals 

Standard deviation 
- Average spread of values from the mean, only used in ND data 
- square root of variance 

- 1 SD = 68.3% of values  
- 2 DD = 95.4 % 
- 3 SD = 99.7 %  

Standed error of mean (SEM) = precision of mean 
- how close is the calucated mean to the true population mean 
- measure of precision, therefore larger sample size, smaller sem 
- SEM = SD/square root of (n) 

Confidence intervals 
- a 95% CI means that you are 95% confident that the true population mean is within this range 
- smaller CI, usually larger study 
- lower limit = mean - (1.96 x SEM) 
- upper limit = mean + (1.96 x SEM)

Vit D things 

Should take if 
• pregnant/breastfeeding (10ug daily)
• children 6mo-5yrs (unless formula >500ml/d or fortified)
• adults >65yrs 
• poor sun exposure (housebound)

Only test for deficiency if 
• known bone disease (may help sx or pre treatment with bisphosphonate etc)
• MSK symptoms that may attributed 

NB if osteoporosis should be on Ca/Vit D supp anyway so no need to test

Monitor Ca after starting vit D loading however as correction can unmask hyperparathyroidism (after 1mo)

<50 insuff - diet (eg vegan), sun, 800-2000IU maintenance 
<25 def - rapid correction, 300,000IU separate weekly or daily doses over 6–10 weeks, calcium check at 1 month 

Secondary causes or when to get specialist advice - Hypercalcaemia/stones risks, malabsorption, liver/renal disease, pregnancy 

Assessing calcium need - CGEM calculator, if inadequate <700mg/d or <1000g if osteoporosis give separate calcium supp

Glandular fever 

EBV/ infectious mononucleosis 
Triad - sore throat, fever, LN ++
Other - palatal petechiae, splenomegaly (50%, risk of splenic rupture), macpap rash with amoxicillin 

Bloods 
- Monospot test (heterophil antibody test) in 2nd week of illness 
- FBC shows lymphotocytosis, 50% lymphocytes, 10% atypical lymphocytes, in 2nd week of illness 
- hepatitis LFTs, raised ALT
- cold haemolytic anaemia 

Treatment 
- supportive, usually resolves within 4 weeks, in which time avoid contact sports

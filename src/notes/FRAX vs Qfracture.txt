FRAX vs Qfracture

Assess all women >65 or all men >75, younger if obvious RF (fragility #, steroids, parental hip #, falls, smoking/ETOH, underweight)

Both assess 10 year fracture risk 
FRAX is international data, Q is UK based
FRAX has less RFs but can incorporate BMD

Go straight to DEXA if 
- <40 with major RF (multiple fragility #, prednisone >7.5mg for >3 months) 
- before anti-oestrogen Tx like anastrozole

Go straight to bisphosphonate if >=75 with fragility fracture 

FRAX will highlight
- low risk (reassure, lifestyle)
- intermediate risk (DEXA)
- high risk (bisphosphonate)

Repeat FRAX after 2 years or when RF changes if original score was in intervention threshold

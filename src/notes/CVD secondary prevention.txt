CVD secondary prevention

Antiplatelet
• Post MI = DAPT 1y (usually if had PCI = prasugrel, if not had PCI = ticagrelor), then aspirin LT 
• Any other non heart (CVA, PVD) = clopidogrel LT (interacts with omeprazole or esomeprazole but other PPIs ok), if not tolerating aspirin + dipyramidole MR 200mg BD
• If also AF consider DOAC (initial triple Tx post MI)

Atorva 80mg (unless also CKD)

Post MI cocktail (rest)
• ACE/ARB, Bb, Spiro (MRA) if HFREF, may consider ICD
• Diet - Mediterranean, no omega 3, limit ETOH 
• Exercise - 20-30mins/d, until slightly breathless, cardiac rehab within 10d for d/c (reduces CV mortality by 25%!)
• Stop smoking, reduce stress 
• Sex - Resume after 1mo, viagra after 6mo
• Driving - Resume after 1mo medical, 1wk if PCI
• Flying - check Civil Aviation Authority

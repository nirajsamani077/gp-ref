Facial pain RW 

With nasal blockage /congestion 
• Rhinosinusitis (irrigation/douching & nasal steroids, if polyps and persistent try oral pred 10d) 
• Migraine (episodic, photophonobia, NV UL)
• Cluster (sudden UL red watery eye severe 8x/day, refer immediate neurology high flow O2 SC sumitriptan and consider neuroimaging) & other autonomic trigeminal cephalgias eg paroxysmal hemicrania)
• Midfacial segment pain (tension headache of face, mild tenderness cheeks forehead, try 6mo low dose amitryptilline)

Without nasal symptoms
• Tension headache (avoid triggers, lifestyle, stress, sleep, caffeine, acupuncture, amitryptilline) 
• TMJ dysfunction (jaw clicking, grinding, stress relaxation, splint, CBT, maxfax)

Chronic sinusitis is less common than we think so think about other causes esp TMJ and midfacial, consider dental check, and 4w initial trial of treatment

Prostatitis RW

Rare severe bacterial infection of prostate
• Fever LUTS pain, tender DRE, often quite acute 
• High rate of serious comps (retention, pyelo, epididymitis, abscess, sepsis) & 10% with chronic prostatitis 

Needs prolonged Abx (usually cipro 500mg Bd 14 days or ofloxacin - quinolones 1st line penetrate prostate well and for severe infections but MHRA risk of tendon rupture warn pt, 2nd line TMP 200mg BD 14d, alt levofloxacin or co-trimoxazlole) 
• RV at 48h as if not improving should admit 
• RV at 14d as often needs 4w total 

NSAIDs and quinolones together can lower seizure threshold so consider risk 

Sx of acute prostatitis can last several weeks 

Chronic prostatitis May need recurrent abx, tamsulosin which helps LUTS relax urethra and urology/pain clinic

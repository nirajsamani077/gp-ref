CT head trauma criteria 

Immediately if 
• GCS <13 or <15 within 2hrs of injury
• Suspected skull or basal skull #
• Focal neuro, seizure or >1 vomit 

Within 8hrs if 
• >65yrs old 
• Bleeding diathesis (including anticoagulation)
• Dangerous mechanism (eg fall >1m/5 stairs)
• Retrograde amnesia >30mins

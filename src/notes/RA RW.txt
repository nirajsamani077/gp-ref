RA RW

Consider if inflammatory peripheral arthritis (not DIP) with morning stiffness >30min and systemic symptoms (fatigue, weight loss etc)
• Tender swollen joints positive squeeze test 
• Refer urgent rheum + check inflamm markers RF CCP and XR hands and feet if symptomatic 
• Don’t start steroids in primary care (A&G May recommend oral or IM)

See DMARDs section, often MTX HCQ LFN or SSZ
If failed 2 = Biological 
NSAIDs with PPI and oral/IM pred for flares
Avoid live vaccines (ensure flu pneumococcal Hep B vaccines)

Very high Cv and osteoporosis risk , MH common 
Anti TNFs have very high SCC and BCC rate 

Average Gp sees 1 new case a year of RA

Sex development disorders

Androgen insensitivity - X linked recessive, end organ resistance to testosterone so genetically male but looks female (rudimentary vagina & testes, no uterus), raised testosterone/oestrogen/LH

5-a reductase deficiency - AR, unable to convert testosterone to active DHT, ambiguous genitalia, hypospadias, virilisation

Male/female pseudohermaphrotidism - either testes but external genitalia female, or ovaries but external genitalia of male/ambiguous

True hermaphroditism - both ovaries and testes

Diabetic neuropathy 

Glove and stockings length dependent 
Neuropathic pain meds 1st line (amitryptilline, pregabalin, gapapentin, duloxetine), should try others if one doesn’t work 
Tramadol as rescue therapy 
Topical capsaicin for local pain (post herpetic neuralgia) 
Pain clinic if resistant 

Autonomic
- Gastroparesis (incomplete gastric emptying) causes bloating, vomiting, erratic BMs. Prokinetics such as metoclopramide, domperdione, erythromycin 
- Chronic nocturnal diarrhoea 
- GORD from reduced LES pressure

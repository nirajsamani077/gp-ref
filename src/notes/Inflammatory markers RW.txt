Inflammatory markers RW

Avoid requesting both CRP & ESR - unless for suspected PMR/GCA (although possible for both to be normal but still have so a good story trumps in the negative bloods) - CRP most of the time is preferred to check as slightly better accuracy but if suspecting myeloma (>60 unexplained back bone pain # or weight loss etc) check ESR (as CRP is unhelpful) .

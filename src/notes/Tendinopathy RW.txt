Tendinopathy RW

Tennis/golfers elbow, Achilles, patellar 
Supraspinitis and trochanteric are more “bursitis”

Multiple factors but usually overuse (cycle of excess loading leading micro strain/tears which cause inflamm and heal dysfunctionally, degenerate, redamage, risk of rupture), sports and occupation important, intrinsic factors like weight age genetics 

Often localised pain with activity, tends to ease with continuing activity and much worse after - with swelling tenderness etc 

Treatments 
1. Eccentric loading exercises -controlled stretching of tendon under progressive load to lengthen and strengthen = THE BEST , opposite which is concentric eg bicep curls 
• Tennis elbow = wrist over edge table facing down, dumbell slowly lowering (flexing) to stretch tendon 
• Patellar tendon = slow squats for quads 
• Achilles = slow lowering of heel when front of foot on a step 
2. Stop doing the overuse issue - rest but not too much rest, avoid high impact , try slow exercises, reduce load!
3. Braces for tennis elbow (Masalo Cuff)
4. Topical NSAIDs (remember not inflammatory but overuse degenerative)
5. Other treatments - shockwave, injections, surgery equivocal benefit

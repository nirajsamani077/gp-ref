Seborrhoeic dermatitis

Inflammation from normal skin fungus (Malassezia furfur) causing dermatitis in sebum rich areas (scalp with dandruff, periorbital, periauricular, nasolabial fold) 
Association: OE, blepharitis 
Associated conditions: HIV, PD

Scalp 
1st line- OTC zinc (Head & Shoulders) or tar (Neutrogena T gel) shampoo 
2nd line - Ketoconazole shampoo 
Other - topical steroid, selenium 

Face/body 
Topical anti fungal 
Short use of topical steroid 

In young children is common in nappy, face, flexure
Cradle cap of scalp in newborns, red rash with coarse yellow scale - usually leave alone & resolves almost always by 8 months , coconut oil & dentinox  https://dentinox.co.uk/cradle-cap-shampoo/ 
If mild - baby shampoo, if severe - topical HC

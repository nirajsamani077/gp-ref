Joint hypermobility RW

Common cause of MSK (and many other) symptoms which can be localised, peripheral only or generalised which can be part of specific syndrome (Ethers danlos or Marfans)

Check Beighton score = max is 9

Often self management with lifestyle, physio, avoid overstretching, taping, bracing 

Ethers danlos (EDS)
• Rare but important CT disorder with 13 subtypes 
• Hyper mobility + other features (hyperextensible skin, bruising/striae, long fingers, cornea, dental crowding, hernias, prolapses, cardiac AR MVP)
• Refer rheum

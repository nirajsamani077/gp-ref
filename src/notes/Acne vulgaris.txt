Acne vulgaris 

Mild = open closed comedomes 
Mod = more papules pustules (inflammatory)
Severe = + nodules, cysts, scars (atrophic/ice pick or hypertrophic)
Acne Congolobate - rare severe trunk UL acne in men with nodules, sinuses (fulimnans = systemic)

DDx- rosacea, perioral dermatitis, folliculitis, keratosis pilaris (dry skin keratin hair follicle bumps harmless)

General advice 
Don’t overclean, use non-alkaline cleanser (face wash) (acneicide) BD (human skin slightly acidic 4-6.5, using alkali detergents change flora make acne more prone), avoid oil-based skin products 

Mild - Epiduo (BP + adalapene) ON or Duac (BP + clinda) ON 12w. Can also try tretinion + clinda (Ziana). Remember mono therapy with BP if can’t use retinoids (<12, pregnancy, BF). Can go up to BD if needed. BD can irritate skin & bleach stuff (can start alt day or wash off after 1h initially)

Mod - BD/adalepene(retinoid) + Po abx (lymecycline 408mg or doxy 100mg OD) 12w, alt TMP or erythro. Never oral and topical abx together. COCP can help (stop Dianette after 3/12 VTE risk +++)

Sev - Refer consider isotretinoin (roaccutane)

Consider maintenance Epiduo or mono if frequent relapse

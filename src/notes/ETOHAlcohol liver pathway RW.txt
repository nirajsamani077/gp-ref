ETOH/Alcohol liver pathway RW

If history of ETOH XS quantify 
• 1 unit = 1 shot spirit, half a pint beer, half a small glass of wine 
• Excess defined as 
   - Male >50 units / week or female >35 units/ wk
   - ETOH related liver disease (normal LFT no ro)

If history of XS as defined above 
• Offer Fibroscan and refer, if negative repeat 2y

If not reaching threshold check AUDIT-C if raised (significant if >=5 procede to full AUDIT)
• Check GGT (if >100 treat as high risk as above)
• ELF or Fibroscan if full AUDIT >19
• Refer ETOH services or brief intervention .

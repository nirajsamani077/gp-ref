Norethisterone (NET) for delaying period RW

Norethisterone 5mg TDS 3 days before anticipated period to postpone/delay period 
Does not act as contraception
Periods heavier afterwards 
Menstruation will occur 2-3 days after stopping 

NET metabolises info ethinyestradiol which 1mg equiv = 5mg oestrogen each, so is actually stronger than COCP so shouldn’t be used if high risk of VTE (eg overweight, smokers, aged over 35) — so use MPA (Provera) 10mg TDS/BD instead

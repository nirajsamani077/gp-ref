Adult constipation RW

If CIBH >60 = FIT & 2WW, consider meds (opiates) although most idiopathic, IBS or inadequate fluid/fibre 

Check for faecal loading/impaction & disimpact

Otherwise
• Fluid, fibre (although no good evidence)
• Semi-squatting posture (raise feet with stool) 
• Laxatives (bulk forming, osmotic, stimulant)
   - Fybogel (Isphagula husk) & hydration first 
   - Movicol /lactulose stool softener if stool hard 
   - Stimulant senna or bisacodyl if can’t pass 

Laxative doses 
• Fybogel 1 sachet BD 
• Movicol 1 sachet BD
• Lactulose 15ml BD 
• Senna 15mg ON 
• Docusate (softener & stimulant) 100mg TDS

Newer laxatives for chronic constipation 
• Prucalopride - 5HT4 agonist which stimulates bowel motility, 2mg Od, only used if failed 2 laxatives 6mo & considering invasive Rx (expensive £60 / month)
• Naxogel - for opiate-induced constipation, limited role, expensive as above £60 / month 

Treat comps 
• Chronic fissure - topical GTN ointment 
• Proctalgia - amitryptilline/gabapentinoids 
• Levator ani syndrome - similar chronic rectal pain (LA, massage, biofeedback etc)
• Rectal prolapse - diet, biofeedback, surgery

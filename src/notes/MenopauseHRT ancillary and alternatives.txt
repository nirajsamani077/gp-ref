Menopause/HRT ancillary and alternatives RW

• Signpost to Menopause matters website and decision flow chart 
• No FSH if >45, only x2 if <45 (Prem) or <40 (POF), check 1 month apart >30 (FSH also used when deciding to stop contraception when amenorrhoeic >50 if FSH >30 then can stop after 1y)
• Refer any POF or premature not improving with HRT, or any contraindications (eg VTE breast Ca smoker) or uncertainties re Rx to menopause clinic 
• Osteoporosis wise HRT beneficial but not a treatment alone, age and low oestrogen of course RFs, don’t need to FRAX based on menopause alone, optimise lifestyle, much more important bone benefit in prem or POF where used as treatment 
• Cognition - whilst HRT can help brain fog it doesn’t help with actual cognitive impairment, no conclusive evidence that HRT causes or prevents dementia, again prem POF higher risk 
• Big study did not recommend HRT to be used for chronic disease prevention in absence of menopausal sx (although some benefits to osteoporosis, CVD, dementia with risks breast CA, VTE, dementia , stroke) 

• Lifestyle - for flushes exercise but not late in day, light anti-flush clothes (eg Become), avoid etoh spicy foods caffeine, bed fans and cooling pillows, sleep hygeine, OH at work - check Menopause Matters and Rock My Menopause websites 

Alternatives for vasomotor only - pharm Vs non pharm which does have less evidence. 
• Supps and herbal remedies like Soy, Black cohosh, St John’s wort, Vitamin E can help with flushing but evidence not great & poorly regulated, interact with other medicines, if going to use check MHRA herbal remedies stamp. 
• CBT and hyponosis can definitely benefit. 
• Clonidine (bp med relaxes blood vessels, 25mg BD initially then increase, only licensed treatment for flushing but can have lots of S/E dry mouth dizziness constipation sleep & if taking must gradually reduce due to risk of rebound HTN)
• SSRI /SNRI can be used (but avoid paroxetine and fluoxetine if on tamoxifen, citalopram better option), gabapentinoids could help but not licensed 
• If Hx of Breast Ca or other hormone sensitive CA then these are the options, even topical oestrogen C/I, specialist led & FSH levels can be more helpful (although would need to stop tamoxifen 6w before so specialist decision ), definitely no paroxetine or fluoxetine if on tamoxifen 

Tibolone (Livial) 2.5mg OD is synthetic steroid with oestrogenic progestagenic and androgenic activity (making it better for libido like testosterone) but low quality studies show less effective but perhaps less risks (breast ca ) 

“Bioidentical” hormones available privately unregulated more “natural” but really into unknown , nowadays most HRT has bioidentical oestrogen and micronised progesterone which is bioidentical plant based

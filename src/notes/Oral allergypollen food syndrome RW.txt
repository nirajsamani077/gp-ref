Oral allergy/pollen food syndrome RW

History of hayfever (seasonal allergic rhinitis) + rapid onset oropharyngeal symptoms to raw plant based food (IgE mediated cross sensitivity reaction to pollens which are similar proteins to these foods) 

If not raw then isn’t OAS 
Any nuts, banana, kiwi, melon isn’t OAS 
Any anaphylaxis features isn’t OAS

Consider checking IgE RAST to birch and grass pollen if no hx of hayfever 

Avoid or try cooking raw 

BSACI leaflet on pollen food syndrome

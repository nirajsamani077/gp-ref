Placebo effect 

Inert substance with no inherent pharmacological activity, active if it has a different activity, nocebo if it has prominent side effects, can also be procedural. 

Injections more effect than oral
Capsules more effect than tabs 
Senior person more effect than junior 
Mild illness more effect than severe 
Placebo response typically abrupt & early + tends fade (placebo sag is when less effect with repeated use)

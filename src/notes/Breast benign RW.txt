Breast benign RW

Most breast sx non cancerous 
• Fibroadenoma, fibrocystic change (lumpy breasts), breast cysts common in premenopausal 
• Mastalgia (often cyclical = hormonal) without lump usually benign (only refer if persistent 3mo despite supportive bra, evening primrose oil , nsaid), if non cyclical consider non breast cause 
• Nipple discharge common physiological (if galactorrhoea non-pregnant check prolactin TSH and check meds eg COCP antiemetics), pathological is UL bloody /pussy or associated mass (duct papilloma, duct ectasia, ductal CA) refer 2WW
• Mastitis breastfeeding usually staph with fever, pump and frequent feedings, warm compress and bath to help open up ducts, if not improving after 24h 10d fluclox, if ?abscess refer urgent (for non lactational mastitis often related to duct ectasia centrally or AI diseases peripherally - above but coamox instead as tends to be less staph)
• Fat necrosis - benign adipose inflammation sometimes trauma with mass erythema skin change, usually 2WW as bx confirmative 

Must 2WW breast 
• Any unexplained breast /skin/axillary lump >30 (non urgent if persistent <30, persistent pain) 
• Any UL nipple discharge/changes >50 (non urgent if persistent <50 or nipple ezcema)

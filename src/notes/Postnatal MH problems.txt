Postnatal MH problems 

Baby blues - majority, within 1wk, tearful/irritable, reassure/support with health visitor 

Post natal depression (PND) - 10%, most within 1mo peaking at 3mo, as for baby blues but may need CBT and/or SSRI (sertraline & paroxetine best for breastfeeding), Edinburgh scale (10qs, max score 30, last week, self harm q)

Post-partum psychosis - 0.1%, 2wks post birth, severe + psychosis, safeguarding on baby, admit to baby/mother unit, high risk of recurrence in next pregnancy (50%)

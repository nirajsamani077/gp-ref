Insurance reports 

Points to know 
• GP charge a free for reports (standard GP report by ABI and BMA websites usually used)
• needs written consent from patient, and pt has right to see report before it’s sent 
• only send relevant info, don’t send full copy of notes or photocopies/print outs 
• do not comply with pts request to leave out relevant info on report, if they don’t consent to this then inform insurance company that you can’t complete it 
• if insurance company querying about pt having certain condition, doing an NHS referral to specialist to clarify is not appropriate
• return within 20d
• in special circumstances May need access to records of deceased pt

Acupuncture RW

Safe and effective based on sound neurophysiology principles 
• Recommended for tension headache or migraine prophylaxis 
• Can be useful in chronic pain 
• Evidence base not as strong as some other Rx

Different types 
• Traditional - small needles into skin muscle left in or stimulated with small electric impulse up to 30mins, up to 8 sessions 1-2x/week 
• Trigger point - on myofasical release points 
• Dry needling - on tender points

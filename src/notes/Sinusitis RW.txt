Sinusitis RW

Acute sinusitis 
• Usually virus, self limiting 2-3w without ABx which will make little/no difference
• Blocked discharging nose, facial pain on bending, anosmia, URTI, fever 
• If sinusitis >10d can use high dose intranasal steroid (mometasone 2 sprays/nostril BD 14d, alt beconase nasonex clarinaze nasocort) off licence 
• Simple analgesia fluids saline nasal irrigation /salt water sniffs (douching) and steam inh (less evidence)
• Bacterial treatment if >10d fever purulent discharge severe pain or deterioration after milder phase - offer narrow spectrum Pen V 500mg QDS 5d (or coamox if severe /IC) - pen allergy doxy clary erythro - immediate or back up script 

Chronic sinusitis
• Common with blockage discharge pressure and anosmia, affects 10% of population at least 
• Red flags = UL, epistaxis, eye sx /neuro 
• Check with nasal speculum for obvious septal deviation or polyps 
• Many have nasal polyps associated 
• Main treatment nasal steroids and nasal irrigation (clean pot 1 pint cooled boiling water 1 tsp salt 1/2 tsp baking soda and a neti pot or medical syringe to sniff or squirt into nose and spit out every day (steam inh not that great but can be advised)
• Nasal steroids as above - usually head between knees position (may even use short course of oral pred or 12w azithro in severe cases with polyps )
• Refer ENT if poor response to treatment after 1mo (or 2WW if red flags) - nasendoscopy and sinus surgery 
• Samters triad = asthma aspirin sensitivity and polyps , benefit from montelukast

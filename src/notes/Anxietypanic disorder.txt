Anxiety/panic disorder 

Step 1 watch wait educate 
Step 2 low intensity psychological (self help group)
Step 3 high intensity psychological (CBT) or drug
Step 4 specialist input 

SSRI sertraline 1st line 
If fails - alternative SSRI or SNRI (venlafaxine, duloxetine) 
SSRI/SNRI failed - consider pregabalin 
For panic disorder - TCA (imipramine/clomipramine) if SSRI failed after 12 weeks

Erectile dysfunction (ED)

Organic - gradual onset, normal libido, no morning erections
Psychogenic - sudden, reduced libido, morning erections, life/psych problems, prev premature ejaculation

Use SHIM score for questionnaire 
https://www.baus.org.uk/_userfiles/pages/files/Patients/Leaflets/SHIM.pdf

Causes (organic) - CVD, endocrine (DM, hypogonadism), ETOH/drugs (Bb, SSRI), prostate, pelvic surgery/spinal (rare) 

CVS exam, BP, BMI - QRISK + cardiac risk stratify for safety of sexual activity (low, int, high with unstable CVD = refer for cardiology assessment) 

Check testicles for lumps, hypogonadism + penis
DRE for prostate + PSA if any LUTS, Hx of CA, >50 - refer to urology if abnormality or young person who always struggled with erection 

Bloods - Lipids, HbA1C, morning free testosterone (if low/borderline repeat with FSH, LH, prolactin - if abnormal refer to endocrinology) 

Rx - sildenafil, vacuum device

Infantile haemangioma RW

Bright red raised rubbery bump that rapidly grow first 3mo of life - most don’t need surgical treatment (usually oral propanolol from 6mo - 1y)

Usually going to refer although many don’t need to if following usual timeline but refer if uncertainty, not following timeline, atypical location (face breast sacral), large >4cm, multiple, ulceration, functional impairment or risk of underlying syndrome (PHACE, LUMBAR)
• Can use Infantile Haemangioma Referral Score IHRS score to assist 

Typical timeline 
• Birth usually not present then first few weeks appears and rapid growth weeks 5-8 of life 
• Growth usually full at 6mo and then involution starts around 1yo .
• Fully resolved by 4yo, 70% residual skin change

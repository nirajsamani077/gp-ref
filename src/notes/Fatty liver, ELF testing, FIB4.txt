Fatty liver, ELF testing, FIB4

USS confirms fatty liver, rest of liver screen NAD (use EMIS fatty liver template) 

If no XS ETOH - calc FIB4 (advanced fibrosis risk) 
• <1.3 = low risk, manage NAFLD in GP with annual LFT/UE/HBA1C/lipids/BP and treat with statin/diabetes/weight loss 10%
• 1.3-3.25 = check ELF and as below 
• >3.25 = high risk so refer 

If XS ETOH (>14 units) - check ELF test first (separate gold top) as FIB4 inaccurate
• >9.8 high risk so refer to Gastro 
• <9.8 manage in GP, ETOH reduction

Sarcoidosis RW

Rare 1/10000 per year diagnosed 

Multisystem inflammatory condition (non caseating granulomas on histology but no other unique Sx)
• 50% incidental on CXR (BHL)
• Lung (BHL, upper lobe fibrosis) - SOBOE, cough 
• Fatigue, malaise, fever, WL, raised CRP, raised Ca
• Skin (erythema nodosum, lupus pernio dusky nodules, other macpap lesions)
• Other (Deranged LFT/HSM, LN, uveitis, renal/neuro/cardiac sarcoid) 

If suspecting check
• Bloods - FBC, U&E, CRP/ESR, LFT/bone (calcium), Igs (polyclonal), don’t do serum ACE (not diagnostic, better for monitoring) 
• CXR/ HRCT (BHL, UL fibrosis)
• Specialist do spiro, echo, eye check, tissue Bx 
   - Many don’t need Rx but may monitor 
   - If Rx steroids, biologicals 
   - Good prognosis 50% spontaneous regression, especially if just Lofgrens syndrome (triad BHL, arthritis, erythema nodosum)

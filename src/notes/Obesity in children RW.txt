Obesity in children RW

BMI centile on RCPCH/WHO chart 
• >85th centile = overweight 
• >95th centile = obese 
• >99th centile = severely obese 
Can use weight to height ratio if >5yo (>0.5) “keep your weight less than half of your height” 

Causes 
• Almost all due to lifestyle/deprivation 
• Secondary - Hypothyroidism, DM, Cushings, GH deficiency, Downs, Prader-Willi (check HbA1C, lipids, TFT at least) 

Risks - SUFE, tibial bowing (Blounts), OSA, IIH, CVD, bullying and psychological 

Management - multicomponent intervention 
• Get family involved, exercise mainstay 1h a day with cutting out junk in diet, engage school 
• Orlistat only in secondary care if >12yo
• Bariatric surgery only exceptional circumstances .

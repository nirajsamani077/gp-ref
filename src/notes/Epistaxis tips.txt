Epistaxis tips 

Common causes
- nose picking/blowing
- trauma/FBs
- cocaine 
- bleeding disorders (ITP, haemophilia, leukaemia)
- GPA (Wegeners)
- HHT
- juvenile angiofibroma

First aid 
- sit forward, mouth open, spit out blood
- pinch soft part of nose for 20 mins
- call 999 if not improving for cautery or packing, especially if <2 (haem cause) or morbid 

Chronic
- self care, stop picking/blowing, hot drinks, etoh
- Naseptin topical cream (chlorhexidine + neomycin) to reduce crusting & infection but cannot use if peanut or soy allergy as uses this in it

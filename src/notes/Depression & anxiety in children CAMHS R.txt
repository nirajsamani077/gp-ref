Depression & anxiety in children CAMHS RW

RFs - Parental MH/conflict/substance, being looked after child in care or homeless/refugee, stressful events eg loss, bullying or abuse 

Corroborate info - school, parents, family & listen to the CHILD & enquire sensitively about abuse 
• Also consider sexuality, substance & schooling 

Anything other than very mild (watchful waiting 1mo) should be referred to CAMHS (FTB) to assess and give either or both of specific psychotherapy (CBT individualised, family/interpersonal therapy or psychodynamic therapy) or SSRI (fluoxetine 1st line) - don’t initiate in primary care 
• If refractory CAMHS may consider changing to other SSRI or antipsychotic adjunct .

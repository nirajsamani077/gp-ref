Statistical terms 

Reliability - is something repeatable or consistent?

Validity - is something accurate for what it’s measuring?
• Internal validity (precision) - confidence we can place on cause-effect relationship in study (ie other confounders/variables not interfering), threats including reliability of instruments, sampling, loss of participants, history, maturation, sensitisation/instrument learning 
• External validity (generalisation)- are conclusions generalisable both in place & time, threats including representativeness, artificial research setting, multiple treatments 
• Other types - face value, content value, concurrent/predictive validity, construct validity 

Bayesian probability - constant adjustment of initial beliefs/hypothesis

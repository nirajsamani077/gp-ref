Contact dermatitis RW

Consider in people with high risk jobs (cleaners, cooks, hairdressers) with ezcema in unusual sites 
• Irritant contact dermatitis (80%) - irritant such as soap water detergents damage skin quicker than can be repaired 
• Allergic contact dermatitis (20%) - delayed hypersensitivity reaction 2-3d after exposure to allergen been previously sensitised too (nickel latex poison ivy)

Refer all to derm for patch testing = gold standard test with battery of common chemical allergens applied to skin under occlusion (3 visits to hospital over 1 week) - positive for allergic, negative for irritant 

Management  
• Avoid triggers, wear gloves, discuss with OH
• Emollients and potent steroid (or protopic 2nd line) for flares 4-6w max 
• Derm may give pred, PTX, immunosuppression .

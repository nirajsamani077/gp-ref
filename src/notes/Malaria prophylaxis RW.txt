Malaria prophylaxis RW

DEET based insect repellent (20-50%) can repel most mosquitos after 2mo (but dilutes sunscreen so apply sunscreen first) - for all, esp child 

Adult options
• Atovaqone + proguanil (malarone) - 1d before, 1wk after, GI upset 
• Doxycycline - 1d before, 1mo after,photosensitive
• Chloroquine - 1d before, 1mo after, weekly, epilepsy C/I (causes headache) 
• Mefloquine - 2wks before, 1mo after, weekly, epilepsy (causes neuropsych)

If pregnant - avoid travel, chloroquine safest
If child - doxycycline only if over 12

If in doubt use Travel Health Pro - country information & UK Gov websites .

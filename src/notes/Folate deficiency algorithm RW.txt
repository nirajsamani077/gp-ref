Folate deficiency algorithm RW

Rarely isolated, usually with B12 def so check & replace B12 first if deficient in both (risk SACCD)

Comes from healthy diet sources (greens, legumes, milk - cows >>goat, fortified cereals)
• Diet deficiency usually cause 
• Malabsorption (Crohns, Coeliac, Chronic panc)
• ETOH/pregnancy/BF/anaemias eg SCD all increase requirements of folate so deficiency 
• Meds - MTX, TMP & AEDs 

Px - Usually on bloods (reflects recent intake so may hide true deficiency - only way to truly check is checking red cell folate to see 3mo reflection rarely done)
• Mouth ulcers 
• Megaloblastic macrocytic anaemia (rarely pancytopenia) 
• Memory/cognitive impairment 
• Risk of NTD in pregnancy 

Treat - always check & replace B12 first, otherwise folic acid 5mg for 4mo then recheck 

In WCBA /early pregnancy 400mcg folic acid til 12w for all to prevent NTD, 5mg dose if higher risk 
• FHx NTD, epilepsy on AED, BMI >30, DM, Coeliac 
• Hx of thalassemia or sickle cell = 5mg whole preg  & need to be under haem monitoring risk anaemia & will have Hbopathy screen as part of booking .

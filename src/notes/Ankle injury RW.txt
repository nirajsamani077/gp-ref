Ankle injury RW

Ottowa ankle rules - fracture Vs sprain ATFL or calcaneofibular
• Pain in malleolar/midfoot zone + bony tenderness or unable to WB at time and now 

If not referred for XR
• RICE, ice 20mins 2 hourly at least 2 days or more 
• Crutches and early WB as pain able, gentle exercises 
• Moonboot may help keep foot in neutral 
• Usually ligs heal within 12w

Allergic rhinitis/hayfever RW

Rhinitis can be allergic of non allergic, allergic is 
• Intermittent or persistent (over half the time)
• Mild or severe (affecting QOL/function) 

Causes runny nose, blocked nose, itching and sneezing 

Allergen- housedustmite (HDM), grass/tree pollen 

DDx
• Nose issues - polyps, septal dev, adenoids, FB
• Drugs - Aspirin, NSAIDs, COCP, sprays, cocaine
• Endocrine - pregnancy, thyroid 
• Immune - Churg Strauss, wegeners 

Management 
• Nasal saline rinses (clears allergens, reduces inflammation, cooled boiled water with tea spoon canning (not table) salt and baking soda (bicarb), use syringe to squirt solution each side of nose to back when bent over sink 1-2x/d)

If need treatment either/or 
• Non sedating antihistamine OD BD up to QDS slowly 4 weeks spacing or..
• Nasal steroid spray (mometasone eg Nasonex/Clarinaze & fluticasone Avamys) 2 sprays each nostril OD (can initially do 2 sprays BD if severe or polyp and taper down) - blow nose first, head look down, same hand same nostril, gently slowly breathe in nare and press (fast sniff sends too far back and end up swallowing), slowly breathe out through mouth - need to use daily to be effective 

If failed more advanced options
• Nasal antihistamine (Dymista = azelastine/ fluticasone) or nasal sodium cromoglicate (mast cell stabiliser) - intranasal options preferred to oral as 3rd line, more cost effective to use each individually than combined, also useful if eye symptoms 
• Nasal ipratropium for old mans drippy/watery nose (anticholinergic though)
• Montelukast (LTRA) if also asthmatic/catarrh 
• Oral pred 30-40mg (0.5mg/kg) 5d acute relief (last resort), only if severe or intractable 
• Refer ENT for sinus surgery, nasal phototherapy/laser or sublingual immunotherapy (Grazax for Timothy grass pollen rhinitis)

In pregnancy 
• Pregnancy induced rhinitis is self limiting 
• If pre-existing needing Rx nasal saline irrigation and nasal steroids (fluticasone Avamys) safest, anti  histamines fine but not chlorphenamine if BF

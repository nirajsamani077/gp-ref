Breast CA screening RW 

50-70 3 yearly mammogram  (patients can self-refer above age (unlike cervical screening)) 

Refer familial breast CA for younger screening if 1st/2nd deg relative with 
• <40 at diagnosis 
• bilateral breast cancer with 1st primary <50
• male breast cancer 
• ovarian CA 
• Jewish ancestry 
• numbers - 2 first deg or 1+1 1st & 2nd deg or 3 2nd deg 

Trans registered as male won’t automatically be offered but should be referred directly to national screening programme (if taking high dose oestrogen clearly at higher risk).

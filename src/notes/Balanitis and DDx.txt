Balanitis and DDx

Very common - candidiasis (itch, white discharge), dermatitis contact/irritant (clear discharge), ezcema/psoriasis, bacterial/anaerobic

Zoons balanitis - rare, orange/red lesions with pinpoint red spots of glans of penis & foreskin in uncircumcised men 

Circinate balanitis - well demarcated red plaques with ragged white border, seen in Reiter’s 

Psoriasis - small red shiny patches on glans or shaft, not usually scaly, can be circumcised 

Seborrhoeic dermatitis - itchy well demarcated rash on penis 

Lichen sclerosis (balanitis xerotica obliterans) - tight white ring at top of foreskin with phimosis 

General treatment 
- gentle saline washes 
- 1% HC or stronger if zoons/lichen sclerosis 
- May need top clotrimazole/Po fluclox/metro
- May need circumcision if recurrent severe or lichen sclerosis

Guttate psoriasis vs. Pityriasis rosea 

Guttate psoriasis
- post strep infection 2-4wks prior 
- pink scaly tear drop papules on trunk/limbs acute onset over days 
- most resolve within 3mo, can use similar topical psoriasis agents (vit D analogue, steroid) if local 
- if >10% BSA refer urgently to derm for phototherapy 
- may need tonsillectomy if recurrent 

Pityriasis rosea 
- herald patch 1-2wk prior 
- multiple red oval papules, fine outer scale, may have fir/Christmas tree pattern along middle of back , not very itchy 
- self limiting 6wks

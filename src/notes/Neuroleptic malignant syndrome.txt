Neuroleptic malignant syndrome 

Rare condition, often after just starting antipsychotic or dopaminergic drugs 

Quadrad of 
- fever 
- rigidity
- delirium
- autonomic instability (tachycardia, HTN)

Bloods show raised CK ++ and AKI

Stop med, IVI, cooling & dantrolene, BDZs 

Serotonin syndrome is caused more by SSRI/ecstasy and is faster onset with hyperreflexia, clonus, dilated pupils (Rx cyproheptadine)

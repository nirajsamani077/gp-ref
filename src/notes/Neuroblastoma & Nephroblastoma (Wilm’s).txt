Neuroblastoma & Nephroblastoma (Wilm’s) RW

Neuroblastoma 
• Child <5 SNS tumour usually adrenal causing large abdominal mass & featues mineralocorticoid excess due to raised catecholamines (eg HTN) - mets very common 
• If suspected discuss with paeds consultant on-call same day - for admission & USS 
• Low grade may only need observation but high grade is very aggressive (<50% survival rate)

Nephroblastoma (Wilm’s)
• Child <3 renal tumour most common cause of abdo mass, refer same day urgent as above if abdo mass or visible haematuria .

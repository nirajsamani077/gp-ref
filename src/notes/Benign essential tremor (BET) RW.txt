Benign essential tremor (BET) RW

Common (1%) BL postural/action tremor (with voluntary muscle contraction)  with nil other neuro, possible FHx (AD) & present for at least 3y
• Usually upper limb, can be head (titubation), vocal or lower limb 
• Can be better with ETOH 
• Different to intention (goal directed movement eg finger nose test = Cerebellar) 
• Different to physiological tremor (is postural but due to thyroid, phaeo or drugs (SABA, caffeine, sympathomimetic, SSRI, lithium) 

TETRAS scale for affect on ADLs - https://www.bcm.edu/neurology-apps/pdf/poster_other_TETRAS.pdf

Rx
• Propanolol (40mg TDS) or Primodine (50mg/d), slowly increase both, both 50% discontinuation (fatigue, dizziness etc)
• Refer to neurological if not responding (Botox, deep brain stimulation, thalamotomy)
• Can use weighted pens/computer mice to reduce amplitude tremor

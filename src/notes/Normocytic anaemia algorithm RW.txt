Normocytic anaemia algorithm RW

Acute bleeding - may be obvious 
Iron deficiency or mixed picture 
Anaemia of chronic disease (ACD) - tired BM, CKD
Haemolysis 
Haematological malignancy /myeloma (esp if pan)

Check RETICS (+ other haematinics, LFT, TFT, +/- myeloma screen if >60) and FIT test if >60 with unexplained anaemia 
• Retics high >80 - if also BR high, check LDH hapto and refer haem, if BR normal then Ix bleeding 
• Retics normal <80 - all other causes inc myeloma

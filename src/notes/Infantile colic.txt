Infantile colic

Usually starts first few weeks of life & resolves 3-4mo, benign condition 

Bouts of crying and pulling legs up, arching back, usually worse in evening/night (generally happy during day)

No treatment (apart from antimuscarinics with significant S/E) have evidence 

Simple measures (holding, gentle motion, white noise) might help 

NICE don’t recommend Infacol (simeticone) or Colief drops although widely used

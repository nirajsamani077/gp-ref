Urinary incontinence

Common in elderly ladies with prev pregnancy/surgery, obesity 

Ix - examine for prolapse, bladder diary 3d, urine MSU, urodynamics

Stress - pelvic floor exercises “Kegel” (8 contractions TDS 3mo), surgical tape procedures (duloxetine if declines)

Urge/OAB (detrusor overactivity) - bladder retraining 6wks, antimuscarinics (avoid immediate release in older frail women eg oxybutinin/tolderidone/solifenacin TDS)…mirbegron b3 agonist better for frail 

Other - mixed, bladder outflow/overflow, functional

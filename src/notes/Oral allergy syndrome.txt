Oral allergy syndrome 

Itching/swelling of lips/mouth within mins of eating certain foods, cross sensitivity with birch pollen on skin, no systemic symptoms but other atopic features - tends to resolve within the hour, but some have anaphylaxis!

Clinical Dx, avoidance & ensure food cooked/heated to kill off pollen, antihistamine 
But if concerned refer for allergy testing (IgE RAST, food specific RAST, skin prick)

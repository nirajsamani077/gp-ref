Mania vs hypomania 

Mania - >7 days severe functional impairment/psychotic sx….+ depression = type 1 BAD

Hypomania - >4 days, not functionally impaired, no psychosis….+ depression = type 2 BAD

If mania on an SSRI/SNRI (particularly venlafaxine) consider stopping the antidepressant & starting an antipsychotic (risperidone) 

Must stop driving & inform DVLA
- if stable disease = 6mo
- if unstable (>4 swings/yr) disease = 3mo

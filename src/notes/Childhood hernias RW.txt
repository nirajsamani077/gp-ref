Childhood hernias RW

Inguinal 
• All need referral, often BL, high risk of strangulation/incarceration so all need treatment

Umbilical
• Most resolve by 4yo and don’t need referral, only refer if persisting or strangated/incarcerated (rare)

Epigastric 
• Most need routine referral as don’t tend to resolve but don’t tend to strangulate/incarcerate, associated with other midline defects

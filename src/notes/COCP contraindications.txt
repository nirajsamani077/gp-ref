COCP contraindications 

UKMEC4 (unacceptable)
• migraine with aura 
• previous VTE or mutation, positive APS/lupus
• previous stroke or IHD
• uncontrolled HTN
• current breast CA
• >35 smoking >15/d
• within 4 weeks of major surgery (stop, barrier) 

UKMEC 3
• FHx VTE
• BMI >35
• controlled HTN
• BRCA mutation or FHx breast CA
• immobility (wheelchair)
• >35 smoking <15/d
• gallbladder disease 
• breastfeeding <6wks PP

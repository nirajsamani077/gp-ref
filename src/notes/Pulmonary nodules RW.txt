Pulmonary nodules RW

Common incidental finding on CXR/CT thorax (confirm with CT)

Discuss all with resp via A&G for surveillance plan 
• Small solid nodules <5mm usually don’t need further surveillance and d/c
• Anything else will need surveillance
   - <8mm usually CT at 3mo & 12mo then RV
   - >8mm use “Brock” model to decide, may need PET-CT +/- VATS Bx if high risk 

Most turn out to be benign but don’t forget smoking cessation

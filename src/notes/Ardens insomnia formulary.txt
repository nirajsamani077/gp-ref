Z drugs - use 3-7d max
- Zopiclone 7.5mg tablets - 7 tablet - Take ONE tablet at night when required to help sleep. FOR SHORT TERM USE ONLY. (3.75mg for elderly)
- Zolpidem 10mg tablets - 7 tablet - Take ONE tablet at night when required to help sleep. FOR SHORT TERM USE ONLY (5mg for elderly)

Melatonin (red on formulary though, but on NICE) - use 13w, if >55yo 
- Melatonin 2mg modified-release tablets - 30 tablet - Take ONE tablet at NIGHT 1-2 hours before bed

Daridorexant newer drug max 3mo but has 3 specific criteria 
1. Insomnia >= 3 nights/wk for >= 3 months and daytime impairment
2. Cognitive behavioural therapy not available / unsuitable / not worked
3. Duration of treatment as short as possible + review within 3 months

- Daridorexant 50mg tablets - 30 tablet - Take ONE tablet at night 30 mins before bedtime (25mg if on CYP inhibitors, ciclosporin or cipro)

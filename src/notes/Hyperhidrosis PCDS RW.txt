Hyperhidrosis PCDS RW

Axillary /palmoplantar- common, usually primary
• Driclor (aluminium chloride) antiperspirant at night after area dried, and wash off again mane, twice weekly initially up to nightly due to irritant effect, eumovate if irritated 
   - Start with 12.5% prep, if no imp 6w then 30%
   - Can still use antiperspirants with it 
• If failed refer to derm for consideration of iontophoresis (electrical current treatment), botox, anticholinergics or endoscopic sympathectomy as last resort 

Generalised (rather than focal) 
• Ix underlying cause (thyroid, DM, menopause, drugs ETOH, haem malignancy, TB)
• Often not related to medical condition = primary hyperhidrosis if well with sx starting adolescence and improving with age - anxiety obesity cofactors 
• Refer specialist if no cause found & distressing affecting QOL (only Rx = anticholinergics)

Hyperhidrosis UK for support & simple lifestyle 
• Wear loose fitting cotton clothes 
• Avoid spicy foods or stressful triggers 
• Use antiperspirants rather than deodorants

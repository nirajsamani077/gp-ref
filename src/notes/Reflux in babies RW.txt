Reflux in babies RW

Physiological,  only GORD if distressed /FTT/ recurrent resp infecfion or dental erosions 

Most need reassurance and simple advice 
• Reduce feeding volume, smaller frequent (BF on demand, 150ml/kg/d, usually max 210ml/feed
• Observe feeding /HV assessment /BF nurse 
• Safety netting on red flags 
   - Projectile = pyloric stenosis 
   - Billious = obstruction
   - Abdo mass = possible ca 
   - FTT or other sx = CMPA 

Otherwise if treating 
• BF = Trial infant Gaviscon 1-2w with each feed (mix sachet to paste with 5ml cooled boiling water, then add further 10mls and give in bottle or spoon midway through feed) 
• FF = Trial thickened formula (anti-reflux Aptamil or C&G) or infant Gaviscon 1-2w
• If persistent or distress or FTT = PPI /H2RA 4w 
• If persistent despite this refer (no Prokinetics)

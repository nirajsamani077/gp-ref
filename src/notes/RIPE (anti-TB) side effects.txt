RIPE (anti-TB) side effects 

Rifampicin - CYP inducer, orange secretions, hepatitis, flu like sx 

Isoniazid - CYP inhibitor, peripheral neuropathy (add pyroxidine), hepatitis, agranulocytosis 

Pyrazinamide - gout, arthralgia/myalgia, hepatitis 

Ethambutol - optic neuritis (check VA, colour 1st)

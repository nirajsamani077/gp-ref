Interstitial cystitis/bladder pain syndrome RW

Chronic bladder pain with symptoms like dysuria fx urgency (usually better with voiding) >6w at least with no clear cause like UTI STI OAB prostate adhesions/endo 

Keep urine frequency volume chart (bladder diary) for 3 days (BAUS)
• Usually small volume voids, certain triggers etc

Check urine, STI, bloods , USS KUB 

1st line Rx (within 6mo if Ix normal) 
• Reduce etoh/caffeine, stress red, PT, support groups (Pelvic pain support network), CBT
• If simple analgesia not helping can trial amitryptilline (min evidence)
• Refer after >6mo for cystoscopy (shows friable haemorrhagic areas called “Hunner” lesions) and other Rx (laser, intravesicular injections, cystectomy)

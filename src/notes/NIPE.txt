NIPE

Within 72h of birth 

Gestational age 
Weight, height, head circumference 

Head - HC, fontanelles, sutures, cranial swelling, cuts/bruising 
Face - obvious orofacial, ear line from edge of eye (should be under), ears straight, cleft palate/lip, 
Eyes - symmetry, red reflex (fundoscope)
Apex, murmurs 
Chest sounds
Femoral pulses 
Genitalia - hydrocele, swelling, testes in place/location, 
Hips - Barlow’s (adduct, down to dislocate), Ortolanis (abduct, GT up to relocate)

POP RW

Safe and fairly effective contraception, everyday pill (ED) with no break, generally less risks / CI vs COCP but often more unpredictable bleeding and maybe S/E (wg mood swings etc) but limited evidence for these 

Works by thickening cervical mucus and also has some effect reducing ovulation 

Main UKMECS 
- 4 = Current breast Ca 
- 3 = Prev breast Ca within 5y, liver disease, new CVD/migraine with aura whilst taking 

Can start anytime in cycle if not pregnant with 2d precautions unless d1-5 of cycle

Traditional (LNG 30mcg NET 35mcg - Norgeston
• Most won’t have significant bleeding pattern change (2/10) 
• 3h missed pill window, if UPSI between missing pill and within 2d of restarting then need EC

Desogestrel 75mcg - Cerazette/Cerelle 
• Often does effect bleeding pattern (6/10)
• Desogestrel probably more effective suppressing ovulation and has longer missed pill window 12h

Newer one with drosperinone (Slynd) not available in UK yet, longer missed pill window

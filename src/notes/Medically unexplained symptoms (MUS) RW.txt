Medically unexplained symptoms (MUS) RW

AKA persistent physical symptoms 
• Somatisation = multiple symptoms >2yrs
• Hypochondriac = convinced has serious/cancer 
• Conversion = loss of motor/sensory function, is not intentional or for material gain 
• Dissociative = similar to conversion but psych sx 
• Factitious (Munchausens) = intentional but for no apparent material gain 
• Malingering = intentional & for material gain 

Important to maintain rapport and aim for steady improvements - maintaining paced activity/exercise rather than boom-bust patterns & CBT is most effective treatment but not a cure 
• Make person feed understood & avoid conflict 
• Negotiate change of agenda - looking at things in a different way 
• Make link between physical and mental health - the brain pain/skin/gut axis - importance of sleep and mindfullness techniques 
• Acceptance & commitment therapy - accepting coping and living with the issue rather than seeking a definitive cure 
• Focus on ADLs function life & what’s important to the patient .

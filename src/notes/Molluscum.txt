Molluscum

Pink pearly papules with central umbillication
Cluster anywhere apart from palms/soles
Through close contact 

Reassure, resolve after max 18mo, but are contagious so don’t share towels/clothes, avoid scratching as may get ezcema (mild topical steroid) or infection (2% fusidic acid) around it 

If wish to remove or irritate can either 
- squeeze or pierce after warm bath 
- cryotherapy if older 

Refer if immunocompromised (HIV), ocular/lid or anogenital lesions

Premenstrual syndrome (PMS) RW

Common, 40% of women, 5% severe 
Usually hormone fluctuations before period 
Lots of generalised sx (keep diary, as only Dx if >2 cycles using NAPS website)

Simple lifestyle diet exercise stress sleep etoh
COCP - esp drospirenone containing (Yasmin), continuous rather than cyclical 
SSRI luteal phase or continuous /CBT
Complimentary options may benefit (primrose oil)
Secondary care options if severe 
• Oestrogen patch 100mcg + cyclical progestogen Utrogestan 200mg luteal phase (protection)
• GNRH analogue (prostap) to supress cycle (with add back HRT) avoid prolonged use 
• Surgical options TAH BSO!

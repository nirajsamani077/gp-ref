Hydradenitis supparativa

Nodules pustules sinuses boils in intertrigenous regions (axilla, groin) with rope like scarring

Good hygeine, weight loss, smoking 
Acute flare - steroid (intralesional, oral), fluclox 
LT - topical clindamycin, oral lymecycline

DDx - acne, folliculitis & other follicular pyodermas (furuncles, carbuncles), granuloma ingunale/donovanosis (klebsiella STI with bleeding inguinal ulcer)

Trichomonas vs bacterial vaginosis

TV
• protozoan STI
• offensive green discharge + vulvovaginitis
• strawberry cervix, pH >4.5
• motile trophozoites on wet mount 
• oral metronidazole 5-7d, or one off 2g

BV
• non-STI 
• fishy thin white discharge 
• pH >4.5
• clue cells on microscopy 
• oral metronidazole
• risk of miscarriage in pregnancy

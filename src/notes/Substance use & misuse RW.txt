Substance use & misuse RW

Common- ask esp if physical/MH/social issue 
Find out specifics of what how when etc 

Signpost to FRANK website to give them more information about the harms of using the drug & inform of risks of BBV if needles (offer testing HIV Hep B/C)

Offer self referral to CGL for specialist drug & alcohol service 

Opiates & BDZ specifically need withdrawal and prescribing & monitoring for this (eg methadone/buprenorphine - partial opioid agonists) should be done by CGL or specialist service 
• Newer drugs for relapse prevention = naltrexone (opioid antagonist) & sublingual buprenorphine/naloxone which can cause dental decay 

Depressants specifics 
• Cannabis (THC) - “high” red eyes slow movements tachycardia but can be low or high stimulant effect, risk of long term resp & cognitive
• BDZ - feeling sedated/less anxious, risk of addiction & cognitive, withdrawal dangerous (peaks d10 anxiety hallucinations seizures)
• Opiates /Heroin - pinpoint pupils, hypotension, risks of IVDU (BBV, VTE, endocarditis) addiction and OD causing resp depression, withdrawal dangerous (peaks d2 insomnia secretions yawning vomiting sweaty dilated pupils) 

Stimulants specifics
• Cocaine - rapid speech agitation dilated pupils HTN, risks MI switching rhabdo & dependence 
• Ecstasy (MDMA) - same as coke, risks sweating dehydration hyponatraemia coma 
• Speed (amphetamines) - same as coke, risks both of above and jaw clenching/grinding & paranoid delusions/hallucinations (LT psychosis)
• Nitrous oxide balloons (happy gas) - very short onset giggles lightheaded but risk of brady LOC and long term B12 deficiency 

Hallucinogens & dissociatives specifics 
• Ketamine - dream like detached, profound detachment/stupor (K-hole), deaths rare but can cause reckless behaviour and LT cognitive, haemorrhagic ulcerative cystitis etc 
• LSD /magic mushrooms (psilocybin) - sensory distortion, can be distressing ++ (“bad trip”) causing self harm & suicide, hallucinations can persist 

Novel psychoactive drugs (legal highs) specifics
• Synthetic cannabinoids = Spice, Black Mamba, Pandoras Box - sprayed onto leaves which are smoked or liquids in vapes, unpredictable effects psychosis HTN tachycardia & much more addictive hangover effect and withdrawal 
• Downers - BDZ (diclazopam) with longer half life, opiates (fentanyls) needing bigger dose naloxone
• Stimulants - Mephedrone (M-CAT) similar but can cause panic attacks, ANS instability & malignant hyperthermia, ++ withdrawal 
• Hallucinogens - Mexxy is related to ketamine but causes lots of severe toxicity CV Cerebellar etc 

Other specifics 
• Anabolic steroids/doping eg nandrolone can cause CVD, hypogonadism & other adjuncts (clenbuterol B2 agonst, GH, insulin, diuretics)
• “Smart drugs” modafinil (used for narcolepsy) and Ritalin (used for ADHD) - common in uni 

Always important to ask - even if just for the severe and complex potential interactions with other prescribed drugs, especially MH drugs .

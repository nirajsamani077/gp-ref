Teething 

Normal process where baby/milk teeth emerge through gums, usually around 6 months. Full baby/milk teeth appear by 2-3yrs

Usually mild localised pain, biting, drooling, irritability, mild temp 

Reassurance, gentle rubbing of gum, allow child to bite on cool clean object (teething rings)
Can consider calpol/ibuprofen suspension 
Topical analgesics not recommended (risk of Reyes)

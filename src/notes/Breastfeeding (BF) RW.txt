Breastfeeding (BF) RW 

Multiple multiple multiple benefits - reduced SIDS risk, infections, allergies, obesity, even malignancy & reduces maternal breast & ovarian CA risk + great bonding!

Be mindful of vit D deficiency & tooth decay >1y

Problems to look out for with BF - signpost CMW, HV, breastfeeding specialist midwife /support 
• Mastitis risk - see section 
• Poor supply - most common reason for quitting, establish over time, regular pumping, demand led feeding, breast surgery/TFT/PPH can cause, liase specialist to observe feeds 
• Nipple pain - can be related to 
   - Tongue tie, poor latch 
   - Nipple ezcema (can use mometasone OD for 10d no need to wash off but wipe excess off)
   - Nipple thrush use miconazole cream for mum & miconazole gel for baby (failed=fluconazole 150mg stat then 50–100mg BD 10d but consider risks, small amounts in breastmilk, prolongs QTc & can be hepatotoxic/cause GI upset 
   - Vasospasm - purple colour Hx Raynauds, keep warm & trial nifedipine 20mg OD 

Drugs C/I in BF 
• ABx - tetracycline, sulphonamides, cipro,chloram
• Psych - lithium, clozapine, BDZ
• Endo - carbamizole, sulfonyureas
• Rheum - MTX
• Cardiac - amiodarone, aspirin,ARB, diuretics.

ICE phrases RW

You sound concerned about ….
Is there something specific you’re worried about….
I get the sense/feeling you’re worried, tell me more about what’s worrying you...

What do you make of all this…

Do you have thoughts as to what might be going on…

In terms of today, how did you feel I could help you best or was there something specific you wanted me to do….

It sounds as though this has been going on a while, was there something thats happened that made you come specifically today?…

Have you discussed this with your family/friends - what do they make of it??….

Did you have something particular in mind that I could do for you today? 

MIDPOINT summary - if haven’t or have kind of got to ICE by half way summarise to say - So just to summarise it sounds to me that you’ve had these symptoms for xyz and think it might be xyz. Were you worried about anything else or how did you feel I could help you the most today… or if not got much use well you’ve told me xyz what do you make of it all? ***try to summarise ICE rather than symptoms***

Use what they said - you mentioned an X-ray, you mentioned bloods etc, you were worried about cancer, you thought this might be an infection & then add your management or view on it agree for example or “from my point of view or but actually the good news is that I am less worried about this being something like cancer” 

For most cons 
• Opening gambit with ICE- let them talk,  be open & general. Don’t but in unless clear pause. Tell me more. Tell me how this is affecting you. What are you worried about? What do you make of it? What have you tried already? 
• Hone down - so to narrow down things a bit or to get to the bottom of this I’ve got a few specific yes/no or quick questions to ask you - other symptoms and red flags. If mentions something write down say let’s park that we’ll come back fo that later but make sure important qs in there.
• Come back out - tell me a bit more about you. Social side, medical hx, stress sleep etoh smoking life position work family kids 
• Mid point ICE - so you’ve told me xyz which is really useful thanks, you sound like you might be worried about xyz, tell me if I’m missing something, is there something particular you were hoping for out of today 
• Quick double check/clarify - Before I tell you how I think we could manage this perhaps let’s quickly check , you mentioned xyz before could I just clarify a bit more …missed flags 
• Examine with ICE - I think we need to examine you F2F to check for xyz as you’re concerned this might be xyz
• Investigate with ICE- as you told me xyz I agree it might be a chest issue so I’m going to arrange xyz to rule that out. Or if 2WW scenario- from what you’ve told me I’m concerned this might be symptoms where we ought to do tests to rule out cancer, or these symptoms do sound worrying so perhaps it’s best to we do some tests quickly to make sure something sinister isn’t going on. Whilst I’m not sure exactly what’s going on the tests might be able to help us more 
• Management with ICE, it sounds like you’ve been trying all the right things or you mentioned xyz or that you felt you needed xyz. I do agree or perhaps let’s talk about a few different options that people tend to try first that usually get things better. You mentioned about your weight, and would be inclined to agree that that might be impacting on your symptoms and perhaps thinking about methods of losing some weight could be really helpful . I’m not sure medication is the answer here, easy for me to prescribe a pill but I do wonder if that’s actually the best thing for you here, what do you reckon? 
• Shared agreed management plan and clear next steps - OK so it looks like we’ve come to an agreement about xyz, that’s great. I can help you along with these things by sending you xyz or referring you to xyz. I am hopeful that this will have some impact but I’m interested to follow things up with you 
• Follow- up for Almost all -let’s check in in a couple of weeks to see how you’re getting on, I’m always interested to see your progress, perhaps if this hasn’t improved after 6 weeks we could discuss the next steps of referring you or doing some blood tests 
• Safety net - now before you go I mentioned some other symptoms before & I want you to be mindful that if things change or get worse especially if you start getting xyz then book a review earlier or even attending A&E/calling 999/get some urgent advice.  - then back to f/u but touch wood that won’t happen and I’m looking forward to catching up with you in x or I want to wish you the best of luck going forward!

Achilles tendinopathy 

Aching heel pain, worse with activity/pressure 
Can be insertional (posterior calcaneous) or mid-portion (2-6cm up) 
Hopping and heel-raise 
Check for tendon rupture (sudden, snap, bruising, can’t WB, Simmonds triad when prone with feet over edge, angle of declination/Dorsiflexion increased, palpable gap, calf squeeze doesn’t cause PF)

DDx - plantar fasciitis, severs disease (calcaneal apophysitis), bursitis (retrocalcaneal), fracture, sural nerve 

Remember cipro (quininolone) can cause 

RICE, physio after 7-10d if not improved (eccentric exercises, heavy load), Refer (shockwave therapy, surgery)

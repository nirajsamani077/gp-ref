Measles RW

Highly highly contagious - single case results in nearly 20 others! Incidence rising again due to low MMR vaccine uptake 

Incubating period 1-3w 
Flu like illness with fever coryza conjunctivitis then 
Macpap red rash starting on face/behind ears spreading down + Koplik white buccal spots   
• Rubella, slapped cheek, roseola can be similar 

If suspecting measles case  
• Notify PHE immediately who will contact trace and offer PEP with measles Ig to high risk (pregnant, IC) - effective up to 6d post exposure 
• May do throat swab PCR or IgM serology 
• Isolate with supportive, infectious 4d either side of starting rash so keep away from high risk 

Comps - pneumonia, encephalitis (late = subacute sclerosing panencephalitis)

MMR 2 doses confers lifelong protection .

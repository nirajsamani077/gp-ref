Genital ulcers DDx RW

Painful 4
• Common 2 = Herpes & cancer 
   - Genital herpes (HSV2) - crops of multiple painful vesicles to ulcers, associated with fever/dysuria etc, painful ++ in primary, recurrent less severe & more localised
   - Carcinoma (vulval, penile)
• Rare 2 = Chancroid & Behcets 
   - Chancroid - haemophilus ducreyi tropical STI, painful sharp ragged undermined border, ulcer with unilateral painful inguinal PN. Rx with azithro
   - Behcets -genital/oral ulcers, arthritis/vision/VTE

Painless 4
• Common 2 = Syphillis & pearly penile papules 
   - Syphillis - primary painless chancre, IP 9-90d
   - Pearly penile papules (normal variant, 1-2mm)
• Rare 2 = LGV & granuloma inguinale 
   - Lymphogranuloma venerum (LGV) - chlamydia trachomatis tropical STI = stage 1 painless pustule to ulcer, 2 painful inguinal LN, 3 proctocolitis. Rx with doxycycline 
   - Granuloma inguinale - klebsiella tropical STI, painless red lump slowly enlarges & breaks down into sore, donovanosis. Rx with azithromycin/doxy

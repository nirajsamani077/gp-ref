Bronchiolitis RW

Viral LRTI in <2yo (peak 3-6mo), 1/3 under 1 get it, due to RSV - common reason for admission but Rx supportive observation O2 feeding etc (May need CPAP, NGT feeding, and even intubation)

Coryza, cough/wheeze, IWOB, apnoeas (esp <6w)
Fever usually mild (<39), feeding down & mixed chest signs wheeze crackles etc 
Severity peaks at 3-5d (so if early ?admit) 
• DDx = Bacterial pneumonia or VIW (old wheezer)

Any sats <90% (<92% if <6w), severe iWOB (RR>60), Hx of apnea or poor feeding (<50%)/dehydration = admission, if former 3 call 999 

Lower threshold if premature, age <3mo, CLD/CHD

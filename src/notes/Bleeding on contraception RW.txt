Bleeding on contraception RW

Within first 3mo of starting or changing contraception unscheduled bleeding is common 

Check compliance 
Smears 
STI risk /symptoms 
If IUS/IUD where are the threads 
Any RF endometrial CA (PCOS, DM, BMI, tamox)

If <3mo generally reassure and offer TXA, ensure compliance, back to back 3mo, can increase oestrogen or change progestagen if COCP, can try different POP, can add 30mcg COCP continuous to those with LARC (implant depot Mirena) 

If >3mo examine STI swabs, smear if due & consider TV USS pelvis & referral 

Bleeding pattern changes 
• COCP - usually 1/5 have change within 3mo usually settles LT
• POP - usually 1/3 have change which may not settle (desogestrel probably worse than traditional POP)
• Depot/implant - bleeding is common first 3mo, about half amenorrhoeic at 1y
• IUS - similar to above but higher eventual amenorrhoea/less heavy bleeding rate

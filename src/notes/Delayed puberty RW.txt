Delayed puberty RW

Suspect and refer if 
• Girl with thelarche not happened >13 (remember precocious age = 8)
• Boy with no testicular development >14 (remember pp age = 9)

Needs HPG axis maturity (which is different from androgen production from adrenals) 
Check growth, FHx , MPH, examine for SSCs, make sure good nutrition/exercise then refer 

Causes
• Constitutional - majority 
• Hypergonadotrophic hypogonadism (normal or high GNRH) - Gonadal issue Turners, chemo/DXT
• Hypogonadotrophic - CNS issue eg tumour, infection, Kallmans, functional (systemic illness exercise etc)

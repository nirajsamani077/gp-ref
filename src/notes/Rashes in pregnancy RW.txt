Rashes in pregnancy RW

Pre-existing skin conditions can get better, worse or stay same in pregnancy - consider risks of certain treatments when becoming pregnant 
• Retinoids & tetracyclines/TMP (acne treatment)
• MTX and other DMARDs 
• Moderate topical steroids safe but up to 200g of potent is safe during pregnancy, risk of IUGR

Specific conditions
• Atopic eruption of pregnancy - common, ezcematous lesions on trunk/limbs, manage as per ezcema 
• Polymorphic eruption of pregnancy - primips first few weeks or immediately post partum, starts in stretch marks itchy papules spares umbilicus, manage as per eczema, no adverse effect to baby
• Pemphigoid gestationis - rare blistering, pruritic periumbillical lesions any TM, may need oral steroids & derm input, risk of IUGR 
• Obstetric cholestasis - If itch without rash 3rd TM check bile acids LFT and refer for UDO scans .

Raised platelets (thrombocytosis) & cancer RW

Plts >400 & age >40 = significantly predictive of cancer diagnosis in next 12mo so investigate!!!

LEG = Lung, Endometrial, GI (GO, CRC) or LEGO-C 

Primary (clonal) thrombocytosis = myeloproliferative /haem disorders (essential thrombocytosis, CML)

Other secondary (reactive) thrombocytosis causes apart from solid malignancy as above = IDA, infection/inflamm,blood loss, splenectomy, surgery 

If no obvious UL/reactive cause (eg infection inflammation) & no immediate 2WW based on sx 
• Examine - HSM, LN, chest /abdo, weight 
• Bloods - repeat FBC with film, haematinics, CRP
• CXR & FIT test (if >40 but really any age) 
• OGD & TVUSS/CA125/hysteroscopy if >55 focal 

If malignancy R/O above or unlikely - check in 1mo and if remains raised refer haem for JAK-2 & BM biopsy 

Tumours to bone = LUB 
• Lung Urol (prostate sclerotic, renal) Breast .

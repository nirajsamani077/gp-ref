Actinic keratosis (AK)

Sun exposure causing premalignant small crusty scaly lesions multiple colours in sun exposed areas 

Treatment 
- sun avoidance and sunscreen 
- 5-FU (efudix) cream - 4 weeks once at night before bed, causes red sore skin initially usually settles (worse before better), if not try topical hydrocortisone
- Other - cryo/cautery/curettage if single lesion, topical imiquimod or 3% diclofenac (mild only)

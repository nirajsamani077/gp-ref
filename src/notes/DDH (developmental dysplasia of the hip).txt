DDH (developmental dysplasia of the hip) RW

1% of newborns clicky hips main RFs = family Hx, multiple pregnancy or breech (all need USS), big premature oligo baby also, most common reason for young hip replacement 

On NIPE & 6-8 week baby check 
• Barlow - to dislocate hip, when abducted adduct hip with posterior stress on GT 
• Ortolani - to relocate hip, lift hip by toggling GT anteriorly + abducting 
• Easy = limited hip abduction in full flexion (<60)
• Later = foreshortening, limited hip abduction 

If normal exam but RFs = USS @ 6w (hospital)
If abnormal exam = USS @ 2w, refer specialist & start treatment ASAP (reduce & Pavlik harness or surgery) .

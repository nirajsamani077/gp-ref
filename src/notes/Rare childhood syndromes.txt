Rare childhood syndromes

Patau (t13)
- Microcephalic, small eyes
- Cleft palate 
- Polydactyly

Edwards (t18)
- Micrognathia (small jaw)
- Low-set ears
- Rocker bottom feet
- Overlapping fingers 

Noonan
- Short 
- Webbed neck
- Pectus excavatum
- Pulmonary stenosis

Williams 
- Short
- Learning difficulties, friendly/extrovert personality
- Transient neonatal hypercalcaemia
- Supravalvular aortic stenosis

Pierre-Robin (similar to Treacher-Collins, is AD)
- Micrognathia (small jaw)
- Posterior displacement of tongue
- Cleft palate

Prader-Willi
- Obesity
- Hypotonia
- Hypogonadism

Fragile X (everything big apart from IQ)
- Learning difficulties
- Macrocephaly
- Long face, large ears
- Macro-orchidism

Cri-du-chat 
- Characteristic cry
- FTT
- Learning difficulties
- Microcephaly & micrognathism

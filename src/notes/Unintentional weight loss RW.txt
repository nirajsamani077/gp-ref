Unintentional weight loss RW

Worrying! 5% over 6-12mo = significant, not normal to lose weight with age (usually maintained within 0.5kg each year). Common 4 causes 
• Malignancy - esp if smoker, ETOH, FHx red flags 
• Malnutrition/chronic disease (CKD, CCF, COPD)
   - Don’t forget neglect, isolation, poverty 
• Depression 
• Dementia 

Hx/Ex - Objective weight, food intake, general symptom & mood/cognitive screen, chronic illness 
• Abdo/chest, PR rectum prostate, LNs, breast 
• Check for dental issues eg thrush, dentures 

Investigating 
• Follow any abnormalities & 2WW appropriately 
• Bloods - FBC UE LFT bone ESR myeloma screen, PSA in men, CA125 in women 
• Symptomatic FIT any adult >50 (any age really)
• Imaging - direct access CT TAP (if not CXR USS abdo/TV pelvis etc)

Management (if no cause found) 
• Refer to elderly care if >3mo 
• Dietician SALT & nutritional assessment - consider fortisips, thickeners etc 
• Consider carer to assist feeding, meal delivery services & shared mealtimes

GBS RW 

Most common cause of neonatal sepsis 
20% of mothers are carriers - in vaginal flora 

Risk factors - preterm, PPROM, previous which can lead to neonatal sepsis (1% - early onset GBS)
• Will often be screened for sepsis if RFs or have GBS observations if not 

IV benpen intrapartum (IAP - intrapartum antibiotic prophylaxis) 

Not universally screened for but if picked up on HVS/urine then needs intrapartum IV Abx and also in subsequent pregnancy.

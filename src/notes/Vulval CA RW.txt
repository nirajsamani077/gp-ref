Vulval CA RW

Rare, elderly women (esp >90), mostly SCC related to chronic vulval dermatoses (eg lichen sclerosis) or previous oncogenic HPV infection 

VIN (vulval intraepithelial neoplasia) is pre-malignant/early malignant phase, similar to CIN

Obvious 2WW referral to Gynae = irregular ulcer/bleeding/lump (complex = fungating mass or LNs) 
• Will Bx, FIGO stage & usually surgical excision depending on fitness often with good prognosis if no mets but can have significant impact .

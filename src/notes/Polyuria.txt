Polyuria

Large urine volumes (not frequent amounts of small urine which = frequency) 

Top causes 
• DM
• DI (usually nephrogenic related to hypercalcaemia/drugs E.g. lithium, rarely cranial due to pituitary tumour/trauma/sarcoid)
• Diuretics 
• ETOH 
• Renal/ urinary (CKD, relief of CUR)

5 ready reckoners 
• Thirst?
• Episodic or continuous?
• Abdo pain/vomiting?
• Weight/appetite loss?
• Urinalysis abnormal?

History 
• Thirsty? (DM, DI think high calcium)
• Episodic? (Diuretics, ETOH) 
• LUTS, haematuria, dysuria, vomiting, TATT 
• Bones, stones, moans, groans 
• In smoker/cough with weight loss + polyuria think lung SCC (ACTH secreting tumour)

Examine 
• Abdo mass, signs of DM, urine dip 

Investigate 
• Urine MSU. Bloods - HBA1C, bone profile, U&E

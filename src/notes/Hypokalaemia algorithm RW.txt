Hypokalaemia algorithm RW

If <2.5 or <3 with ECG changes (TWI, ST depression, long QT, U) = admit for IV replacement 
• Symptoms (myalgias, cardiac, neuro) or high risk group (diabetic check BM as sign of DKA, HF, IHD, on digoxin) = low threshold to admit 

98% of causes = Diuretics or GI loss (D&V) !!!
• Hypomagnesaemia, dig toxicity 
• Endocrine + HTN - Conns, Cushings, CAH, Liddle
• Other drugs - SABA, insulin, laxatives, ETOH
• Rare - RTA, eating disorder (anorexia), burns 

Oral replacement (>3 or >2.5 no ECG, A/S, no risk)
• Sando K 2 tabs TDS for up to 5d and repeat 
• Stop diuretics ST and manage /Ix GI losses 
• Don’t give Sando K if on spiro (risks HyperK ++)

If choosing to Ix underlying cause
• BP & BM 
• Mg, FBC (leukaemia rare cause), dig level 
• Refer endocrine if ?Conns or Cushings (can check Urine K:Cr ratio if raised = renal loss)

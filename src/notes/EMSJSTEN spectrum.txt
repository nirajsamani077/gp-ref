- Allopurinol
- COCP

Erythema multiforme - target lesions, starts back of hands/feet spreading to torso. Also caused by virus (HSV), bacteria (Mycoplasma), SLE, sarcoidosis, malignancy

Toxic epidermal necrolysis (TEN)
- systemic blistering easily rupturing rash with MM involvement, systemically unwell, Nikolskys splitting on lateral pressure 
- stop ppt, ITU care, IVIg, immunosuppression

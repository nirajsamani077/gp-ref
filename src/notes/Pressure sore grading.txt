Pressure sore grading 

Waterlow score 
1 - non blanching erythema 
2 - partial skin loss (abrasion/blister)
3 - full thickness skin loss down to fascia 
4 - extensive necrosis with muscle/bone damage 

Treatment 
TVN, hydrocolloid/gel dressing, avoid soap (dries wound, want to keep moist), abx/debridement if clinically indicated

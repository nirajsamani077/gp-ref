Chronic pelvic pain RW

Long term multi factorial common 80% non gynae (bowel bladder msk neuro psych) 
First proper Hx and red flags - PMB PCB IMB mass new bowel sx WL PR bleeding 
Abdo PV spec swabs STI If RFs Check pelvic floor tone (squeeze during bimanual) 
Ix - TV USS, CA125 esp >50 bloating bowel urinary 

Management is hard - biopsychosocial
• Keep pain diary and listen 
• If cyclical pain dyspaeurnia trial 3-6mo ovarian suppression (continuous COCP or LARCs) 
• Manage lifestyle (caffeine ETOH stress sleep exercise), treat IBS, CBT, acupuncture, neuropathic meds and SSRI if component , consider myofasicial pain (physio) and referral to pain clinic

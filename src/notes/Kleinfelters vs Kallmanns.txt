Kleinfelters vs Kallmanns

Kleinfelters
- LH/FSH raised, low sex hormones 

Kallmanns
- hypogonadotrophic hypogonadism (low GnRH from hypothalamus)
- X-linked recessive - boys
- delayed puberty, cryptorchidism, anosmia, height can be normal 
- LH/FSH low or inappropriately normal with low sex hormones

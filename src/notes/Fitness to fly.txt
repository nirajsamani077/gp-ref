Fitness to fly 

CVS 
- any uncontrolled/severe disease should not fly 
- post uncomplicated MI >1wk, complicated >1mo
- Treated with PCI >3d
- post CABG >2wk
- Stroke generals >10d but may be >3d if stable 

Resp 
- PTX only >2wk after successful drainage with no residual air (CAA), >1wk post check X-ray (BTS)
- For diving should be completely avoided unless had surgical pleurectomy with normal post-op normal lung function and CT chest 

Haem 
- Hb <80 cannot travel 

Post-op
- laparoscopic or scope >24h
- abdo surgery >10d
- POP >24h if short haul, >48h otherwise 

Pregnancy 
- >28wk need certificate, >36wk cannot fly (32wk for twins)

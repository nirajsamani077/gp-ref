Carpal tunnel (CTS) RW

Common, self limiting, clinical Dx
Intermittent pain tingling lateral 4 digits & palm 
Trapped median nerve under tight carpal ligament at wrist, often no obvious cause, sometimes thyroid pregnancy RA fracture overuse

DDx
• Cspine radiculopathy (esp BL sx) or thoracic outlet obstruction 
• Cubital tunnel (Same on little finger from elbow)
• DQTS and thumb Oa can cause pain but no sensory change 
• Nerve conduction not usually indicated or helpful 

Rx
• 2 conservative measures = wrist splint at night, steroid injection x1 = if not improving by 6w refer as should not delay surgery if eligible as can lead to poor outcomes - for carpal tunnel release to decompress space (1% recurrence rate)

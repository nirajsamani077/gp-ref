UGI 2WW RW

2WW OGD if 
• Dysphagia 
• Any upper abdo mass
• >55 with weight loss and pain or reflux 

Non urgent OGD if almost anything else 
• Haematemesis (usually admission!)
• Upper abdo pain, N&V
• Treatment resistant reflux  
• Anaemia, raised platelets (check FBC)

Pancreatic /HPB (2WW urgent access CT or USS)
• New painless jaundice >40
• >60 with weight loss + any GI symptom/ new DM 
• Palpable liver edge or ?enlarged gallbladder although if upper abdo mass would be UGI 2WW.

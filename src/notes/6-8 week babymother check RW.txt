6-8 week baby/mother check RW 

General check
• How was the birth? Was motherhood what you were expecting? Any specific concerns?
• Ask about sleep, support and think about SG
• Ask specifics of gestation, delivery, comps, SCBU, hospital f/u, HV/CMW input

Mother check 
• Medical stuff 
   - PET - Recheck BP & urine dip 
   - GDM - Fasting glucose, annual HbA1C if nad 
   - VTE, anaemia (consider rpt FBC), sepsis 
   - Offer MMR if not immune to Rubella 
   - Advise smoking cessation, avoid ETOH
   - Low impact exercise when feeling ready 
• Wound issues (tear, episiotomy) & pelvic floor 
   - Wound healing/infection, D/C, bleeding, pain 
   - If any above examine & swab, consider FGM 
   - Bladder/bowel continence & prolapse
   - PFE 3mo TDS 8 contractions (Squeezy app)
• Breast issues if breastfeeding 
   - Feeding, latch, HV input, BF nurses, expressing 
   - Nipple pain or mastitis 
• Contraception 
   - Enquire about sexual activity & plans for further 
   - If BF may suppress periods but not ovulation 
   - Encourage LARCs as most effective if no plan 
   - Check smear UTD (if not can be done 12w PP)

Baby check - DC summary, NIPE, Red book 
• Development - smile? fix/follow? noise response?
• Feeding - consider tongue tie (short thick tight lingual frenulum restricting bottom of tongue causing BF difficulty and nipple pain) - refer HV or breastfeeding MW to Dx & consider frenulotomy
• Nappies 
• Overall apprearance - colour, cry, dysmorphia

• Head & face
   - Fontanelles soft, anterior closes 9-18mo, posterior closes 6-8w, refer if ?craniosyntosis (premature closure of posterior fontanelle)
   - Head shape brachycephaly (UL flat) & plagiocephaly (posterior flat) usually resolves 
   - Cleft palate & obvious dysmorphic features 
• Eyes - red reflex, strabismus, fix & follow 
   - Refer if no red reflex ?retinoblastoma/cataract

• Cardioresp & abdo/hernias/genitals/anus (front)
   - Check neck and clavicles ?undiagnosed #
   - Full HS all 4 areas for murmur (soft systolic murmur at LSE in well infant likely innocent but refer if concerned), then listen lungs 
   - Femoral pulses (equal) & any obvious inguinal hernia (refer if present) 
   - Umbillcal stump (if hernia observe as should resolve, refer if persists 3y& organomegaly (HSM) 
   - Nappy off, testes, hypospadias, anal patency 
   
• MSK limbs & hips (peripheries) 
   - Check over all limbs and digits 
   - Clubfoot (talipes) PF inverted foot, ref PT/ortho 
   - Hip exam to R/O DDH (check leg length/symm)
      - Barlow’s - flex & adduct hip while keeping UL hip fixed then downward pressure on knee, feel pop if dislocates 
      - Ortolani’s - pop index & middle on GT, thumb in groin, abduct and upward pressure on GT, femoral head relocates with a clunk 
      - Refer urgent ortho if positive/less abduction 

• Neuro & skin (back) 
   - Check tone, lie on front and if can hold head level with body = good truncal tone (reflexes Moros & Grasp if concerned) 
   - Check over spine for sacral dimple (>5mm in size, >25mm from anus) hair tufts - refer USS lumbar spine or paeds if concern ?bifida occulta 
   - Check any unusual skin marks, blue spots etc 

• Red book measurements (HC, weight) & if faltering growth repeat & refer, check rendering issues and ensure any breastfed or mixed <500ml formula/d should use vitamin D drops 400IU (and mother 400 IU), from 1y start Healthy Start vitamin

• Obvious final things - avoid cosleeping (SIDS risk), encourage skin to skin time & check in re perinatal MH again (can refer perinatal MH services or get HV help) .

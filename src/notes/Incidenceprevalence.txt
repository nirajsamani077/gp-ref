Incidence/prevalence 

Incidence = number of new cases per population in certain time-frame 
• 40 new cases in a 1200 population in last year = 3.33% annual incidence 

Prevalence = total number of cases per population at certain time (point prevalence) or in certain time-frame (period prevalence)
• 500 obese people in 10000 population = 5% prevalence of obesity 

Prevalence = incidence x duration of condition 
If chronic disease prevalence > incidence 
If acute incidence usually = prevalance

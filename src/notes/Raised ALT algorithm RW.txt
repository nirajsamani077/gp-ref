Raised ALT algorithm RW

Consider investigating even if mildly raised (as degree of rise may not correlate to damage)
Remember raised ALP, GGT main differentiator 

Check 
• ETOH
• BMI /metabolic history 
• Hepatotoxic drugs (MTX, amiodarone, statins, terbinafine, valproate, tetracyclines etc)
• Viral hepatitis RFs 
• FHx HH Wilson’s A1AT

Repeat 
• Liver + AST, GGT (AST>ALT suggests significant fibrosis)
• FBC, ferritin, iron studies (calculate TSAT)
• HbA1C & Hep B/C serology 
• AI screen (AMA, ASMA, ANA, Igs)
+ US abdomen 

AST plts useful for Fib-4 and AST:ALT ratio >1 suggests advanced fibrosis (>2 suggests etoh, <1 suggests viral)

Based on results will be 
• ETOH liver disease or NAFLD - follow pathway 
• Hepatitis / AI / unclear cause = refer

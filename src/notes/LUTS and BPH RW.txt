LUTS and BPH RW

Nearly half of men >50 have LUTS to some degree

2 categories in history 
• Storage - more OAB type sx urgency frequency nocturia incontinence 
• Voiding - classic obstructive BPH type hesitancy poor stream straining incomplete emptying terminal dribbling 

Ask all with LUTS to do IPSS (impact on QOL)
• <7 mild, >20 severe 

Examine abdo, genitalia, DRE prostate 
Urine dip +/ MC&S
Consider PSA (counsel) deps sx (WL, back, FH)
Consider U&E if obstructive evidence (bladder) 
Urine frequency volume chart (usually normal 3 cups of urine per void, 3h between each, total 3L per day, most younger won’t have to wake up to void) - chart for 3d using a jug, if reduced volumes more frequent variations OAB, if good volumes more frequent night excess fluid or polyuria, if reduced volume no variation red flag CA)

Management 
• Lifestyle - modify fluid caffeine etoh timings amount decaf, don’t reduce intake overall of water
• Containment products via Bladder Bowel UK if incontinence , urethral milking if terminal dribbling 
• If OAB storage sx go down continence nurses route for bladder retraining anticholingergics etc
• For voiding sx with suspected BPH
   - Tamsulosin (Alpha blocker, Flomax)- relaxes prostate muscle to open up urethra if moderate + IPSS not responding to lifestyle, works rapidly, 400mcg OD, main S/E dizziness and postural HTN take at bedtime , hypotension 
   - Finasteride (5ARI) - reduces prostate size (inhibits testosterone to DHtestosterone) only in large prostate >30g golf ball size or bigger considered high risk of progression (older). Delayed effect 3-6mo, main risk ED/sexual dysfunction and masks a high PSA
   - Consider OAB management anticholingerics, mirabegron etc if elements of storage sx not responding to initial Rx 
   - Both diuretic in mid afternoon and desmopressin for nocturia unlicensed 

Refer to consider TURP 
• Any obstruction (retention, AKI,CUR)
• Failed above management 
• Suspected Ca via 2WW

LTC if above failed, not suitable for surgery , tried ISC, distress etc (remember acute retention catheter before twoc give tamsulosin for 2 before to relax prostate 

TURP
• cystoscope, shave off urethral prostate using an electric loop
• Comps = Bleeding, UTI, retrograde ejaculation (almost all), ED (10%)
• Minimally invasive surgery (eg prostatic urethral lift, microwave, water vapour tx) emerging

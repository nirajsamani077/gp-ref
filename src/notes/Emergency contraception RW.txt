Emergency contraception RW

Levonelle (LNG) 
• within 72h (3 days) of UPSI (85% effective) 
• single dose 1.5mg (double if weight >70kg or BMI >26 or on inducers, EllaOne Better for high BMI)
• can rarely cause vomiting, repeat dose if vomits within 3h
• can use >1x in cycle 
• can start other hormonal contraception immediately 

EllaOne (UPA)
• within 120h (5 days) of UPSI, better if high risk 
• single dose 30mg
• can now use >1x in cycle although previously not allowed (don’t use with LNG though)
• caution in severe asthma and interacts with enzyme inducers 
• can only start hormonal contraception after 5 days as reduces effectiveness, delay BF 1 week (also check wasn’t taking hormonal before as can’t use although if was on COCP and missed 2 pills in first week after break and UPSI then only situation where can use EllaOne and re quick start COCP again with precautions 7d)

Copper IUD
• most effective so offer to all (99%)
• within 120h (5 days) of UPSI or likely ovulation date
• can leave in situ for LT but if want to remove have to wait until after next period 

Check pregnancy test at 3w

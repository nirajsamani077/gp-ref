Limping child 

Any age can be septic joint (fever can’t WB), malignancy, JIA

<3 =Toddlers fracture (spiral tibial fracture), sprain/pull/growth plate injury/NAI or DDH (birth RFs, assymetric skin folds, shortening, limited abduction). 

3-10 = Irritable hip or Perthes (AVN femoral head, usually UL, subacute limp reduced rotation, 

10+ = SUFE (overweight boys, limb pain, out-toeing shortened gait), overuse apophysitis /small microavulsion fractures in ossification centres (osgood TT, severs calcaneal usually self limiting 2wk- 2mo) can recur til skeletal maturity 

Low threshold to refer any all for same day review XR

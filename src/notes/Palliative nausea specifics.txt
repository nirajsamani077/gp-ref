Palliative nausea specifics 

6 main causes & treatments 

1. Gastric stasis (often opioid) - prokinetics such as metoclopramide (avoid if bowel obstruction)
2. Chemical (calcium, chemo, opioids) - ondans, haloperidol
3. Visceral (constipation, candida) - cyclizine, levo
4. Raised ICP (mets) - cyclizine, dex, DXT
5. Vestibular (often opioid) - cyclizine, metoclop
6. Anxiety - BDZ (lorazepam)

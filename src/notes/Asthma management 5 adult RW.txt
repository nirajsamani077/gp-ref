Asthma management >5 /adult RW

New GINA guidance -single inhaler therapy (SIT) generally >12

Initial PRN with single inhaler therapy (SIT)
• SIT-AIR - low dose ICS /LABA fast acting formoterol ie ICS-formoterol (eg Symbicort 100/6 2 puffs Bd budesonide or Fostair NEXThaler 100/6 beclo) as PRN only (max 8 puffs a day), used to be SABA PRN
• (Can use NICE traditional SABA PRN + low dose ICS preventer rather than GINA SIT, SIT preferred for >12)

If not controlled…after 8-12w 
• SIT-MART same inhaler as maintenance and PRN (can only use >12, if <12 can use SIT + reliever with same inhaler as preventer & sep SABA reliever = traditional model) 
• (Can use NICE add LABA+ICS)

If not controlled..after 8-12w
• Trial montelukast 8w
• Increase mod ICS dose (to 400mcg/d ie 200 BD) 8w which can be done as SIT-MART

If not controlled…after 8-12w
• Refer + High dose ICS (to 800mcg/d) can’t be SIT-MART) theophylline, LAMA, long-term azithro, mabs (anti-IgE = omalizumab, anti-eosinophils = mepolizumab), bronchial thermoplasty
• Difficult to control asthma (will be specialist)
   - Other Dx? - ABPA, bronchiectasis, 
   - Drug-induced? - Bb, NSAIDs/aspirin, ACE
   - Occupational asthma = PEFR dip, OH, refer 
   - Consider Igs, IgE aspergillus/RAST, CT thorax 

Aims for treatment  
• TOTAL CONTROL, sx free rarely using reliever 
• Poor control
   - Waking at night >1x/w, using SABA 3x/w
   - Issuing >3 SABA/y or <3 ICS/y
   - Exacerbation needing steroid/admission
   - Exercise induced sx 
• Make sure has personal asthma action plan (PAAP) from Asthma UK, monitor annually with PEFR diary & ACT, check inh technique & adherence 

Doses (beclometasone equivalents)
• V low dose ICS = 50mcg BD
• Low dose ICS = 100mcg BD 
• Moderate dose ICS = 200mcg BD
• High dose ICS = 400mcg BD 

Extra info 
• DPI (dry powder) have a much better carbon footprint than MDI (metered dose puffers)
• Mainly GINA guildelines (and NICE) - SIT is better for control/compliance and less carbon footprint 
• Max doses for SIT inhalers = 8 puffs/d 
• Only lower dose 100/6 inhalers licensed for SIT apart from Symbicort 200/6 turbohaler (not pMDI) 
• When stepping down ICS dose -reduce 25-50% 
• Never use LABA or LAMA without ICS in asthma - risks lifethreatening exac (can be used alone in COPD though)

Puberty signs 

Boys - 1st sign ~12yrs (10-15yrs) testicular volume >4ml, max height spurt 14yrs 

Girls - 1st sign thelarche 11.5yrs usually (9-13yrs), max height spurt 12yrs, menarche at 13yrs (11-15), 

Normal features include gynaecomastia, asymmetrical breast growth and diffuse thyroid enlargement

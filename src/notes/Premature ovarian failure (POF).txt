Premature ovarian failure (POF)

Onset of menopause <40yrs old (<45 = early)
1/100 women 
Climacteric sx + secondary amenorrhoea + infertility triad 

Causes 
- most are idiopathic 
- BSO
- DXT/chemo
- Infection (mumps)
- Autoimmune 
- Resistant ovaries (fsh reception mutation)

Bloods 
- raised FSH/LH, FSH >40 4-6 weeks apart 
- low oestrodiol (<100)

HRT or COCP until 51 (average age of menopause) 
HRT doesn’t provide contraception (risk if spontaneous ovarian activity)

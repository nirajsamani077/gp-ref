Nipple pain post partum/BF

Minor 
- poor latch
- blocked duct (milk bleb) continue breastfeeding, massage, reposition 
- nipple candidiasis - miconazole for mum, nystatin for neonate 

Major 
- mastitis - keep breastfeeding, abx if unwell, nipple fissure or not improving after 12-24h of effective milk removal, fluclox up to 14d
- engorgement - common, worse just before feed, poor flow, poor suckle, breast hard & red, can progress fully blocked duct, hand express milk 
- raynauds of nipple (vasospasm)- intermittent severe pain around feeding, colour change white red blue, avoid cold/caffeine/smoking, use heat packs, trial nifedipine (off licence)

>10% weight loss threshold in 1st week of life (1 in 10) = early FTT —> midwife led breastfeeding assessment, look for underlying cause in neonate

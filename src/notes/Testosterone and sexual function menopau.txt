Sexual dysfunction common post menopause 

Traditional HRT is first line esp with an androgenic progestagen like MPA or NET (although older than micronised which is better for other risks) , and change oestrogen to transdermal , topical oestrogen, other factors 

Oddly post menopausal ovary does produce androgens still so role is unclear 

If distressing low libido + not responded to HRT can offer testosterone but is not licensed for this (usually used for male testosterone deficiency), counsel about risks as unknown LT safety data and risk of androgenic S/E (acne hirsuitsm mood WG)

Interstitial lung disease (ILD) RW

Complex heterogenous fibrosis - Hx (SOBOE progressive, dry cough, weight loss) & Ex (clubbing, cyanosis, crackles (fine end expiratory))
• IPF (most common, idiopathic) with histological/HRCT subtypes (UIP, NSIP) 
• CT disease (RA, scleroderma, SLE, sarcoid, TB)
• Drug-related (BANS Me) & DXT
• Occupational (coal workers, bird fanciers/farmer)

Think lung CA/ILD if SOB/cough resistant to Rx 

Refer early with direct access CXR & HRCT, spirometry (restrictive) & bloods including AI screen (ANA, ENA, C3/4, ESR etc) 
• Pulm rehab, LTOT, PT/OT, MDT, advanced plan 
• Antifibrotics (pirfenidone), steroids/DMARDs in some subtypes, lung Tx

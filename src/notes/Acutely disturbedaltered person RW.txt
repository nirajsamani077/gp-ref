Acutely disturbed/altered person RW

Keep calm (listen engage reflect reassure safe)
Raise the alarm (silent panic button, police, 999)
Bring in familiar face if available 
Consider risk, suicidality, weapons, psychosis 

DDx = drugs, psychosis, acute stress, acute medical (eg encephalopathy)

If accepts referral - 999 or to A&E best route for safe place otherwise acute crisis team 
If not - if immediate risk to self/others = police to detain under section 136 and move to place of safety (ie psych hospital) for assessment .

ADHD

Persistent attention deficit & hyperactivity/impulsivity with children needing 6 diagnostic features (5 if older than 16) 

AD - easily distracted, does not follow through, avoids mentally intense tasks, difficult to organise, forgetful, does not listen when spoken to directly 

H - unable to be quiet, talks a lot, does not wait turn, spontaneously leaves seat, on the go, interruptive, answers before question finished

Prevalence = 2.5%, most dx 3-7, male > female 

Rx: 
10 week watch and wait 
Refer to secondary care
Parental education/training 
Methylphenidate last resort if >5yrs old, 6 week trial. ECG at baseline (cardiotoxic). Monitor weight & height every 3-6 months + BP/HR. GI side effects. If not effective, lisdexamphetamine

Referral questionnaire on accurx
Birmingham community pathway - referral website and questionnaire

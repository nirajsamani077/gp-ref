Aphthous ulcers 

Rounded ovoid mouth ulcers with clear margin, floor of slough & eryhtematous periphery 
Most heal within 14d
If >3wks refer urgently to maxfax

Avoid ppts - smoking, trauma, hot drinks, stress, chocolate/cheese/nuts 
Short course of HC lozenges, topical analgesia or antimicrobial mouthwash

Do not usually need any Ix but may need FBC, ESR, haematinics if suspicion

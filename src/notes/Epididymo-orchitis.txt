UTI vs STI
Consider torsion 
Remember - hydrocele anterior + below testicle, transilluminates, can get above it (unlike hernia), vs  epididymal cyst posterior & separate to testicle 

Urgent USS, maybe same day if ?torsion

If UTI more likely check Urine dip/MC&S
• oral quinolone 10-14d (levofloxacin, ofloxacin), coamox alternatively, inform of rare irreversible tendinopathy and to stop if getting pains 

If STI more likely refer to Umbrella for STI NAATs, partner notification and contact tracing 
• Any STI - Cef 1g IM, doxy 100mg BD 10-14d
• Non-gonococcal (chlam) - Doxy or quinolone
• Unsure STI or UTI - Cef + quinolone 

Safety net - if not improving 3d return, review after 2 weeks as most should have resolved otherwise ensure compliance/USS/urology referral 

If child most likely UTI so check urine and seek advice from paeds/paeds urology re ABx, USS etc

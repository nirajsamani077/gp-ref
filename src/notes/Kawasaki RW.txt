Kawasaki RW

Rare medium vessel in children with risk of coronary artery aneurysms (CAA)
• High prolonged fever >5d, resistant to para/nsaid
• Red things - conjunctival injection, red cracked lips, swollen red palms/soles which peel/desquaminate, strawberry tongue, cervical LN

If suspecting refer urgently to paeds  
• High dose aspirin, steroids, IVIG, echo surveillance - high risk of atypical ACS LT.

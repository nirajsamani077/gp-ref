Blood-stained nappy RW

Well or unwell (abdo pain, bilious vomiting, distension, dehydration, fever), usually bright on wiping & not mixed in with stool 
• Well = Usually constipation +/- anal fissure 
• Unwell = Intussusception, Meckels diverticulum (can have massive bleed), NEC/infection, severe oesophagitis (dark/black)
• Chronic = CMPA, rectal polyp, bleeding diathesis 

Intussuception - RUQ sausage shaped lump & redcurrant jelly stool systemically unwell = refer immediately 

Examine for palpable stool, obvious anal fissure/severe nappy rash if less concerned for this & Rx constipation aggressively (Movicol disimpaction, lidocaine topical gel for fissure)

If chronic consider CMPA esp if recent change in food or skin symptoms with atopy Hx, trial exclusion diet & refer if persistent to paeds

Respiratory examination 

Position 
• 45deg chest exposed 

End of bed 
• Age, obesity 
• Resp distress
• Comps of treatment (steroid signs) 
• Chest wall (deformities, scars, DXT tattoo)
• Clues around bed (O2, inhalers, aids, nebs)

Nails 
• Clubbing - perch down 90deg to nail
• Cyanosis 
• Tar staining/yellow nails 

Hands 
• Wasting of small muscles (Pancoast)
• Rheum signs (RA, scleroderma)
• HPOA
• Palmar erythema
• CO2 retention flap with hands outstretched 

Pulse and RR at same time (bounding in CO2) 15s

Face 
• General - Cushingoid from steroids, facial plethora (polycythemia, SVC obstruction), signs of rheum diseases (scleroderma = microstomia/ telangectasia, dermatomyositis = heliotrope eyelid rash, SLE butterfly rash, lupus pernio on nose)
• Eyes - pallor/suffusion of conjunctiva, Horners
• Mouth - cyanosis, candidiasis (from steroid)

Neck 
• Large collar size = risk of OSA
• JVP with HJ reflex - Raised - cor pulmonale, fixed raised - SVC obstruction
• LN - submental to supraclav, if present offer to examine axillary LN

Chest inspection 
• Deformities (pectus excavatum/carinatum, barrel chest /hyperexpanded, kyphoscoliosis)
• Scars/DXT marks (anterior, posterior) 
• Equal breath movements

Chest palpation
• Trachea - uncomfortable, middle with either side, deviated? + cricosternal distance <3 fingers = hyperinflation
• Apex + left parasternal/RV heave (in cor pulmonale) 
• Expansion upper & lower chest 
• Tactile vocal fremitus - 99 with ulnar hand border across ribs upper mid and lower for feeling loss 

Chest percussion all zones anterior and posterior 
• Resonant, dull, stony dull, hyperesonant

Chest auscultation all zones anterior and posterior 
• BS - Vesicular, reduced, bronchial breathing 
• Added sounds (exp/insp) - wheeze (mono or polyphonic), creps (fine or coarse, early or late, cough clearing), pleural rub (creaking leathery sound)
• Prolonged expiratory phase (usually I:E = 1:2, if 1:3 or more sign of obstruction eg COPD)
• Vocal resonance - 99, increased over consolidation/fibrosis/reduced over air/collapse/effusion (whispering pectoriloqurey 99 whisper enhanced if increased) 

Ankles - for pedal oedema in cor pulmonale 

To complete examination 
- O2 saturations, vitals 
- PEFR/bedside Spirometry 
- Sputum, CXR if available

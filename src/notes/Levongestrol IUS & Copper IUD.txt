Levongestrol IUS & Copper IUD

Both are 99% effective, IUD causes direct death of sperm, IUS stops endometrial proliferation and thickens cervical mucus. 

IUD effective immediately but IUS after 7 days 
IUD usually up to 10 years, IUS up to 5 years 

IUD worsen bleeding, IUS generally improve after period of worsening

Risks
- uterine perf 1/500
- expulsion 1/20, most in first 3 months 
- PID, most in early stages 
- ectopic pregnancy, but less pregnancy so higher proportion but lower overall 

Mirena IUS is the traditional 52mg LNG, stays in for 5 years, and is effective for HMB/amenorrhoea however 2 other newer:
- Kyleena: smallest frame/insertion tube, less LNG 19.5mg, 5 years 
- Jaydess: slightly bigger than Kyleena but smaller than Mirena, has least LNG 13.5mg therefore slightly lower amenorrhoea/control of bleeding but less side effects. 3 years

If being used with HRT then only Mirena, licensed for 4 rather than 5 years. If not being used as HRT if inserted >45 can stay in for up to 10 years 


Copper IUD can have copper in arms (T-safe380/TT 380 slimline) or in stem (Nova-T380), are slightly better than LNG IUS

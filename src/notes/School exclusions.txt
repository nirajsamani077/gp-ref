School exclusions 

Rash-related 
• Chickenpox = all lesions crusted over/5d after rash started 
• Impetigo = all lesions crusted over 
• MMR = 4-5d after rash started (or mumps swollen glands) 

ABx-related 
• Scarlet fever = 24h after Abx started 
• Impetigo = 48h after Abx started 
• Whooping cough = 48h (or 21d if no ABx)
• Scabies = until treated 

Rest (HFM, slapped cheek, glandular fever, conjunctivitis, head lice, threadworm, roseola) has NO EXCLUSION

Hypercalcaemia algorithm RW

Symptoms = bones stones moans groans, nephrogenic DI symptoms (thirst polyuria GI upset) 

90% primary HPT or bony mets /myeloma 

If corrected Ca >3 usually admission for IVI 
If corrected Ca 2.6 - 2.9 with sx admission if not 
• Repeat with PTH (consider TFT, myeloma screen)
 
Primary HPT if PTH raised or in normal range (can even be PTH below mid ref range with raised Ca) = Refer 

If PTH low suggests bony mets /myeloma or other rarer causes (hyperthyroid, sarcoid, vit D tox, TZD) = Investigate and 2WW

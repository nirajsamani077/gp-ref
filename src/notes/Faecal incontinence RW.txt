Faecal incontinence RW

Risk groups = constipated, frail elderly, post pelvic surgery/partum/DXT with tear/prolapse, neuro/spinal injury/LD

Can be urgency related or passive (no warning)
Drugs can worsen (nitrites, CCB, loperamide, Abx)
Examine abdo PR and Neuro

Management 
• Avoid triggers - large meal = gastrocolic reflex, avoid prunes pulses caffeine etoh sweetners
• Pads - DN/continence nurse, Bowel & Bladder UK, can also recommend PFE & Biofeedback 
• Rx constipation & impaction aggressively, for urge may need drugs that slow bowel transit (loperamide/codeine)
• Refer (urgent if ?secondary cause, routine if failed above) - can consider bulking procedure, sacral nerve stimulation or diversion stoma!

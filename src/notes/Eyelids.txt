Eyelids 

Stye (hordeolum externum) - sebaceous gland infection of external lid, hot compress

Chalazion (hordeolum internum) - Meibonian tear gland infection of internal lid, leaves firm painless retention cyst, hot compress but some may need surgical drainage 

Blepharitis - inflamed lid margins, red eye crusts, clean lids & hot compress 

Entropion/extropion

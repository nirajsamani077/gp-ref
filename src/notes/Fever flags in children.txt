Fever flags in children 

Red - mottled/cyanosed, appears ill, poor response to social cues, weak high-pitched cry, grunting, recessions/tug, RR>60, reduced skin turgor, <3mo with fever >38, meningism signs (non blanching petechial/purpuric rash, neck stiffness, bulging fontanelle, seizure)

Important ambers - non WB limb, not using extremity, bruise in non mobile infant (safeguarding), 3-6mo with fever >39, HR >160 (<1y)/ >150 (<2y)/>140 (<5y), RR >50 (<1y)/>40 (>1y), poor feeding/moderate dehydration signs (CRT, UOP, dry MM)

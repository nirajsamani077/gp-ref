Prediabetes RW

HbA1C 42-47 - 1 result is sufficient (non diabetic hyperglycaemia) - should be checked annually 

Lifestyle lifestyle lifestyle - fully reversible with improvements & prevention of T2DM & all the various comps (CVD, kidney, blindness, legs, dementia, cancer)
• Explain insulin resistance, fatty liver/pancreas 
• Refer diabetes prevention programme “Healthier You” which run over 9mo talking about diet, exercise, weight loss, stress sleep etc 
• Look at other CVD RFs - lipids, BP, FHx, QRISK 
• Possible role for metformin /orlistat in specific 
   - BMI >35, especially younger group <60
   - Prior history of GDM 
   - Rising HbA1C closer to diabetic end 
   - Lifestyle intervention failed after 6-12mo 
If Px metformin - titrate in usual way to 1.5-2g & repeat HbA1C in 3mo, monitor again 3mo and if no improvement stop 
   - Orlistat can be used if BMI >28 and HbA1C >42 after 3mo lifestyle intervention - aim to lose 5% body weight but weight loss may be slower, review at 12mo if not improving/tolerating stop.

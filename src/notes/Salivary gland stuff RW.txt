Salivary gland stuff RW

3 main causes of swellings
• Infection (sialadenitis) - bacterial or MUMPS usually parotids, ABx hydration and sour sweets 
• Stone (sialolithiasis) - usually submandibular and painful, USS, usually resolves can use balloon ductoplasty or lithotripsy  
• Tumour benign malignant (rare) or general enlargment (anticholinergics DM acromegaly) - red flags for 2WW = CN7 weakness, pain, rapid size tethering Hx of DXT  

Halitosis = common but hard to Rx consider 
- Orodental disease eg food impaction, gum, saliva (xerostomia), dry socket post extraction, ulcers/malignancy 
- Drugs eg ETOH tobacco betel nitrates cytotoxics
- Systemic (almost any) 
Rx oral hygeine teeth tongue cleaning mouth washes and stop trigger if found

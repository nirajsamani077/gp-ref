Like back pain 3 Ns
• Nasty red flag, neuro & non specific musculoligamentous 

Nasty = malignancy, progressive myelopathy, fever, fragility fracture (osteoporosis, steroids)

Neuro = cervical radiculopathy/spondylosis with OA or prolapsed disc impinging nerve roots like sciatica LMN sensory but myelopathy (DCM) compressive can cause UMN and LMN + sensory of arms and legs = refer urgent CES like 

Non-specific = most, no red flags or neuro, myofascial tenderness, referred pain, fibromyalgia
• Most tend to resolce within 3mo, biopsychosocial, get moving early, exercises physio NSAIDs topical ideally

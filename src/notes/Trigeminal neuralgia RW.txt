Trigeminal neuralgia RW

UL episodic sharp/electric shock facial pain 
• Trigeminal nerve distribution only 
• Paroxysms 
• Triggered by innocuous stimuli (touch)

Most are classical (ppx ?vascular compression of CN5/idiopathic), small proportion secondary to 
• MS (20x more likely) or CPA tumour (2WW)
• Usually 50-60yo 

DDx red flags = SOL in young <50, GCA in old >50
• BL, ophthalmic only, sensory loss or hearing loss also red flags needing urgent referral 

Treatment 
• CBZ
   - 100mg OD/BD initially (max 200mg QDS) 
   - 90% get improved pain control 
   - C/I if cardiac arrhythmia 
   - S/E Neuro dizziness diplopia, skin rashes, leucopenia, low Na, LFTs (check FBC UE LFT 1st)
   - Headache diary & continue, taper if 1mo free
   - Asians may not respond, refer genetics first 
• Refer if red flags, not responding CBZ or MS 

Refer if fails to respond to CBZ or atypical (<50)

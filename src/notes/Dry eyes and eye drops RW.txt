Dry eye disease = keratoconjunctivitis sicca , loss of tear-film which lubricates eyeball. Related to prolonged computer use, AC, ETOH, keeping in contact lenses for too long & if also dry mouth consider CT disease (Sjogrens) & meds (diuretics, antihistamines, SSRIs)

Tear-film has 3 layers  
• Inner mucin layer - sits on skin cells of eye 
• Middle aqueous layer - tears from lacrimal glands (reduced Sjogrens Vs Non-Sjogrens)
• Outer lipid layer - prevents tears from evaporating, from Meibomian glands 

Lubricant drops (artificial tears)
- drops best in day, ointment/gel at night as can blur vision and can cause stinging 
- Hypromellose most common, use every hour til symptoms improve 
- Carmellose 1 drop QDS (5mg/ml mild, 10mg/ml severe) is middle thickness 
- Consider preservative free options separately if using frequent >4x/d, treatment >3mo, ?allergy

Gingivitis 

Inflamed gums due to poor dental hygiene 

Simple - painless red gum swelling with contact bleeding —> routine dental review 

Acute necrotising ulcerative gingivitis - painful spontaneously bleeding gums with punched out ulcers —> urgent dental review, oral metronidazole, choorhexidine mouthwash, analgesia or benzydamine mouthwash for pain

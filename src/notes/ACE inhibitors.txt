ACE inhibitors 

Can cause cough (bradykinin) in 15%, angio-oedema, hyperkalaemia & first dose hypotension (especially with diuretic) 

C/I - pregnancy, BF, AS (hypotension), RAS (AKI), angio-oedema, hyperkalaemia (if borderline K>5 consult renal) 

Check U&E before, yearly & after increasing dose 
- can accept rise Cr <30% or K <5.5 but may cause AKI if undiagnosed bilateral RAS 

AfroCarribbean should be able to tolerate ARB

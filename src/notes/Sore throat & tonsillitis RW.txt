Sore throat & tonsillitis RW

Facts 
• Nearly 90% of sore throat get better after 1w without Rx!!! & to prevent 1 case of quinsy would need to Px >4000 Abx 

Usually viral but FEVER PAIN or Centor (FEVER PAIN probably better) 
• Fever, tender anterior cervical nodes, no cough, exudates on tonsils all score 1 for Centor & if 3 or 4 consider immediate or delayed script ? Group A strep infection (don’t use system in <3yo)
• Usually Pen V 500mg QDS 5-10d (check BNFc), Amox in children with low risk of glandular fever, clary and erythro 500mg BD or QDS 5d .
• Oral dex 8-10mg or 0.6mg/kg can help swelling & pain but not advised generally 
• Difflam fluids analgesia bed rest safety net airway 

Rare comps or mimics 
• Quinsy - trismus, can’t move neck, toxic, drooling, can’t swallow saliva, uvula displaced, airway risk to A&E for I&D by ENT
• Epiglottitis - child toxic stridor sitting forward, airway risk don’t examine 999
• Scarlet fever - GAS with sore throat fever macular blotchy rash torso sandpaper (sunburn with goose pimples) & orofacial (strawberry tongue, flushed face perioral pallor, LN) - Notifiable and give Pen V/Amox 
• Glandular fever - EBV kissing disease with fevers prolonged pharyngitis, ++ cervical LN and splenomegaly, FBC lymphocytosis & Monospot or EBV IgM positive - settles within 1mo bed rest fluids, avoid contact sport /impact due to risk of splenic rupture 
• Pertussis - now v rare due to vaccination, but if unvaccinated with sore throat bull neck LN and grey green membrane take diphtheria swab and notify - Abx + antitoxin 
• Oropharyngeal CA - tonsillitis rare >45, consider in persistent pharyngitis LN earache odynophagia in smoker 

Tonsillectomy only - risk PTB airway 
• >7 last year, >5 in last 2y each, >3 in last 3y each 
• OSA in children, renal disease from GAS, tumour 
• Quinsy

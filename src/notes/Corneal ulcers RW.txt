Corneal ulcers RW 

Consider FB causing abrasion, but otherwise pain gritty photophobia lacrimation - subacute & main RF being CONTACT LENS WEARER - examine   
• VA, lids (Entropion means lashes cause abrasion, Ectropion means exposure keratopathy)
• Fluorescein stain & use blue light 
   - Pick up abrasions/ulcers that strain 
   - Dendritic ulcer suggests herpes keratitis 
   - Small punctate suggests dry eye 
   - Check for FB (better slit lamp) and rust rings 

Management
• Dry eyes = lubricating drops & refer if persists 
• Abrasion = prophylactic chloramphenicol, review at 48h, if vision affected immediate referral 
• Keratitis = bacterial/HSV immediate referral (can also have ulcerative related to rheum & marginal related to blepharitis - all need referring) - usually topical/oral abx or antiviral drops & removing of contact lenses 

Any suspected chemical/metal injury = same day referral

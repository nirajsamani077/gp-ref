2WW Bible RW

Prostate (if WL back pain LUTS haematuria ED)
• Abnornal hard craggy nodular prostate on DRE 
• Raised PSA >age cut offs without other cause 
   - <40y: use clinical judgement
   - 40–49y: >2.5mcg/l
   - 50–59y: >3.5mcg/l
   - 60–69y: >4.5mcg/l
   - 70–79y: >6.5mcg/l
   - >80y: use clinical judgement 

Bladder/renal (urothelial/haematuria)
• Unexplained visible haematuria >45
• Unexplained non-visible haematuria >60 + either dysuria or raised WCC (check FBC) 

Testicular/penile 
• Any unexplained testicular or penile mass /ulcerated lesion (consider STI, direct access USS)

UGI (oesophageal/ gastric)
• Dysphagia 
• Upper abdo mass
• >55 with weight loss + pain or reflux
(Non-urgent any other sx /abnormal FBC Hb Plts)

Pancreatic /HPB 
• New painless jaundice >40 = 2WW 
• >60 with weight loss + any GI symptom/ new DM = 2WW direct access CT abdo
• Upper abdo mass (eg liver edge) UGI 2WW 

Colorectal (changed 2023)
• Abdo/anorectal mass or positive screening FIT = straight 2WW (still do FIT for abdo mass to determine source though) 
• Unexplained symptoms = symptomatic FIT then 2WW if positive (changed 2023 though) 
• >40 weight loss AND abdo pain (or >50 with 1)
• >50 PR bleeding (or <50 with another Sx)
• >60 CIBH OR IDA 
    - Any age CIBH should have FIT, if younger calprotectin, if older CT abdo, if female CA125/USS
    - Any age IDA should have FIT, and if >60 even any anaemia (regardless of ID) should have FIT 

Lung 
• >40 with haemoptysis or CXR suggestive = 2WW 
• >40 & 1 symptom if smoker/asbestos or 2 symptoms if not for Urgent CXR (within 2w) 
 - Resp/gen sx = Cough, SOB, CP, weight loss etc 
 - Resp/gen ex = Recurrent LRTI/signs, LN,clubbing 
 - Abnormal FBC (ie raised plts /thrombocytosis)

Derm (melanoma) 
• ABCD2 or 7 point checklist 
   - Assymetrical
   - Border irregular 
   - Colour inconsistent 
   - Diameter >6mm or Dimensions changing 
  EFG for nodular melanoma/SCC
   - Elevated 
   - Firm 
   - Growth, persistent 
Unless clearly nodular BCC(slow grow, rolled edge)
• 7 point checklist = Major (2) & Minor (1) criteria 
  - Major = change in size, irreg shape, irreg colour 
  - Minor = diam > 7mm,inflamm,ooze, sensation  >3 is suspicious, refer if any major category 

Myeloma 
• Any paraprotein (IgA/M >10 or IgG >15) or abnormal kappa:lambda ratio (<0.1 or >7), ensure CRAB FBC ESR calcium checked & symptoms >60 with persistent bone/back pain or unexplained # to start 2 step process

Leukaemia 
• 3 things - pancytopenia Sx, B Sx, exam LN/HSM = check urgent FBC & film within 48h and admit if blasts or pancytopenia, flow cytometry if isolated lymphocytosis (usually not 2WW but urgent A&G)

Lymphoma 
• Any persistent >6w lymphadenopathy or mass anywhere cervical, axillary, inguinal, abdo - especially with B symptoms (fever night sweats weight loss) =2WW (probably threshold is >3w unexplained)

Ovarian
• Any obvious abdo/pelvic mass or ascites = 2WW
• >50 with persistent or frequent vague symptoms (bloating, early satiety, abdo/pelvic pain, LUTS) or more obvious red flags (weight loss, CIBH) or just new TATT = CA125 & if >35 refer for urgent /direct access USS abdomen & TV USS pelvis 
 - If suspicious scan result = 2WW 
 - If negative = keep looking (FIT, CT abdo, urine)

Endometrial 
• PMB (>1y post LMP, usually >55, not on HRT)
• On HRT with bleeding persisting >6w after stop

Breast (lump >30 or nipple >50)
• Any unexplained breast /skin/axillary lump >30 
• Any UL nipple discharge/changes >50 

HNSCC/thyroid 
• Oropharyngeal (maxfax) - Unexplained lump or red/white patch (erythro/leucoplakia) >3w
• Laryngeal - >45 persistent unexplained neck lump or hoarseness >3w (also do urgent CXR)
• Thyroid - any unexplained thyroid lump 

Vulval /penile 
• Irregular ulcer/bleeding/lump (complex = fungating mass or LNs) 

Testicular 
• Any unexplained testicular lump or persistent pain - using direct access urgent USS as part of assessment (also consider non-resolving epididyo-orchitis or hydocele - so do image and do follow-up/safety net .

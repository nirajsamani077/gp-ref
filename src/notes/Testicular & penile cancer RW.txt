Testicular & penile cancer RW

2WW criteria 
• Any unexplained testicular lump or persistent pain - using direct access urgent USS as part of assessment (also consider non-resolving epididyo-orchitis or hydocele - so do image and do follow-up/safety net ) 

Types = Seminoma & Non-Seminoma Germ cell
• Tumour markers - BHCG, AFP, LDH done secondary care to monitor treatment 

After USS confirms hypoechoic mass, CT TAP to stage & inguinal orchidectomy (with immunohistological testing) for further treatments depending on stage 1-3
• Seminoma - active surveillance Vs radio/chemo 
• NSGC -active surveillance +LN dissection/chemo 

Many are young & many survive although LT risks hypogonadism, infertility, chemo SE (neuro nephro hepatotoxic), risk of 2nd cancer 
 
Orchid - fighting male cancer website (penile testicular prostate)

NB Penile Ca very rare <1/100000, has PIN stage, associated with HPV & lichen sclerosis just like Vulval, presents as persistent ulcerated or abnormal lesion (check for Inguinal LN) & refer 2WW (esp if trial of topical steroid etc for borderline cases & not resolved….needs Bx)
• Rx = Bx, circumcision to allow monitoring, if not completely excised with Bx then choice of 5FU, laser or surgical removal/LN clearance .

6mo-5yrs
3% of children 

After fever/viral infection with brief (<5mins) GTC seizures 
Complex - >15mins (status >30mins), focal, not completely recovered within 1hr, repeat within 24hrs= admit to paeds 

Risk of further febrile seizure -= 1/3, some variables 

If no RF for developing epilepsy, risk of getting epilepsy = 3% but 3 major RF (complex seizure, FHX epilepsy or neurodevelopmental disorder) increase risk to 50%

If repeats, teach how to use rectal diazepam/buccal midazolam, recovery position, call ambulance if >5mins

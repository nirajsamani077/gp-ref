Hiatus hernia RW

Common, stomach into chest cavity (mediastinum) through diaphragm found on OGD, esp if obese 
• Sliding (most), rolling (paraoesophageal defect) 
• Most AS but can cause GORD & rolling can cause mechanical sx/obstruction/ strangulation risk 
• Long term min dose/PRN PPI & if ongoing sx refer for fundoplication (rolling refer even if AS due to risks above)

ADHD RW

Inattention - give examples 
• Hard organising things, forgetful, can’t follow/complete tasks, careless mistakes 
• Hyperactivity /impulsivity - give examples
• Figety, talks a lot, on the go always, interrupts 
Persistent Pervasive & Pactful multiple settings 

Most of time pt will ask for assessment but consider if impact work relationship school, anger issues, substance issues, self esteem issues, linking with MH 

Often sx start in childhood, approx 5% prevalence children and just under 5% adults, genetic and environmental factors poorly understood but is a well defined valid diagnosis with safe treatment that improves health and life outcomes

In children R/O hearing vision sleep MH problems although in adults MH may be comorbid 
• Get info from many sources schools (some can refer), parents, clubs 
• If classes mild-mod impairment can 10w watchful waiting with ADHD focused parent support (advice regarding planning days, reward systems, positive attention, clear instructions)
• If classes severe impairment then refer 

In adults as above, were sx first noticed <12yo?, 
• Complete ASRS (adult self report scale) - score >=4 is sensitive so refer if wants formal Dx
• Provide self care advice for all (CANDDID ADHD videos), CBT also helpful, Young Minds and ADDISS services 

Secondary care will make Dx and start treatment 
• If asking private ensure is a validated diagnosis using NICE approved guidance and formal shared care agreement for medications
• New QbTest to help with Dx (computer tasks whilst wearing headband with reflective ball) 

Meds (methylphenidate/Ritalin, dexamphetamine)
• Only if >5yo, OD slow release 
• Monitoring - height weight HR BP 6 monthly 
• ECG only if cardiac concerns 
• S/E sleep issues appetite behaviour tics psychosis /seizures = rare stop!

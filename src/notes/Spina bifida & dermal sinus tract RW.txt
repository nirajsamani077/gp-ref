Spina bifida & dermal sinus tract RW 

On 6-8w baby check look for dermal sinus tract - deep pit above gluteal cleft with associated with skin/hair change which may indicate spina bifida 
• Sacral dimple with base not visible 
• DDx = coccygeal dimple within gluteal cleft with base visible 
• Incomplete closure of midline structures, NTD
• Comps - progressive LL neuro, hydrocephalus 

If dermal sinus tract/dimple with no base = refer urgent paeds review for USS, MRI & excision to prevent neuro deficit 

Long term spina bifida /NTD
• Main consequence = hydrocephalus (treat with VP shunt, may have comps eg shunt failure cog impairment bladder bowel sexual dysfunction)
• Other = foot deformity/talipes, contractures, scoliosis

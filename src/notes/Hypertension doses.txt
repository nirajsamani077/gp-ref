Hypertension doses 

ACE - start low dose, check U&E within 2 weeks, then annual or 2 weeks within any change, eve as 1st dose hypo, BP 1mo after each change 
• Ramipril 1.25mg, up to 10mg
• Lisinopril/Enalapril 2.5mg, up to 20mg
• Perindopril 2.5mg, up to 10mg
(Most start 2.5 and end at 10) 

ARB - same advice as ACE
• Losartan 25mg, up to 100mg 
• Irbesartan 75mg, up to 300mg
• Candesartan 4mg, up to 32mg 
(Losartan convert 25 end at 100, irb is x3, cand 4) 

Thresholds for eGFR drop 25%, or Cr rise 30% 

CCB DHP (non rate limiting) 
• Amlodipine 5-10mg, Alts felodipine same but up to 20mg, lercanidipine 10 to 20, lacidipine 2 to 6
Start low dose, titrate monthly with repeat BP

CCB non-DHP (rate limiting)
• Diltazem MR OD - differ based on preparation 
• Verapamil MR OD - 120-240mg (max 480mg)
Start low dose, titrate monthly with repeat HR

TZD
• Indapamide 2.5mg (or 1.5mg MR) preferred 
• Metolazone 5mg or chlortalidone 25-50mg
If already bendroflumethiazide (2.5-10mg) cont
Monitor U&E, LFT/bone prior and regular intervals, esp in elderly and cirrhosis (low Na/K common) 

Spironolactone - 4th line (already on 3) if K <4.5
• Start low dose 25mg, only titrate up in HF
• Check U&E prior, 1mo and regular after, stop K>5

Beta blockers (Bb) - increase/withdraw dose 2 wky
• HTN + HF - Bisoprolol 5mg up to 20mg 
• HTN + angina - Atenolol 25-50mg up to 100mg
• HTN + prev MI - Metoprolol IR 100mg up to 400mg and MR 200mg OD, Propanolol 80mg BD up to 160mg BD 
C/I severe PVD, asthma/COPD, bradycardia/block 
Caut DM, psoriasis, MG, 1st deg AVB

Alpha blockers - 1st dose hypotension 
• Doxazosin - 1-4mg (max 16mg), MR 4-8mg (usual maintenance dose for both is 4mg)

Parkinson’s (PD) RW

Neurodegenerative, loss of dopaminergic neurons causing movement disorder, autonomic dysfunction & dementia (cognitive, mood)

Classically triad 
• Resting tremor - UL, progresses to BL
• Bradykinesia - shuffling gait, mask face, speech 
• Rigidity - cog-wheeling 
Other associated affects = 
• ANS dysfunction - Postural drop/instability, falls, hypersalivation, bladder/bowel 
• Sleep dysfunction - REM disorder, akinesia, daytime somnolence 
• Cog dysfunction -Dementia, mood, psychosis 

Any suspected PD refer urgently to neuro for Dx
• Clinical Dx (UK PD society Brain Bank criteria)
• May use SPECT to distinguish PD & BET tremor 

DDx Tremor (Resting = PD) 
• Intention = Cerebellar 
• Postural/action = Benign essential tremor (BET), endocrine (thyroid, phaeo), SABA, physiological 

DDx Parkinsonism (ie PD signs but not PD)
• Parkinson’s plus - MSA, CBD, PSP
• Other dementias - Lewy Body, AD, Vascular
• Drug-induced - Antipsychotics, phenothiazines

Management = MDT, PT OT SALT dietician & PD specialist nurse, advanced planning & motor drugs (all specialist initiated & managed) 

If motor sx not affecting QOL (Early PD) = Dopamine agonist
• Non ergot derived e.g ropinorole/apomorphine causes less lung/cardiac/RP fibrosis than ergot derived (cabergoline, bromocripine)
• ECR, CXR, bloods (UE ESR) prior to starting ergot 
• S/E = impulse control disorder, hallucinations ++, day somnolence, nasal congestion, hypotension
• Consider need for L-dopa as best Rx for motor sx but highest risk of motor comps & wears off in time 

If motor sx affecting QOL = Levodopa + dopa decarboxylase inhibitor (carbidopa, benserazide) which stops peripheral destruction of L-dopa - has best improvement of motor but most comps 
• Dose wearing off - sx worse just before next dose, can be severe acute dystonia, “On-off” variations, Dyskinesia (Xs movement) @ peak dose 
• If oral route lost don’t stop suddenly - risk of acute severe dystonia, use a ropinorole patch 
• ANS S/E - dry mouth, palpitations, postural hypotension, psychosis 

If develops worsening sx/dyskinesia whilst on optimal levodopa = Add adjuncts ABCD
• Amantidine (unclear mechanism)
• MAO-B inhibitor (selegiline, stops breakdown)
• COMT inhibitor (capones, stops breakdown) 
• Dopamine agonist 

Other treatments 
- Antimuscarinics (procyclidine) for dyskinesias  
- Glycopyronium for drooling 
- Modafanil for daytime somnolence 
- Midodrine for postural hypotension 
- Rivastigmine for PD dementia
- Screen for depression & impulse control disorder on dopamine agonists

Phaeochromocytoma RW

Rare, benign or malignant, adrenaline secreting adrenal tumour (paragangliona extra-adrenal)

Triad of headache, palpitations & sweating ++
HTN with paroxysms &panic attacks 
Associated with NF1, MEN2, VHL

Check urine or plasma metanephrines & refer endocrine if suspected (surgical removal, high risk intraoperative HTN crisis, usually annual metanephrine monitoring post op)

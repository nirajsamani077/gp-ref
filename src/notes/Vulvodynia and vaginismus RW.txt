Vulvodynia and vaginismus RW

Vulval pain >3mo - usually no obvious cause 
Multi factorial but relates to sensory /psychological 
DDx - pudendal neuralgia causes perineal pain worse when sitting and PV fullness 
Provoked = discomfort only when touching 

Consider topical lubricants/steroids/oestrogens (if UL cause), or topical LA (2% lidocaine gel), drug options for non-provoked not licensed but include gabapentinoids 

For provoked vulvodynia - first line = physio PFMT and CBT

Surgical options (injections or vestibulectomy) last resort 

Vaginismus = sexual pain , difficulty on any entry, sometimes linked to underlying pathology or psych trauma (sexual abuse) . Must examine v carefully smallest possible speculum, and refer. CBT and desensitisation with vaginal trainers (rods of increasing size with lubricant) can help

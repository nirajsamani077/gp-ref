Endometriosis RW

Common with cyclical /chronic pelvic pain, painful periods, painful intercourse, cyclical GI/GU and infertility - affects 10% WCBA. Easily missed & debilitating 

Womb lining deposits outside of the womb causing pain and dysfunction - oestrogen dependent 
• Superficial peritoneal (80%)
• Ovarian (endometrioma/choc cysts)
• Deep endometriosis 
Genetic factors and association with chronic health 

Major cause of infertility (2x above average, and at least 1/3 women in infertility clinic Dx with it)

Normal PV exam (can see visible tender lesions) and TV USS (endometrioma, deep lesions) does not R/O = refer for MRI pelvis and diagnostic laparoscopy where can treat also (diathermy) - although nearly half of laparoscopy’s find nothing 

Primary care treatment (before referral)
• Biopsychosocial, and contraception/fertility plan (refer if trying to conceive) 
• Non hormonal = NSAIDs
• Hormonal (ovarian suppression) = COCP continuous, POP (diongest 2mg is newer mainly secondary care, desogestrel, MPA /provera 10mg TDS or NET 5mg TDS VTE risk), any LARC including Mirena but not IUD as no hormones!

Secondary care treatment 
• GNRH analogue injections (prostap for women/zoladex for men…) 3 monthly to suppress ovulation, add back COCP or combined HRT for menopausal sx and bone protection. 
• Laparoscopy (diagnostic, therapeutic ablation adhesiolysis, excise lesions, ovarian cystectomy) often debulk after MRI with prostap 
• May end up with hysterectomy if severe with adenomyosis (endometriosis in myometrium causing heavy bleeding) - remember don’t give oestrogen only HRT as will bring back endometriosis 

Signpost to Endometriosis UK and RCOG PIL

Dyspaerunia other causes 
• Superficial - Candida, HSV, dermatitis/lichen sclerosis), atrophy (preg/BF or PM due to low oestrogen), vulvodynia/vaginismus, FGM
• Deep - Endo, PID, fibroid/polyps/CA, ovarian cysts/CA, prolapse, non-gynae, pelvic congestion 
• Can try coconut oil BD, avoid irritants, topical lubricants /steroid /oestrogens, psychosexual

RE: HRT - Endo usually improved after menopause as hormone driven, but HRT theoretically may bring back residual Endo but no data for this, usually women benefit, if TAH BSO then still combined HRT due to theoretical risk of malignant residual endo elsewhere

Eosinophilia RW

Mild = >0.5, Significant >1.5, Severe >5 

Causes 
• Allergy /atopy 
• Resp - asthma/ ABPA, eosinophilic pneumonia 
• Infection - parasitic, HIV 
• Gastro - IBD, eosinophilic oesophagitis
• Systemic - CT disease, malignancy, CML, DRESS
• Many drug causes eg AEDs ABX NSAIDs 
• Idiopathic hypereosinophilia 

Check - Film UE LFT/bone ESR haematinics LDH 
End organ - CXR, spiro, echo, VTE - if evidence of this or severe refer urgently to haem .

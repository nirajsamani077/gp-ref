Lipids and statins RW

If CVD - atorva 80 for secondary prevention. 
If CKD - atorva 20 to all as primary and secondary 
If T1DM >40, >10y, renal or CV RFs - atorva 20
If >85 - consider, do not QRISK

Every one else >25 with raised cholesterol QRISK
• If >10% offer statin and discuss lifestyle 
• If <10% discuss lifestyle and consider statin 

Lifestyle - obvious stuff but cardioprotective diet reducing processed food sugary sweets saturated fats but focus on healthy fats in oily fish veg nuts seeds. Exercise 2.5h moderate or 1.25h intense /w. Stop smoking and reduce ETOH avoiding binges 

Before statin ensure LFT normal 
Lipids LFT 3mo and 12mo
40% reduction non HDL cholesterol 
Don’t need to recheck LFT again after 1y
• Only withhold if ALT >3x ULN 
Counsel most tolerate, few myalgias, rare risk rhabdo & deranged LFTs 

If statin muscle intolerance 
• Avoid labelling as intolerant as statins the best treatment, so can dechallenge rechallenge after symptoms settled (checking CK & U&E first) 
• Can try lower dose or change to rosuva which may be more muscle friendly and both have long half life so can be use alt daily, can even use lower dose statin with ezetimibe or bempedoic acid (usually ezetimibe + bempedoic acid if can’t actually tolerate statin) - refer lipid clinic for PCSK9 inhibitor (evolucumab, inclisiran) if couldn’t tolerate 3x statin
• CK <4x ULN = continue if sx tolerable, if >10x ULN or sx not tolerable stop 4-6w then rechallenge as long as CK normalises but if renal Fx poor then refer ?rhabdo 
• Highest risk of myalgias older female frail etoh

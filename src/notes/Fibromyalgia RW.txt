Fibromyalgia RW

A chronic pain syndrome - often when we’ve excluded other things, causes generalised pains fatigue, often hard to treat, absence of things like arthritis or abnormal blood tests/X-rays 

Quite poorly understood - some think may be to do with sensitivity to pain of the central or peripheral nerves whereby a normal issue that wouldn’t usually cause pain may do in this case (almost like an allergy), sometimes linked to underlying psychological stress 

Not FND - FND not painful (but neuro sx)
Not somatoform disorder - focus on distress related to symptoms 
Overlap with CFS/ME (fatigue sleepiness more)
Consider hyper mobility (ethers danlos, Beighton)

Main DDx to R/O is RA - inflamm markers normal in 60% of early RA (so check RF CCP!) - but inflamed joints

Approach 
1. Suspect - >3mo generalised pain fatigue hypersensitivity overwhelmed nothing working 
2. R/O RA, hypothyroid , chronic illness, depression 
3. Confirm with RCP fibromyalgia diagnostic worksheet which uses symptom severity index (SSI) and widespread pain index (WPI)
https://www.rcplondon.ac.uk/file/36231/download
• WPI >7 & SSI >5 or WPI >6 & SSI >9 for Dx 
• Refer rheum only if clinical uncertainty 

Management same for chronic pain 

PAIN
• Physical activity/Psychological (CBT ACT)- Acceptance & Commitment Therapy 
• Acupuncture & Antidepressants (SSRI!)
• Involve pain team /integrated pain team 
• No para NSAIDs opiates gabas tens

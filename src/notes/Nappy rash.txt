Nappy rash 

Irritant dermatitis - most common, spared creases
Fungal (Candida) dermatitis - satellite lesions, beefy red, in creases, confluent papules
Bacterial - exudate, pus, cellulitis 
Seborrhoeic dermatitis - flakes, scalp rash too 
Eczema - other areas affected 
Psoriasis - scaly, other areas affected

1. Self care - Use disposable nappies, allow air exposure (keeping nappy off for as long as possible), clean using water, bathe once a day,  change nappy 3-4hrly to avoid irritants staying 

2. Barrier cream (zinc and castor oil ointment, sudocrem, metanium) - at each nappy change,

3. 1% HC steroid cream if more severe causing irritation, max 7 days - apply few mins before barrier

4. If ?fungal - topical imidazole (clotrimazole, miconazole) BD 10 days + stop barrier cream until better, and consider swabbing 

5. Oral Abx - fluclox 7 days, or clary/erythro

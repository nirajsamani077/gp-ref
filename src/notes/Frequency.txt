Frequency 

Top causes
• UTI (infective cystitis)
• Bladder/ureteric stone (better when prone) 
• OAB (storage) or interstitial cystitis in women (pregnancy/dementia causes an OAB type state)
• LUTS (obstructive/voiding) due to BPH /prostatitis in men 
• Anxiety/ “habit frequency”
• Rare (pelvic /bladder/ovarian extrinsic tumour) 

5 ready reckoners 
• Dysuria?
• Haematuria?
• Nocturnal frequency?
• Poor flow (obstructive)
• Abnormal MSU 

History 
• Dysuria/haematuria (more cystitis/stone/tumour)
• Urgency/incontinence/nocturia (OAB)
• LUTS with straining, poor stream, incomplete emptying, terminal dribble, nocturia (BPH/prostate)
• ETOH/caffeine/other triggers 

Examine /investigate 
• Urine dip/MSU + urine frequency volume chart 
• Abdo + PR in men 
• Consider PV in Pm women ?atrophic vaginitis 
• Consider CA125 if unexplained in older women

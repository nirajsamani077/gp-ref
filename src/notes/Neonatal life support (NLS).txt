Neonatal life support (NLS)

Resuscitaire - turn on light, fio2 blender on air, check gases (pressure 30/4 term, 20/4 preterm) and suction, dry towels, stethoscope

Start timer

?Delayed cord clamping 1min (esp preterm, not crying etc) 

Dry stimulate warm 

Colour - pale? blue? pink?
Tone - floppy? normal?
Breathing/chest movement- not breathing? spont?
HR - severe brady <60 (brady <100) or absent? 
— check with steth, tap out rate, attach leads 

Neonatal emergency 2222 if all bad, Hr <60
Get a scribe 

Neutral head
5 inflation breaths with BVM - 2-3s gin & tonic or 123, ensure right mask size, look for chest rise 

If no chest rise airway issue, 2 person technique, suction airway, jaw thrust & 5 more inflation breaths 

Recheck colour, tone, breathing & HEART RATE, if not >100 move to next to…
30s ventilation breaths - shorter 1s than inflation breaths 

Reassess as above, if HEART RATE still <100…
Chest compression (encircling technique) 3:1 inflation breath with 2 people for 30s then reassess

Should really get intubation/LMA & vascular access via umbilical vein or IO

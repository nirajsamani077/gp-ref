SGA /IUGR RW

SGA fetus - EFW <10th centile on mid T scan 
SGA baby - BW <10th centile 

May be constitutionally small or medical cause with RF for this 
• Existing condition (HTN, CKD, SLE, smoking)
• Previous SGA baby, PET, IUD
• Low PAPP-A (combined test) & echogenic bowel 

Management = serial growth scans 28, 32, 36/40
Aspirin 75-150mg & assess for PET
Dopplers can determine management 
If SFH <10th or static growth in MW clinic refer .

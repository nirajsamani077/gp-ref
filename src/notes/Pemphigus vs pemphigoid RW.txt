Pemphigus vs pemphigoid RW 

If suspect either = immediate derm referral & discussion same day 

Bullous pemphigoid 
- Tense blisters, may be itchy, no MM involvement 
- Usually older 80s, may be med related (NSAID)
- Dx = Bx & direct immunofluoresence 
- Rx = Stop Abx, pred (if confined potent topical steroid), may need DMARDs

Pemphigoid vulgaris
- Flaccid blisters easily rupture,MM involved
- Blisters can separate after applying mild lateral pressure (Nikolskys), similar to TEN 
- Rx = immediate steroids & immunosuppression.

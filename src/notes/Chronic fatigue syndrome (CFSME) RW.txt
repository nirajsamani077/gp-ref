Chronic fatigue syndrome (CFS/ME) RW 

Consider if sx in 4 Sx clusters >4-6w (child-adult) 
• Fatigue ++ (worse with activity, rest doesn’t help)
• Post exertional malaise (disproportionate, long) 
• Unrefreshing sleep (constant flu like stiffness)
• Brain fog (slow responses, hard concentrating)
Severity graded on degree of functional impact (in work/education, independence, ADLs, leave home)

Refer if sx persistent (defined >3mo, but refer children >4w adults >6w) to ME/CFS service for Dx 
• Full “TATT” bloods inc TFT, haematinics, inflamm markers, Coeliac screen, CK, Vit D, viral serology based on clinical judgement before referral 
• Important to refer Gen paeds if sx >4w (who can refer on to ME/CFS) once other causes R/O 

Management (no cure) 
• Energy management plan (patient led), rest time, sleep hygeine, relaxation techniques 
• Graded exercise programme with PT
• CBT psychological support 
• No medications can help, can review other meds
• Annual/regular review in primary care

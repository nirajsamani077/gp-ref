OME glue ear RW

Common middle ear fluid build up post infection in children (in adults consider post nasal space Ca)

Retracted TM with fluid level due to neg pressure 

Reduced hearing speech language behaviour most common, issues at school etc 

Watchful waiting 3mo as most resolve, with education and Otovent autoinflation devices (blowing a balloon using nose) which allows equalise middle ear pressure via ET (use for ETD also) - have to be old enough, can Px or buy for £7 

If after 3mo watchful waiting ongoing issues arrange audiometry - if HL 25-30db or <25db with significant impact refer for grommet (ventilation tube) or hearing aid 

New treatment ET dilation under LA can help with ETD which is similar aetiology

Morton’s neuroma

Intermetatarsal benign neuroma, most commonly 3rd space, causing pain worse on walking or like having a pebble in shoe.

Pain worse on metatarsal squeeze & Mulder’s click when one hand holds neuroma between finger and thumb, other metatarsal squeeze, click when neuroma moves between metatarsal heads

Clinical dx, USS can help if unsure 

Conservative management for 3 months with footwear change, metatarsal pads, avoid high heels, orthotics (metatarsal dome) 

If persistent then refer for steroid injection or neurectomy

Freiberg occurs in younger, Morton’s in older & interrupted BS to MT head as cannot keep up with bone growth - tall athletic females

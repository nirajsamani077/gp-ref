Vaginal thrush RW

Common easy diagnosed and treatment , overdiagnosed vs BV & STI
Other vulval conditions (ezcema, lichen sclerosis, STI and even malignancy can mimic)

20% young women colonised, don’t treat if no sx
80% thrush caused by Candida (others azole resistant)

HVS or self taken LVS

Isolated episode:
• Self care - avoid irritants /douching, use soap sub
• First line = single dose fluconazole 150mg + single dose Canesten pessary 500mg repeat after 4d if severe (topical canestan cream can be used but can irritate, can also affect condoms)
• Lots of alternatives (200mg pessary 3d or itraconazole (sporanox) 200mg Bd 1d)
• Cannot use oral antifungals pregnancy or BF
• If severe repeat the first line again on day 4 
• If not improving refer GUM

Recurrent vaginal candidiasis (>4 eps per year)
•RF DM immunsupp oestrogenic state 
•should have at least 2 lab confirmed 
•single dose fluconazole 150mg every 3d x3 and then once weekly for 6mo (check LFT prior) 

In pregnancy/BF tend to go for up to 7 days Canesten 500mg pessary or topical

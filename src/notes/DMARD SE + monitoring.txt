DMARD S/E + monitoring 

MTX - pneumonitis, liver cirrhosis, BM suppression/mucositis (prevented by folic acid). Monitor FBC/UE/LFT 2-3 monthly once stabilised (1-2 weekly initially), stop >6mo before conceiving, starting dose 7.5mg/week, avoid sulfa drugs & aspirin 

Sulfasalazine - ILD, oligospermia, Heinz body anaemia (like G6PDH)

Leflunomide - ILD, HTN, deranged LFT, like thalidomide ++teratogenic with long half life so need to be stopped >2y prior to woman conceiving (>3mo for men) with monitoring. Monitor FBC/LFT 2 weekly for 6mo then 2monthly & BP 

Hydroxychlorquinine - Eye stuff retinopathy, corneal deposits 

Gold - Proteinuria 

Penicillamine - Proteinuria, MG exacerbation 

Biologicals - most reactivate latent TB, rituximab has infusion reaction, etanercept demyelination

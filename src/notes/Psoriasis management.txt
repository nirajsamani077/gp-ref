Psoriasis management 

Plaque psoriasis:

Regular emollient 
1st line - potent topical steroid + vit D analogue (calcipotriol) 4w, apply OD one in morning one in evening, can use combo (dovobet)
2nd line - vit D analogue BD 4w
3rd line - potent topical steroid BD or coal tar preparation (dna synthesis) OD/BD 4w
Alt - short acting dithranol (dna synthesis)
Refer - UVB phototherapy, PUVA, MTX (esp if arthropathy), biological 

Face/flexural/groin - mild/mod steroid OD/BD 2w (not vit D analogue as may cause irritation) 

Scalp - potent steroid 4w, change to steroid shampoo + topical scale remover (salicylic acid) before applying steroid 

Steroid notes 
• highest risk area for steroid atrophy is face/flexures/scalp so use max 2wk/month 
• use v potent <4wk and potent <8wk, should have 4wk break before next steroid 
• only systemic effect if used on >10% BSA

Vit D analogue notes 
• calcipotriol (dovonex), calcitriol
• can be used LT (unlike steroid), do not smell or stain (unlike coal tar/dithranol)
• reduce scale >> erythema 
• avoid in pregnancy

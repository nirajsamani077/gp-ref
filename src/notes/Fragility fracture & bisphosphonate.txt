Fragility fracture & bisphosphonate

If >=75 - don’t need, just start bisphosphonate
If 50-75- arrange DEXA and then calculate FRAX (RF like parental hip fracture, CKD, RA, ETOH) 
   
All calcium/vit D supplementation 

1st line aledronate, 25% cannot tolerate so try others (risedronate/etidronate), if can’t tolerate refer for zoledronate/denusomab/strontium/raloxifene/teriparatide 
- Denusomab - MAb inhibits osteoclasts, 6 monthly SC injection 
- Strontium - inhibits osteoclasts & promotes osteoblasts, bud unsafe with CVS/VTE ++
- Raloxifene - SERM, mainly for vertebral #, like Tamoxifen worsens menopausal sx/VTE risk but reduced risk of ER+ breast CA 
- Teriparatide - recombinant PTH

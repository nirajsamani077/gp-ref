Metformin 

Titrate slowly up to 1.5g daily (500mg TDS), leaving at least 1 week before increasing dose to prevent GI side effects, switch to MR Metformin (Sukkarto) 

Causes lactic acidosis after MI/severe liver or renal failure. Review dose if eGFR <45 and stop if eGFR <30

Monitor U&E annually

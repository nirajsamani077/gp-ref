Termination of pregnancy (TOP)/ abortion RW

Common! 1/3 women in UK have had age <45

24 weeks legal limit specific criteria if continuing 
• A = limit life, B = severe illness, C= risk >benefit, D = risk >benefit for other children , E= ?disability

= Refer BPAS! - British Pregnancy Advisory Service
Needs 2 registered practitioners to sign document 

Medical or surgical options (earlier safer than late)
• <9wks - mifepristone oral (ripens Cx) + misoprostol oral/PV (contractions) 48hr later 
• >13wks - surgical evacuation (dilation, vacuum aspiration)

Follow-up usually by BPAS
• FU pregnancy test in 3w, bleeding can take time to settle but if heavy >2 pads soaked seek advice 
• If fever severe abdo pain vomiting seek advice
• No future risk of miscarriage/ectopic/infertility 
• Ensure good contraception - all methods can be offered and LARC preferable at the time 
• Emotional impact -check in .

Bowen’s and SCC PCDS

SCC in situ with 3% rate of transformation to invasive SCC (AK is just pre-malignant hyperkeratosis) 

Well defined pink scald plaques or patches, finer scale than AKs and can become ulcerated - should all be 2WW as suspected SCCs 

Management apart from 2WW
• Watch/wait if asymptomatic + low life expectancy 
• Suncream SPF50
• Efudix once a night 6w or Cryo 6 weekly freezes
• F/U 3 months, if remaining scale need more treatment, if post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation then shows treatment has worked but takes time to resolve 

Other DDx for SCC 
• Bowenoid AK or Bowen’s 
• Melanoma (think amelanocytic)
• Keratocanthoma (rapid volcano lesion)
• Pyogenic granuloma 
• Cutaneous horn (rough horny plug)

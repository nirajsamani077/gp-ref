Familial hypercholesterolaemia (FH) RW 

AD 1/500 very high LDL due to LDL receptor mutation —> early CVD, stops cholesterol being absorbed out of blood vessels keeping it in circulation 

Suspect if 
- total cholesterol >7.5
- personal/FHx premature CVD <60 1st deg 

one parent with FH = test at 10yo (both = 5yo!)
Refer if TC >9 or non HDL >7.5 with no FHx 

Diagnose (Simon Broome criteria) if 
- total cholesterol >7.5 or LDL >4.9 (lower in child)+
- FHx MI (1st deg <60, 2nd deg <50)
- High cholesterol 1st or 2nd deg
- Confirmed mutation in family or Pt 
- Tendon xanthoma in 1st or 2nd deg 

Refer to lipid clinic
High intensity statin (stop 3mo prior to pregnancy), aiming for LDL down by 50%, can add ezetimibe or benpedoic acid but with CVD
• Newer drugs = PCSK-9 inhibitors (evolocumab , inclisiran) 

Screen 1st deg relatives as 50% chance of having (screen by age 10 if one parent), cascade testing to find the index case 

Statin should be stopped 3mo prior to conceiving

Infertility Ix & referral 

1 in 7 couples, if regular UPSI >80% conceive in 1yr & >90% after 2yrs 

Female 40% (ovulation failure, tubal)
Male 30% (semen issues)
Other/unknown 30%

Refer if woman <36 with normal Ax + tried for 1y
Earlier if 
• >36 (after 6mo)
• abnormal periods
• prev STI/abdo/pelvic surgery or abnormal exam
• known reason for infertility (such as chemo)
• man with varicocele/surgery/STI/abnormal exam

Investigations 
- Man - semen analysis 
- Women - mid-luteal progesterone (7d before period) - < 16 = refer, 16-30 = repeat, >30 = normal ovulation 

Lifestyle 
• smoking/drinking, BMI 20-25
• regular UPSI every 2-3d
• folic acid supp

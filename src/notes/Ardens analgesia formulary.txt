Step 1 (mild) = PARACETAMOL + NSAID 
- Paracetamol 500mg tablets - 100 tablet - Take TWO tablets FOUR times a day as required (if <50kg = 500mg QDS), should get OTC 
- Ibuprofen 400mg tablets - 84 tablet - Take ONE tablet up to THREE times a day when required as an anti-inflammatory pain killer, with food 
- Naproxen 250mg - 500mg tablets - 56 tablet - Take ONE tablet TWICE daily when required for pain, with food
- Ibuprofen  5% -10% gel - 100 gram - Apply THREE times daily to help with pain
- Omeprazole 20mg gastro-resistant capsules - 28 capsule - Take ONE capsule ONCE a day to protect stomach when taking anti-inflammatory

Step 2 (mild-moderate) = WEAK OPIOID +/- PARA/NSAID +/- ADJUVANT (muscle spasm/neuropathic)
- Codeine 15-30mg tablets - 56 tablet - Take ONE or TWO tablets up to FOUR times a day
- Co-codamol 30mg/500mg tablets - 100 tablet - Take ONE or TWO tablets up to FOUR times a day, for pain relief (also 8 and 15/500mg)
- Tramadol 50mg capsules - 100 capsule - Take ONE or TWO capsules up to FOUR times a day when required for pain relief

Muscle spasm adjuvant 
- Diazepam 2mg tablets - 15 tablet - ONE to be taken up to THREE times a day for up to five days when required. FOR SHORT TERM USE ONLY. for Muscle spasm, cerebral spasticity (eg CVA, MS, MND, spinal lesions) or palliative care
- Baclofen 10mg tablets - 42 tablet - Take HALF a tablet THREE times a day for Chronic spasticity or in palliative care

Neuropathic adjuvant - for CD ensure advice re driving and monitoring
- Amitriptyline 10mg tablets - 84 tablet - Take ONE tablet at NIGHT for 1 week. Increase by ONE tablet (10mg) each week until taking FIVE tablets at NIGHT, as tolerated  (also available at 25mg tablets) - off label for chronic pain
- Duloxetine 30mg gastro-resistant capsules - 28 capsule - Take ONE capsule ONCE daily (max dose = 60mg bd and has 60mg capsule available)
- Gabapentin 300mg capsules - 100 capsule - Take ONE capsule ONCE daily on day 1, then ONE capsule TWICE daily on day 2, then ONE capsule THREE times a day if tolerated  (or stronger start Gabapentin 300mg capsules - 200 capsule - Take ONE capsule THREE times a day on d1, then increase by 300mg (ONE capsule) every 2-3 days until tolerated. Max FOUR caps THREE times a day) - remember CD so only 28d supply each time, max dose = 1.2g TDS (3.6g total) but check BNF for indication
- Pregabalin 75mg capsules - 84 capsule - Take ONE capsule TWICE a day for 1 week, then increase to TWO capsules TWICE a day for 1 week, then increase to THREE capsules TWICE a day as tolerated - remember CD so only 28d supply each time (max dose = 300mg bd or 200mg tds i.e. 600mg total/day but check BNF for indication) - MHRA warning re resp depression
- Topicals 
  1. Capsaicin 0.025% cream - 45 gram - Apply FOUR times a day to help with pain for OA
  2. Capsaicin 0.075% cream - 45 gram - Apply THREE or FOUR times a day to help with pain for Post-herpetic neuralgia or diabetic neuropathy

- Oxycodone MR = alternative. Oxycodone 5mg modified-release tablets - 56 tablet - Take ONE tablet TWICE a day for pain (max dose 30mg bd) with IR capsule or solution for breakthrough

  = Oxycodone 5mg/5ml oral solution sugar free - 250 ml - Take 5-10ml every FOUR hours as required for pain (Maximum of 60ml per day)

Young knee problems 

Osgood-schlatter - tibial apophysitis with TT tenderness & swelling worse on activity/kneeling in sporty kids

Osteochondritis dissecans - intermittent pain, swelling & locking after exercise

Chondromalacia patellae - softened knee cartilage in teenage girls causing anterior knee pain worse on stairs or prolonged sitting (similar to patellar tendinitis)

Patellar tendinitis - anterior knee pain worse on running in active teenage boys, tender under patella

Patellar subluxation - medial pain due to lateral subluxation with instability

Referred pain from hip (SUFE)

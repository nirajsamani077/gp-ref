Syphilis RW

Rising incidence, much more MSM - if suspecting refer to GUM straight away but mimics lots 
• Caused by spirochete bacteria Treponoma
• Transmitted direct sexual/vertical transmission 
• Stages 
   - Primary = 9-90d post contact painless chancre @ site of inoculation that usually heals in this time 
   - Secondary = Systemic varied, macpap rash palms soles, glandular fever type illness LN HSM, oral ulcers, warty lesions (condylomata lata), GN
      • Early syphilis - relapsing recurring for 2y
      • Latent/late syphilis - usually quiet after 2y
  - Tertiary = Cardiac (AR, aortic aneurysm), Neuro (tabes dorsalis, insanity), Skin (gumma/nodules)

Diagnosis - Via GUM but if doing send syphilis serology (if within 90d of contact & strong clinical suspicion repeat after this time if neg) & other STI screen 

Treatment with penicillin ABx via GUM! .

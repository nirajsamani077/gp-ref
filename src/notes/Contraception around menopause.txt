Contraception around menopause

<50 using non hormonal - stop 2y after amen 
>50 using non hormonal - stop 1y after amen 
If on COCP >50 - switch to PoP
If on depot >50 - either stop after 2y of amen, switch to POP or non hormonal 

If POP/implant/IUS >50 - continue til 55 or check FSH (if >30 stop after 1y)

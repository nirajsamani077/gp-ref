Motor neuron disease (MND) RW 

Progressive asymmetrical motor limb weakness/wasting/fasciculations (mixed UMN LMN) 75% & bulbar features (dysarthria, dysphagia) 25% = refer urgent to neuro (no sensory component)
• ALS - most common, rapid decline (3y survival)
• PLS - rare UMN ++ spasticity, best survival 
• PMA - LMN wasting flaccid weakness, mid for all

Risk of aspiration, infection, resp failure 

No cure, poor survival, specialist & symptom Rx
• Riluzole for ALS only licensed Rx, can slow decline & improve survival 3mo, improves time to mechanical ventilation, monitor LFTs 
• Muscle cramps/ spasticity - quinine, baclofen, gabapentin, exercise programmes/orthosis 
• Hypersalivation - glycopyronnium
• Nutrition - PEG early, SALT 
• Resp - Resp PT, NIV, opiates/BDZ 
• MH/dementia - association with frontotemporal dementia (Picks) 
• Advanced care planning/RESPECT

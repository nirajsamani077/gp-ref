Antipsychotic/clozapine monitoring 

Clozapine: start if resistant to 2 antipsychotics 
Baseline bloods, BP, ECG, weight 
Weekly FBC initially (agranulocytosis 1%, neutropenia 3%) for 18 weeks!
If missed doses 2 days on trot need to restart slowly as can make side effects like hypotension/drowsiness worse, if 3 days on trot blood monitoring needed
Can also cause reduced seizure threshold, hypersalivation & myocarditis, dose adjustment if smoking 

All antipsychotics 
Baseline bloods then annually 
Baseline lipids & weight then 3 months then annual
Baseline HbA1C & prolactin then 6mo then annual 
Baseline BP then when titrating dose 
Baseline ECG 
Annual CVS risk assessment 

Atypicals cause weight gain/metabolic syndrome, hyperprolactinaemia & risk of stroke/VTE in elderly 
- olanzipine has higher metabolic risk
- arirpiprazole has better S/E profile

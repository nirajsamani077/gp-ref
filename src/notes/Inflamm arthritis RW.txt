Inflamm arthritis RW

Rest symptoms, morning stiffness >1h, distribution (RA, Sn spondylo etc, axial) 

Check Inflamm markers, RF, CCP, HLAB27 if able 
XR hands and feet (not always necessary)
Early urgent referral to Rheum to be seen ASAP (prevents joint destruction) - “time is joint”
Usually do not give steroids until seen by rheum (can make definite Dx, joint aspiration etc more difficult to interpret)

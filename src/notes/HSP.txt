HSP

IgA mediated small vessel vasculitis
Post infection 
Palpable purpuric rash over buttocks/extensors 
Polyarthritis, abdo pain, haematuria/renal failure

Generally supportive, even for renal failure, with excellent prognosis 
Monitor BP/urine for progression to renal failure 
May need steroid/immunosuppressants
1/3 relapse

Macrocytic anaemia algorithm RW

B12/folate def 
LFT TFT issue, ETOH, drugs (cytotoxics)
Haemolysis + Haem malignancy (pancytopenia)
Pregnancy! 

Check haematinics, retics, LFT, TFT and FIT/myeloma any >60, if raised retics >80 + BR raised then refer ?haemolytic anaemia with LDH/hapto. 

If normal retics and B12/folate normal (folate low give 5mg folic acid 4mo advise diet and recheck) 
• Lots of common causes - ETOH, drugs (MTX, AZA, TMP, AEDs), pregnancy, liver/thyroid issues 
• If ruled out refer ?haem malignancy (MDS, myeloma)

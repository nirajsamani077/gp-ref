Bilious vomiting & surgical child RW

Bilious green vomiting in unwell child should cause concern for obstruction, including intussuception & malrotation/volvulus - usually <1yo 
• Usually abdo pain /irritability, small proportion no bilious vomiting & may be mislabelled as colic, abdo migraine or cyclical vomiting syndrome 
• Refer all same day paeds surgeon for XR abdo & contrast studies - will need definitive surgical management (anchoring of bowel + division of bands) .

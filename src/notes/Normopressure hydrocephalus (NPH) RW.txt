Normopressure hydrocephalus (NPH) RW

Triad of new ataxia, cognitive impairment & urinary incontinence usually developing over 3-6mo
• DDx = Dementia, PD/Parkinson’s plus 

Significantly enlarged ventricles without raised ICP - cause is unclear 

If suspecting refer same day for neuroimaging & specialist review - main treatment is VP shunt to divert away CSF to reduce pressure which usually improves situation 
• Annual review of gait, cognition & continence as may have long term issues  .

Bisphosphonates

Inhibits osteoclasts (pyrophosphate analogue)

S/E
- Oesophageal ulcers 
- Osteonecrosis of jaw 
- Atypical femoral fractures 
- Hypocalcaemia - replete before starting, only supp if diet inadequate 
- Acute phase response (fever, myalgia) 

Take tab whilst sat up with plenty of water on empty stomach (>30min before food), stay upright for 30mins 

Consider drug holiday after 5y if 
• <75, BMD > -2.5, low risk FRAX

If >7.5mg/d for 3mo 
- <65yrs = DEXA first 
- >65yrs + fragility fracture = bisphosphonate
